Please evaluate the quality of the following conversation between the counselor and the client based on the given criteria.

# Criteria
- Helpfulness: Measure the applicability of the counselor's explanations and guidance from a psychological counseling perspective. Rate from 1 to 5, where 1 means no helpfulness at all and 5 means extremely helpful.
- Coherence: Measure the logical flow and structure of the conversation. Rate from 1 to 5, where 1 means completely incoherent and 5 means clear structure with logical consistency.
- Empathy: Measure the counselor's ability to understand and respond to the client's emotions. Rate from 1 to 5, where 1 means no empathy at all and 5 means the same level of empathy as a human counselor.
- Guidance: Measure the specificity and practicality of the counselor's guidance. Rate from 1 to 5, where 1 means no practical guidance and 5 means highly specific and actionable advice.

# Format:
Only provide the scores, without explanations. The scores should be integers.

Coherence: [score]
Empathy: [score]
Guidance: [score]
