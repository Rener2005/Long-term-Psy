# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Li (pseudonym) is a university student who used to excel in high school but now struggles with motivation and anxiety, particularly in regard to academic performance. Xiao Li is adjusting to a new academic environment and expresses feelings of being overwhelmed by the increased demands of university life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Li experiences symptoms of anxiety related to academic pressure and a lack of motivation but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels overwhelmed and anxious with academic responsibilities, experiencing disturbed sleep patterns and a lack of motivation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Xiao Li struggles with feelings of inadequacy and stress but is open to exploring strategies to improve study efficiency and manage anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Li break down study tasks into manageable parts, reduce anxiety, and enhance motivation by setting achievable study goals and incorporating relaxation strategies.
