# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhao is a concerned mother worried about her adult daughter's job search and career future. She often experiences anxiety surrounding her daughter's employment situation and its impact on the family's financial state.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhao experiences symptoms of anxiety related to her daughter's uncertain job search. No formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao is feeling anxious and engulfed by worries concerning her daughter's job search and future security.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhao's anxiety impedes her ability to enjoy daily activities, with expressed fears around personal adequacy in supporting her daughter.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to identify and challenge Zhao's anxious thoughts. Encourage self-care and help Zhao separate her daughter's job search outcomes from personal responsibility.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao expresses ongoing anxiety regarding her daughter's job search and its potential effects on family finances.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhao's anxiety is deeply rooted in financial insecurity and external perceptions but sees her openness to exploring alternate strategies to manage these concerns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Zhao in recognizing and evaluating the accuracy of her catastrophic thoughts. Encourage practical steps like budgeting to alleviate financial stress, reinforcing Zhao's supportive role without overburdening herself.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao reports reduced anxiety and a shift in perspective, considering alternative approaches to support her daughter's career path.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist sees Zhao's emerging flexibility and openness to finding constructive paths for her daughter's employment, coupled with a calmer demeanor.

[Consultation Goals]  
Build on Zhao's newfound perspectives, helping her to continue exploring constructive and non-anxiety-driven strategies to support her daughter. Reinforce self-compassion and boundary-setting using CBT strategies.

# Fourth Consultation
