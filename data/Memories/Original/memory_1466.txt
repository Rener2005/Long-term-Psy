# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Zhuang is a student experiencing immense pressure from parental expectations regarding academic performance. This pressure has led to feelings of inadequacy and a sense of being trapped. Xiao Zhuang is seeking ways to manage and communicate these feelings better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Zhuang exhibits symptoms of anxiety and feelings of worthlessness, largely influenced by parental expectations. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Zhuang feels overwhelmed and struggles with dark thoughts due to high parental expectations and perceived inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices Xiao Zhuang's difficulty with self-worth and parental pressure but acknowledges their openness to seeking help and changing perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Xiao Zhuang challenge and restructure negative thoughts, build self-compassion, and manage academic stress through effective communication with parents.
