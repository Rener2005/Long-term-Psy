# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a university student, is navigating the complexities of a romantic relationship that feels overwhelming and suffocating. They have been struggling to balance academic obligations with their personal life, recently expressing a need for personal space in the relationship.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user demonstrates symptoms of anxiety related to personal boundaries and relationship management but has not indicated any formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and anxious due to the lack of personal space in their relationship, leading to difficulty concentrating on academic tasks.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user struggles with assertiveness in expressing their needs, showing underlying anxiety about potential conflict in their relationship.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to assist the user in communicating personal needs effectively using "I" statements, and begin exploring strategies for setting healthy boundaries in the relationship.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user experiences heightened anxiety and fear of losing their partner, compounded by feelings of being trapped and a loss of self-identity.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes a pattern of over-reliance on the partner for emotional fulfillment, with underlying fears of loss exacerbating the user's anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help the user identify and challenge irrational beliefs about relationship dependency, encouraging reconnection with personal interests and social networks to foster a balanced sense of self.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage the user to establish small social goals and explore opportunities to rebuild connections through shared interests, reinforcing CBT strategies to combat negative social self-perceptions.
