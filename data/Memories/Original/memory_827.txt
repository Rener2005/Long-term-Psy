# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoqi (pseudonym) is a college student facing anxiety related to academic stress and social interactions. Xiaoqi reports feeling overwhelmed, especially with conflicts involving a roommate, which exacerbates feelings of negativity and detachment from school.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoqi experiences symptoms consistent with anxiety and potential social anxiety but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoqi feels anxious and overwhelmed, particularly due to tension with a roommate, contributing to a negative view of school life.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoqi's tendency to isolate due to perceived burdensomeness and conflict avoidance, but notes a willingness to seek peaceful resolutions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoqi challenge negative perceptions and foster constructive communication strategies. Support Xiaoqi in developing skills to handle roommate conflicts and manage stress.
