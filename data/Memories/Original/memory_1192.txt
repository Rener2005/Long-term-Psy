# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a university student, often experiences anxiety and inadequacy in social situations, exacerbated by new interactions and environments. They actively seek strategies to manage anxiety and gain confidence in social contexts to improve their overall well-being.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user struggles with symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and slightly fearful about speaking in social situations, worrying about potential judgment from others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user harbors negative assumptions about others' perceptions, yet shows insight in recognizing these are not based on evidence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques focusing on challenging negative self-assumptions and fostering positive self-talk and relaxation strategies to manage anxiety.
