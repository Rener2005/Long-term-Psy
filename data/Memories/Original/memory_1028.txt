# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
X is a young student transitioning from elementary to middle school, experiencing difficulties in adjusting to the new academic demands and expectations. X frequently battles with feelings of overwhelm and a lack of concentration in class.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
X experiences anxiety related to academic performance and social interactions but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
X feels overwhelmed and anxious due to challenges in keeping pace with the fast-moving curriculum and high expectations from teachers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that X tends to internalize feelings of inadequacy when unable to fully grasp lesson content, leading to avoidance behaviors like skipping homework.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to assist X in managing academic-related anxiety, focusing on creating achievable small goals for classroom participation and enhancing engagement through personal interests.
