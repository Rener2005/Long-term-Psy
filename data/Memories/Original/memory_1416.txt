# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Doreen is an individual navigating social discomfort, often questioning the intentions behind social invitations and expressing hesitance in social engagements due to underlying feelings of self-doubt and perceived inadequacy.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Doreen exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Doreen feels anxious and insecure about social interactions, worrying about the genuineness of others' invitations and choosing to avoid social scenarios to prevent potential rejection.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Doreen tends to internalize negative assumptions about others' perceptions but shows willingness to challenge these fears by considering the lack of evidence for them.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Doreen challenge negative assumptions regarding social acceptance and develop proactive strategies for engaging in low-pressure social activities.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Doreen experiences anticipatory anxiety around social gatherings, fearing judgment and overthinking interactions, though acknowledges some positive experiences.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes that Doreen's anxiety is fueled by a critical internal dialogue, yet she shows progress in challenging these thoughts by realizing that her fears are not supported by evidence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Aid Doreen in using cognitive restructuring to balance anxious thoughts with past positive experiences and encourage small, confidence-building gestures in social settings.

# Third Consultation

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Doreen to reinforce self-efficacy by focusing on past successful interactions, and utilize visualization techniques of positive scenarios to build social confidence.
