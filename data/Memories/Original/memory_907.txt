# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student facing anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, primarily stemming from recent false rumors spread by classmates that have left them feeling isolated and misunderstood.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety, including intrusive thoughts and feelings of isolation, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by anxiety, sadness, and frustration due to the spread of false rumors, leading to a persistent sense of social isolation. 

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user's negative thoughts about self-worth are reinforced by the perceived judgment of others but notes a willingness to recognize and focus on positive support from friends.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to challenge the user's negative perceptions and promote reliance on supportive relationships. Encourage small steps towards improved social interactions and emotional resilience.

# Third Consultation
