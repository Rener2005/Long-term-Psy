# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Rosie (pseudonym) is a mother who feels overwhelmed by the challenges of balancing work and family responsibilities, including concerns about her children's school attendance and future. She is expecting another baby, which adds to her stress, and she feels unsupported at times.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Rosie experiences symptoms of anxiety related to family dynamics and financial stress but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Rosie feels exhausted and stressed from juggling work and family responsibilities, and she worries about her children's educational progress, feeling inadequate as a mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Rosie's tendency toward self-blame and her concerns about failing as a parent, reflecting deeply on her children's well-being.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Rosie challenge negative self-perceptions and develop strategies for effective communication with her children, along with managing her stress and financial concerns.
