# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu (pseudonym) is a school student who struggles with feelings of pressure and inadequacy regarding academic performance. Liu feels especially overwhelmed with the fast pace of lessons and the lack of parental support due to familial obligations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu exhibits symptoms of stress and low self-confidence, particularly in relation to schoolwork, but has not reported a formal diagnosis of any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu feels frustrated and overwhelmed by the fast-paced lessons and the perceived lack of understanding and support from parents.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives Liu’s stress as linked to feeling unsupported at home, observing a sense of hopelessness regarding school and family dynamics.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu develop a more balanced view of personal capabilities and find ways to make learning more engaging. Encourage open communication with parents to foster understanding and support.
