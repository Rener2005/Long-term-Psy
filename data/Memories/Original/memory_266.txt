[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A experiences anxiety and low self-esteem but has not shared a formal diagnosis of any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes A's heavy self-judgment and avoidance behaviors but identifies a willingness to explore more compassionate self-perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist A in identifying and challenging negative beliefs using cognitive behavioral techniques and emphasize small, actionable steps to integrate enjoyable activities and social engagement.
