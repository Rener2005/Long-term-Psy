He has expressed feelings of anger, frustration, and guilt related to his interactions with his wife and daughter. Recent workplace pressure has exacerbated these emotions, leading to tension at home.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming experiences symptoms of stress, irritability, and frustration, though there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels overwhelmed and guilty about his reactions towards his family, especially after stressful events at work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Li Ming is struggling with self-regulation of emotions but is motivated to manage his anger and improve family dynamics.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming identify and challenge negative thought patterns, develop strategies for emotional regulation, and improve communication with his family.
