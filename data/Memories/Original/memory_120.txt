# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Little A is a student who struggles with maintaining focus and communication, facing challenges both at school and home. Little A often feels misunderstood by family and peers, leading to stress and anxiety, particularly over academic performance. Recent conflicts with classmates and arguments at home contribute to feelings of frustration.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Little A exhibits symptoms of anxiety and challenges with emotional regulation. There is no formal diagnosis disclosed for any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Little A feels upset about school due to criticism from teachers and a lack of understanding from parents, leading to anxiety and feeling overwhelmed by expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Little A struggles with feeling misunderstood and burdened by academic expectations, yet shows a desire to be heard and understood by parents.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Little A manage distractions, particularly phone usage, and enhance communication skills with parents, aiming to foster understanding and reduce conflicts at home.
