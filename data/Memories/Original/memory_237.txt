# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoxiao is a university student who experiences difficulties in communication with her mother, leading to frequent arguments. She feels stressed and frustrated by their strained relationship and the ongoing challenges in their interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxiao experiences symptoms of stress and frustration linked to familial interactions but has not indicated any formal diagnosis of a mental health condition.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxiao feels low and frustrated, perceiving a lack of understanding and support from her mother during their frequent arguments.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoxiao's struggles with feeling misunderstood by her mother and her frustration with their communication patterns. Xiaoxiao displays a desire to improve their relationship.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to aid Xiaoxiao in managing emotional responses during arguments and developing strategies for effective communication. Encourage exploring ways to express feelings constructively.
