# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xu (pseudonym) is a university student dealing with obsessive thoughts and feelings of anxiety, particularly around social interactions and living arrangements. Xu has recently moved into a new dorm where relationships with roommates are tense, exacerbating feelings of helplessness.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xu is experiencing anxiety and obsessive-compulsive tendencies but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xu feels anxious and overwhelmed by obsessive thoughts about being perceived negatively by roommates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xu is caught in a cycle of overthinking and social anxiety but recognizes Xu's effort to understand and untangle these concerns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to help Xu reframe negative thoughts and communicate more effectively with roommates. Focus on building self-compassion and reducing the sense of isolation.
