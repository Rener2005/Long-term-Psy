# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Honghong (pseudonym) is a young child who struggles with managing anger and sharing, especially during playtime with friends. Honghong seeks to foster better relationships by learning how to express emotions constructively and handle conflict without aggression.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Honghong displays difficulties with anger management and impulse control but has shown a growing desire to improve interpersonal skills.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Honghong feels upset and frustrated after a playground incident, recognizing the need to express desires without aggression.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Honghong's impulsivity and lack of consideration for others' feelings but notes openness to learning alternative behaviors.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Honghong manage emotions and reactions, fostering patience and communication skills for conflict resolution.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Honghong expresses increased frustration over personal space and belongings, feeling anger when boundaries are crossed.

[Consultation Goals]
