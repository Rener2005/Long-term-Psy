# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Wei (pseudonym) is a university student experiencing isolation and misunderstandings with dormitory roommates. Lately, Xiao Wei has felt anxious about these interpersonal challenges and the impact on daily life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Wei reports feelings of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Wei feels isolated, misunderstood, and anxious about maintaining relationships with roommates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Wei struggling with self-blame and fear of confrontation, but there is an openness to exploring conflict resolution strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Wei challenge self-blame, develop healthier communication strategies, and improve relationship dynamics with roommates.
