# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student feeling isolated and hurt due to being called derogatory names by classmates. They are concerned about not belonging and are anxious about how others perceive them.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels really low due to treatment from classmates, leading to doubts about respect and increased anxiety in social settings.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user is trying to maintain a brave front despite feeling significant internal pain, and there's a tendency toward negative self-assessment.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge negative perceptions and reinforce moments of self-confidence. Encourage small, positive social engagements to boost self-esteem.
