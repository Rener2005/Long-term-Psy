[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Wen displays symptoms of anxiety tied to academic performance and self-esteem, although no formal diagnosis has been made. She is keen to develop healthier ways of managing these feelings.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Wen's self-critical thoughts are intense and deeply affect her self-esteem, though she shows potential for growth by recognizing the need for more compassionate self-dialogue.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Wen recognize and challenge her negative thought patterns, while fostering self-compassion and healthier self-assessment practices.
