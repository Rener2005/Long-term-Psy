# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Jun (pseudonym) is a young student dealing with challenges in social interactions, experiencing loneliness, and a sense of isolation from classmates. Xiao Jun is academically successful but perceives this as a barrier to connecting with peers.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Jun exhibits feelings associated with social anxiety and low self-esteem, though no formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Jun feels lonely and low due to perceived social isolation and bullying from classmates, conflicting with the desire to connect.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Jun’s feelings of isolation stem from self-perceptions of being "too good" academically, which influence social interactions. There is an initial recognition of these thought patterns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Jun reframe thoughts of inadequacy and isolation, fostering small initial steps towards social engagement with classmates.
