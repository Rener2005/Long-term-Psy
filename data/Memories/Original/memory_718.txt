Zheng is a university student grappling with intense emotional struggles related to interpersonal relationships, notably with a junior classmate. Zheng experiences insecurity and heightened sensitivity in social interactions, seeking ways to improve communication and emotional management.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zheng exhibits symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, particularly in social and personal contexts, but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zheng is feeling anxious and insecure, particularly in relation to communication challenges within a personal relationship.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zheng anticipates negative outcomes and struggles with fears of inadequacy, yet demonstrates insight and readiness to explore more positive communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Zheng in challenging negative thought patterns and promoting more constructive communication strategies, aiming to reduce anxiety and enhance relationship interactions.
