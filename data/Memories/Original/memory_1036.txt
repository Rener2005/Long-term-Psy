# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaohua is a young adult adjusting to a new class and struggling with social anxiety and feelings of inadequacy. Recent conflicts within the family and challenges in forming connections with classmates have heightened these feelings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaohua experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, but no formal mental health disorder has been diagnosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohua feels overwhelmed and anxious about fitting in with new classmates, leading to a pervasive sense of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaohua encounters significant anxiety in social situations but shows a capacity for reflection and a willingness to challenge self-critical thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ CBT techniques to help Xiaohua reduce social anxiety by identifying and challenging negative beliefs about interactions. Encourage gradual exposure to social settings to build confidence.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohua continues to experience low self-worth, exacerbated by familial conflicts, and feels trapped in a cycle of silence and self-blame.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaohua's internalization of family conflict as self-fault yet acknowledges growing self-awareness of exaggerated negative thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Xiaohua in recognizing and disputing negative beliefs about personal fault using evidence and introduce coping strategies for expressing feelings in safe, controlled ways.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohua is stressed and struggles with concentration due to academic pressures, feeling anxious around peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaohua's overwhelmed state due to ineffective study habits and anxiety-driven avoidance behaviors in social interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Facilitate the development of structured study habits and encourage social engagement through manageable goals, using CBT to build task-specific confidence and social comfort.

# Fourth Consultation

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaohua's tendency to withdraw in conflict but identifies positive shifts towards self-expression and confidence building.

[Consultation Goals]  
Strengthen communication skills by practicing expression of thoughts in low-pressure environments and reinforce positive self-perception through successes and affirmations.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohua feels anxious and doubtful of social abilities due to a recent conflict, leading to self-imposed isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist recognizes Xiaohua's inclination to catastrophize social conflicts and discouragement, yet a willingness exists to attempt re-engagement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiaohua in confronting and reframing thoughts of rejection, implementing gradual social reintegration strategies, and building coping mechanisms for anxiety management.

# Sixth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohua feels uplifted by improved family dynamics, which enhances openness to communicating with classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaohua's increased optimism regarding family interactions and potential for leveraging newfound familial support into broader social contexts.
