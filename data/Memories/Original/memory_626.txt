# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhao (pseudonym) is an older adult feeling lonely due to limited mobility caused by a leg injury. Recent interactions with family have highlighted feelings of neglect, leading Zhao to seek ways to stay socially connected and occupied.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhao has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorder but exhibits symptoms of loneliness and seeks to improve social connections and mental well-being.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao feels lonely and desires more interaction and understanding from family while exploring ways to engage with the community.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Zhao's openness to finding solutions to loneliness and a readiness to try new activities despite physical limitations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Zhao to explore community activities that cater to mobility limitations, utilizing CBT techniques to overcome anxiety about new social interactions.
