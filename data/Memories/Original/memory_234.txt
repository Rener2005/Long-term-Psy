# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ma (pseudonym) is a student grappling with a sense of alienation and disconnection, particularly in academic and social settings. Ma feels misunderstood by teachers and parents, often resulting in frustration and disengagement with schoolwork.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ma experiences feelings of frustration and isolation but has not indicated any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ma expressed feelings of annoyance and feels misunderstood, especially in relation to poor academic performance and lack of engagement in school.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ma struggles with a lack of motivation and disconnection from the school curriculum, feeling trapped in a cycle of disengagement and external blame.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to explore sources of motivation for Ma and develop strategies for connecting interest in hobbies, such as gaming, to schoolwork. Encourage small, manageable goals to foster a sense of achievement.
