# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is dealing with anxiety and loneliness following a recent breakup, leading to self-doubt in social settings and concerns about the future.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei is experiencing social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not specified any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious and lonely, doubting social abilities and fearing judgment from others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang Wei's pattern of withdrawal in social situations despite friends' attempts at inclusion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive-behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei identify and challenge negative thought patterns, fostering self-compassion and building social confidence.

# Second Consultation

[Mental State]

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Zhang Wei in reframing negative perceptions and encourage gradual social engagement through CBT techniques, such as challenging distorted beliefs.
