# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Yang is a student experiencing difficulty with online learning, primarily due to concentration issues and high parental expectations. Xiao Yang tends to feel overwhelmed and stressed by these expectations while aiming to find effective ways to cope with this new learning environment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Yang is dealing with anxiety related to academic performance and has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yang feels stuck and anxious due to the inability to concentrate during online learning, heightened by pressure from parental expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Yang struggles with maintaining focus due to environmental distractions but displays eagerness to find strategies that could improve engagement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Yang develop a structured study environment and implement manageable alterations to reduce distractions and anxiety.
