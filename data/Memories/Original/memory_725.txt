# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Z (pseudonym) is a school student who experiences loneliness and struggles with feelings of being unloved due to their parents working away. Z often feels isolated at school and misses parental presence, impacting their focus and emotional well-being.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Z exhibits symptoms of loneliness and low mood related to familial separation but has not been diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Z feels lonely and believes their parents' absence is due to a lack of affection, causing emotional distress and difficulty in focusing on school.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Z interprets parental absence as abandonment, yet shows resilience and openness to exploring these beliefs and seeking peer support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques to help Z reframe thoughts about parental absence and loneliness. Encourage expression of feelings to friends to foster understanding and connection.
