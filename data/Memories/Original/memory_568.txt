# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wan (pseudonym) is an individual navigating feelings of anxiety and guilt following a recent incident involving a child at a park. Wan has expressed concerns about the incident's impact on family relations and personal emotions, seeking ways to address these feelings constructively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wan experiences significant anxiety and guilt related to the incident but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wan feels anxious and consumed by guilt over a recent incident, leading to a lack of focus on daily activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wan's anxiety is heightened by anticipatory thoughts of rejection but shows a willingness to explore proactive communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wan assess and reframe negative assumptions about others' reactions. Devise a plan for approaching the affected family to alleviate guilt and anxiety.
