# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaobin is a student who experiences feelings of confusion and frustration primarily related to social relationships, especially concerning interactions with his roommate. He enjoys helping others, particularly with studies, but recent communication challenges have led to self-reflection on his approach and expectations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaobin experiences symptoms related to low self-esteem and social anxiety but has not reported any formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaobin feels frustrated and confused about his relationship with his roommate, experiencing doubt over how his communication style affects others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Xiaobin is aware of how his communication might impact his relationships, reflecting a desire to be more supportive and understanding.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaobin develop supportive communication skills, focusing on softer language and curiosity-driven dialogue to foster relationship repair and personal reflection.
