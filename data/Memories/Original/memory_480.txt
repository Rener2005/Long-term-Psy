# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Margaret (pseudonym) is grappling with feelings of anger and loneliness after moving back to her community, which feels unfamiliar and unwelcoming. She desires to return to past experiences due to feeling isolated and struggling with social interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Margaret experiences symptoms of social anxiety and feelings of isolation. She has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Margaret feels angry and lonely, struggling with the transition to a new environment that feels less secure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Margaret battles with negative self-perceptions and social withdrawal due to fear of judgment.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Margaret challenge her negative thoughts and build confidence in interacting with others. Encourage small steps towards social engagement and emotional expression through journaling.
