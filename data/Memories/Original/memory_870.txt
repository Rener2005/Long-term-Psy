# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student who struggles with adapting to the university environment, experiencing feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly related to academic pressures and past exam failures. Recently, the user has been dealing with low self-esteem and sleep problems, impacting daily functioning.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user shows symptoms consistent with generalized anxiety and low self-esteem. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder is disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by academic pressure, anxious, and inadequate due to past failures, leading to sleep disturbances and avoidance behaviors.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that the user is caught in a cycle of negative self-comparison but shows openness to exploring solutions, such as breaking tasks into smaller goals.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user recognize and challenge negative automatic thoughts while developing a structured study plan to manage anxiety and improve time management.

# Second Consultation

# Third Consultation

# Sixth Consultation
