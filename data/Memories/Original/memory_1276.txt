# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Marilyn is a graduate student dealing with significant anxiety and self-doubt, particularly concerning academic performance and social interactions. She recently faced the rejection of an academic project which has exacerbated these feelings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Marilyn experiences symptoms consistent with anxiety and low self-esteem, with no formal mental health disorder diagnosis disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Marilyn feels anxious and frustrated, grappling with a strong sense of inadequacy and hopelessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Marilyn struggles with intense self-doubt and negative self-comparisons but has a reflective nature that can aid in exploring more compassionate self-views.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Marilyn identify and challenge negative thoughts, encouraging her to adopt a more balanced and compassionate self-perception. Aim to foster strategies for managing academic stress and improve motivation.
