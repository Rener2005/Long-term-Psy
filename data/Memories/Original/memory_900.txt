# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a university student who contends with feelings of anxiety and self-doubt, particularly related to academic performance and social interactions. Li is attentive to personal emotional responses and aims to better manage these reactions amidst ongoing academic pressures.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem and has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious and overwhelmed by the perception of falling behind in studies, which triggers self-doubt about intellectual capabilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Li struggles with negative self-comparison and a persistent sense of inadequacy, yet displays insight into more positive, self-compassionate outlooks.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li challenge detrimental beliefs about personal abilities and cultivate a more balanced view of self-efficacy, facilitating improved management of academic pressures.
