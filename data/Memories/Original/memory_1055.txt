# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The User is a university student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly concerning academic performance and meeting external expectations. They often feel torn between personal interests and perceived practical requirements.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The User experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt related to academic and personal expectations but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The User feels lost and anxious about setting personal goals, influenced by parental expectations, which hampers their ability to concentrate on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the User struggles with balancing their personal interests with external demands but shows a willingness to explore personal preferences and goals.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the User define personal academic goals and integrate interests into their studies, reducing pressure and anxiety from external expectations.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The User feels overwhelmed and doubtful about their abilities, fueled by comparisons with peers and the competitive academic environment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the User's deep-seated self-doubt but recognizes their openness to reframe perceptions and consider their previous successes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist the User in identifying and challenging negative thought patterns and foster self-compassion. Encourage setting a personalized study routine to enhance self-efficacy.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The User feels lost and anxious about long-term goals and is overwhelmed by the pressure to choose a major and career path.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the User shows insight into their varied interests but lacks clarity on how to integrate them into a coherent career plan.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support the User in exploring and integrating their interests to reduce anxiety over career uncertainty and employ CBT strategies to enhance decision-making and reduce pressure.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Help the User explore career options aligned with their strengths, challenge assumptions about failure through CBT, and encourage experiential learning for informed decision-making.

# Sixth Consultation

[Observational Impressions]
