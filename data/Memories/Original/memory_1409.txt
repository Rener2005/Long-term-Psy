# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ben (pseudonym) is a carpenter who struggles with perfectionism and anxiety related to the high standards he sets for his work. He recently missed a project deadline, which has intensified feelings of inadequacy and self-criticism. Ben is open to exploring ways to manage these patterns more effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ben experiences symptoms of perfectionism and anxiety. He has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder but demonstrates a strong tendency towards critical self-evaluation.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ben feels stressed and highly self-critical due to missing a project deadline, leading to feelings of failure in his professional life.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Ben's focus on perceived failures while downplaying successes, yet Ben shows a willingness to consider self-compassionate views that he readily offers to others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ben challenge perfectionistic thoughts and enhance self-compassion. Encourage recognition of achievements to balance negative self-perceptions and promote emotional well-being.

# Second Consultation
