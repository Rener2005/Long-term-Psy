# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Dong is a school student who experiences significant anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and social judgments, especially from peers like Mingming. Xiao Dong is eager to alleviate stress and improve self-esteem, acknowledging personal strengths beyond academics.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Dong exhibits signs of anxiety and self-imposed high standards, particularly around exams and social situations, although no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Dong feels anxious due to exam pressures and the fear of disappointing others, linking self-worth heavily to academic performance and peer perceptions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Dong's reliance on grades for self-validation and fear of social judgment, yet there is an openness to exploring cognitive restructuring.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Dong challenge the belief that grades are the sole determinant of self-worth. Develop strategies for managing exam-related anxiety and easing interactions with classmates like Mingming.
