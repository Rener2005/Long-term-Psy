# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Hua (pseudonym) is a professional who experiences significant anxiety related to work performance and project deadlines. Recently, Li Hua has faced overwhelming responsibilities at work that impact focus and emotional well-being. Li Hua is determined to understand and manage stress and enhance productivity while also seeking a better work-life balance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Hua struggles with symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt related to workplace expectations, without disclosing a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Hua feels anxious and overwhelmed by the upcoming project deadlines, experiencing difficulty in maintaining focus and productivity.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes Li Hua's tendency to procrastinate due to overwhelming workload pressures and self-doubt but recognizes Li Hua's openness to adopting new strategies for stress management.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Li Hua challenge negative, self-critical thoughts and develop a balanced approach to work tasks by incorporating breaks and realistic goals.
