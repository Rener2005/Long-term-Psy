# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
A (pseudonym) is a university student nearing graduation, grappling with uncertainty about career choices, particularly in the maritime industry. A values family connections highly and fears the emotional impact of being away from home for work.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A experiences symptoms of anxiety related to career uncertainty and separation from family, but there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A feels unsure about committing to a career in the maritime industry due to anxiety about potential isolation and the impact on family ties.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that A is in conflict between the allure of the maritime career and fears of disconnection from family. There is an openness to explore these feelings further.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help A challenge negative assumptions about becoming a sailor. Focus on developing proactive communication strategies to maintain family ties while at sea.
