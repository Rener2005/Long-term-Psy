# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
W is a student who struggles with feelings of anxiety and insecurity, particularly in relation to social interactions and academic performance. Recent challenges include tension with a roommate and concerns about academic results.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
W exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
W feels overwhelmed and anxious, experiencing significant distress due to perceived negative social interactions and its impact on academic focus.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that W is caught in a cycle of negative thinking but shows readiness to explore these thoughts and considers strategies for relief.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help W identify and challenge negative beliefs, fostering self-expression and enhancing well-being through activities like walking.
