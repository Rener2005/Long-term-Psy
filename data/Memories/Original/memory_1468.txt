# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ke is a young adolescent who struggles with feeling misunderstood and isolated at school. He has recently faced conflicts with classmates, leading to his parents considering a transfer to another school. Xiao Ke is seeking to find a way to improve his interactions without resorting to anger or violence.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ke demonstrates challenges with anger management and social interactions, but there is no mention of a formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ke feels isolated and misunderstood by peers and authority figures, leading to feelings of anger and frustration.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Ke's use of fighting as a means of asserting himself and a belief that others do not respect him. However, there's a willingness to consider alternative communication methods.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Ke express feelings constructively and explore non-confrontational communication strategies. Encourage reflection on past positive interactions to build better connections.
