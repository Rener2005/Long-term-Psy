# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chen is a student experiencing feelings of overwhelm due to academic pressure and fear of disappointing their parents. Chen is introspective and tends to focus on trying to meet external expectations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chen demonstrates symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt without a specific formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen feels overwhelmed and hopeless due to upcoming exams, experiencing significant anxiety about disappointing their parents.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Chen's anxiety stems from perfectionism and fear of judgment, but Chen is willing to explore new perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help Chen identify and challenge negative beliefs about their academic performance and introduce coping mechanisms for stress management.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen feels lost and overwhelmed, expressing amplified self-doubt after an unsatisfactory exam result.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Chen's self-worth is closely tied to academic success, but there is potential for Chen to reframe perspectives about self-value outside of academia.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Chen recognize and reframe negative judgments regarding academic performance, building resilience through self-compassion and balanced self-assessment.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen is feeling overwhelmed by their mother’s health issues, with accompanying feelings of helplessness and responsibility.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Chen carries a significant burden of family responsibility, which contributes to feelings of isolation and anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Chen to develop self-compassion and communicate these feelings within the family to alleviate the sense of isolation and responsibility.

# Fourth Consultation
