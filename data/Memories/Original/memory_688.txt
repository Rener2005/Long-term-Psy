# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, whose pseudonym is not disclosed, is navigating a period of community correction and is seeking ways to feel more in control over their future. They express concerns over dependency on technology, primarily the internet, and gaming, and are looking for strategies to improve familial relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences anxiety and feelings of isolation, particularly exacerbated by familial conflicts and a strong reliance on the internet for escape, although there has been no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder shared.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels unsatisfied with life, burdened by financial worries and future uncertainties, experiencing loneliness due to a strained connection with their family.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's high levels of worry and isolation, with a tendency to use the internet as an escape from reality. There is a noticeable hesitancy in communicating deeply with family members.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to address the user's behavior of internet reliance for escapism. Encourage expression of feelings towards family in a safe way, like writing a message, and promote healthier coping mechanisms.
