# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Song (pseudonym) is a young adult navigating the emotional challenges following a recent breakup, which has impacted Song's focus on studies and social engagements. Song expresses feelings of sadness, loneliness, and frustration, seeking strategies to cope with these emotions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Song is experiencing symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem related to the breakup but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Song feels overwhelmed with sadness and frustration, frequently dwelling on past memories with an ex-partner, affecting daily functioning and motivation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Song's tendency for self-blame and persistent negative thought patterns, but also notes Song's openness to exploring self-compassion and personal worth.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Song challenge self-critical thoughts, encourage self-compassion, and start building a supportive routine to manage emotional distress.
