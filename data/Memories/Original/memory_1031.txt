# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yuyue is a young child who experiences separation anxiety, particularly in relation to attending kindergarten. Yuyue feels safe and prefers staying at home with their mom, expressing fear and reluctance in social settings with unfamiliar peers.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yuyue exhibits symptoms of separation anxiety, marked by fear and avoidance of social contexts, but there has been no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yuyue experiences a high level of anxiety around the idea of attending kindergarten, feeling comfort and safety only at home with their mom.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Yuyue is articulate about their fear and demonstrates a reliance on maternal presence for comfort, yet shows openness to exploring solutions to manage their anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yuyue find comfort in kindergarten settings by introducing supportive strategies such as bringing a favorite toy and practicing interactions, thereby gradually reducing anxiety.


# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yuyue feels somewhat more at ease at home and has a slight improvement in outlook towards going to kindergarten, but still experiences significant anxiety about leaving home.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Yuyue displays a willingness to engage in enjoyable activities at home, indicative of progress, but still harbors anxiety about unfamiliar environments.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize CBT strategies to create positive associations with school-like activities at home, aiming to gradually build Yuyue's confidence in engaging with peers and new environments over time.


# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yuyue feels curious but still anxious about entering new environments and struggles with the unpredictability of these situations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Yuyue shows emerging curiosity about exploring new environments but maintains a need for maternal comfort and security.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage gradual exposure to new environments, leveraging safety objects and preparation strategies to reduce anxiety. Use CBT to support Yuyue's development in tackling social challenges independently.
