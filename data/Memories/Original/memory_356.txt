# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Paul (pseudonym) is dealing with ongoing health challenges and striving to increase his independence. He values social connections and is working on rebuilding his life by getting involved in volunteer work, which provides him with a sense of purpose and accomplishment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Paul seems to struggle with feelings of isolation due to his health issues but shows no signs of a diagnosed mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Paul feels somewhat discouraged due to a recent health setback but remains hopeful and is seeking ways to regain his independence and confidence.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Paul's resilience in wanting to progress toward independence despite setbacks, with a focus on mindful reestablishment of social connections.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Paul build gradual confidence through small social interactions and affirmations, reinforcing the importance of manageable goals in gaining independence.
