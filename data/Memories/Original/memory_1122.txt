# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student struggling with feelings of inadequacy, social anxiety, and academic stress that are exacerbated by the transition from a rural background to an urban university setting. They express a strong desire to fit in and excel academically.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user appears to exhibit symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, though there is no formal mental health disorder diagnosis explicitly mentioned at this time.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user is feeling overwhelmed, lonely, and is perceiving themselves as inadequate compared to their peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that the user is wrestling with intense self-doubt and negative self-comparisons but is open to trying new perspectives through cognitive restructuring.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to challenge the user's negative perceptions, particularly regarding their self-worth and belonging. Begin to build coping mechanisms for managing social anxiety.

# Second Consultation
