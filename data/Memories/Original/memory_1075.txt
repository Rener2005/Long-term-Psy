# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ruth is approaching her 40th birthday and grappling with anxiety about aging and unmet life expectations. As a stay-at-home mom of four, she often feels socially pressured about her career choices, leading to feelings of inadequacy and guilt.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ruth experiences symptoms of anxiety and mild depressive episodes but hasn't disclosed any formal mental health disorder diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ruth feels overwhelmed by the approach of her 40th birthday, anxious about the passage of time, and caught between feelings of pride in her family and guilt for not pursuing her career further. 

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist recognizes Ruth's struggle with societal expectations and internal guilt, noting her desire for self-exploration and sense of being trapped in routine.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ruth explore her unmet expectations, challenge negative comparisons with others, and integrate joyful activities like cooking into her life.
