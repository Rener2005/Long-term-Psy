[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming exhibits symptoms of social anxiety but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels anxious and overwhelmed by the pressure to connect with new people at school, leading to avoidance of social interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Ming struggles with anticipatory fear of social judgment but is beginning to explore alternative, less critical perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Xiao Ming in challenging the irrational fears surrounding social situations and to build confidence through gradual exposure and positive reinforcement in social interactions.
