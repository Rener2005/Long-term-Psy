# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoxin (pseudonym) is a university student who recently moved to a new city for university studies and struggles with anxiety and loneliness related to social interactions. Xiaoxin misses home and finds initiating conversations with new people challenging.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxin experiences symptoms of social anxiety and adjusts to a new environment without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxin feels anxious and lonely due to the transition to a new city and is apprehensive about making new friends.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoxin's anxiety manifests as social withdrawal, but Xiaoxin shows openness to exploring alternative perspectives and strategies for connecting with others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoxin challenge negative thoughts about self-worth and judgment by peers and encourage small proactive steps to enhance social interactions.
