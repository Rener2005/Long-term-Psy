# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyu is a high school student experiencing nervousness and anxiety, particularly about public speaking and social interactions. Xiaoyu is preparing for graduation and is concerned about entering a new school environment where making friends feels challenging due to shyness.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyu exhibits symptoms of social anxiety, especially in situations involving public speaking and social engagement. No formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyu feels nervous and anxious about the prospect of speaking at the graduation event and is worried about being judged by peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoyu's pervasive fear of judgment and public speaking, yet recognizes a willingness to practice and confront these fears gradually.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to help Xiaoyu challenge irrational beliefs about judgment during public speaking. Set progressive exposure exercises to build confidence and reduce anxiety in social settings.

# Second Consultation
