# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang San is a professional managing substantial work-related stress and family responsibilities, which often leaves him feeling overwhelmed. He has been facing increased duties at work and domestic conflicts with his spouse over parenting approaches.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang San experiences significant anxiety exacerbated by perfectionistic tendencies and an all-or-nothing mindset. No formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang San feels overwhelmed and anxious due to increased responsibilities both at work and home, fearing failure in his significant roles.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang San struggles with perfectionism and "all-or-nothing" thinking, yet recognizes opportunities for adjusting perspectives to reduce pressure.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang San challenge perfectionistic thoughts and recognize his achievements. Develop strategies to manage stress and improve communication at home.
