# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Li is a professional in a corporate setting who has been struggling with feelings of self-doubt and anxiety, particularly following a recent project failure. Xiao Li is seeking to rebuild self-confidence, improve emotional responses, and gain clarity about personal and professional futures.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, specifically related to career performance and future uncertainties, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels lost and anxious about the future, with diminished self-confidence following a project failure at work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Xiao Li's mindset is dominated by negative self-judgments and a loop of doubt, though Li shows insight into recognizing evidence of past successes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Li challenge negative thought patterns and develop a more balanced perspective. Encourage setting small, actionable goals to rebuild confidence and manage anxiety.
