Huang displays symptoms of anxiety stemming from academic, financial, and familial pressures. There is no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Mental State]  
Huang feels overwhelmed by the pressure of academic responsibilities and financial stresses, particularly worried about disappointing family expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices Huang's strong feelings of inadequacy and fear of failure, but also their willingness to explore alternative ways of thinking about their academic performance and pressures.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Huang reframe negative thoughts about their academic abilities and develop step-by-step strategies to manage study habits and stress.
