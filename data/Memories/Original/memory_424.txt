# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is feeling overwhelmed with work, expressing a lack of confidence in their skills. They feel stuck in a cycle of negative thoughts, particularly when facing increased pressure and performance-related tasks.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences anxiety and a sense of inadequacy, particularly related to their job performance, but no formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and paralyzed, especially when needing to present or take on new projects.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user struggles with fear of failure and negative self-comparisons, but shows insight into the need for self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge and replace negative thoughts with supportive ones. Encourage gradual exposure to stressful work situations to build confidence.

# Second Consultation
