# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a student facing intense pressure from both academic and familial responsibilities. They recently experienced a setback with their thesis, leading to a sense of hopelessness. Additionally, they care for family members, with a father who has schizophrenia and a mother who is disabled, further compounding their stress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user displays symptoms of anxiety, depression, and significant stress. There is no formal diagnosis disclosed, but their situation suggests a need for strategies to manage emotional and situational challenges.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and hopeless due to a recent academic setback and family concerns.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user is burdened by high expectations and negative self-judgments but shows resilience and a desire for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user recognize their strengths and challenge negative self-perceptions. Develop strategies to balance academic and familial obligations.
