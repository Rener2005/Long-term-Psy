# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyu is preparing for graduate school and struggles with feelings of loneliness and anxiety about social interactions, particularly with classmates. Xiaoyu is concerned about being excluded from social groups and feels pressure during academic preparations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyu experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyu feels anxious and lonely due to perceived exclusion by classmates, leading to self-doubt and increased anxiety in exam preparations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoyu’s tendency to internalize negative perceptions and avoidance behavior, yet there's an openness to reframe and approach social dynamics differently.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyu challenge negative thoughts regarding self-worth and social acceptance. Encourage initiating small interactions to build confidence and reduce loneliness.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyu feels lost and unconfident after facing disinterest from roommates, which exacerbates feelings of worthlessness and leads to withdrawal.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoyu’s negative self-perception is reinforced by unreciprocated social efforts, yet there’s willingness to recognize assumptions and explore more balanced views.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiaoyu in challenging assumptions about self-worth based on others' reactions. Encourage keeping a journal of positive interactions to build a more balanced self-image and manage social anxiety.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoyu’s struggle with assertiveness and confidence in social settings, though there is a readiness to practice and reframe participation as contribution.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Xiaoyu to set small goals for engagement in group settings. Use role-playing and reframing exercises to reinforce the value of personal contributions and challenge fear of judgment.

# Sixth Consultation
