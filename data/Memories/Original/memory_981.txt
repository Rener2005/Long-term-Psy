# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, referred to as H, is dealing with feelings of self-doubt and anxiety, particularly in social and familial contexts. H expresses a desire for acceptance and connection with peers and family, and is open to exploring these aspects to improve emotional well-being.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
H shows signs of social anxiety and feelings of inadequacy. No formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
H feels low and frustrated, particularly worried that classmates and teachers harbor negative opinions, impacting self-esteem.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that H's concerns stem from perceived judgment despite a lack of concrete evidence and notes a readiness to explore alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help H challenge negative thoughts and replace them with more balanced viewpoints, while encouraging gradual engagement in social situations to build confidence.
