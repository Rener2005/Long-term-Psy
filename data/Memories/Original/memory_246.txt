# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Little T is a student struggling with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly in regards to academic performance and group work. They are reflective about their challenges and eager to contribute meaningfully to their school projects.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Little T experiences anxiety related to school performance and low self-esteem but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Little T feels anxious and doubts their abilities to contribute to a group science project, worried about being judged or dismissed by peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Little T's negative self-comparison and fear of criticism, but also observes their willingness to challenge these thoughts and find ways to engage.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to help Little T confront negative beliefs and recognize their unique contributions, fostering confidence in group settings.
