Sun (pseudonym) is a student dealing with performance anxiety, particularly related to exams and specifically foreign language tests. Sun often feels overwhelmed but is diligent and open to learning coping strategies to improve focus and manage stress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Sun experiences symptoms of anxiety correlated with academic performance, particularly during exams, but there's no formal diagnosis of an anxiety disorder.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
