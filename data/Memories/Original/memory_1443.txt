# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Little A (pseudonym) is a university student facing challenges with anxiety and self-worth, often feeling overwhelmed by academic pressures and struggles with reaching out to friends. She is trying to navigate feelings of isolation and desires to manage her emotional responses better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Little A experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Little A feels overwhelmed by upcoming exams, describing a sense of isolation and a physical response of persistent tension.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Little A struggles with burdensome personal expectations and withdrawing from social connections, but she is open to exploring new ways to cope.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Little A use cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge feelings of inadequacy and reduce anxiety. Begin fostering outreach strategies to reconnect with friends and focus on self-compassion.
