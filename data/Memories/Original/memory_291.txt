# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a married adult who experiences anxiety and insecurity related to relationship issues, particularly surrounding potential divorce and feelings of abandonment. The user wants to improve communication with their spouse and gain confidence in addressing emotional challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user shows symptoms of anxiety and insecurity related to relationship difficulties but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by arguments and uncertainties within their marriage, experiencing a persistent fear of abandonment and insecurity.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user is significantly affected by fears of relationship instability but shows an openness to exploring positive communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Facilitate the use of cognitive behavioral techniques to question negative thought patterns related to relationship anxiety and develop strategies for positive communication.

# Second Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
