[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaowang experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaowang’s difficulties in connecting with dorm mates foster negative self-perceptions, yet Xiaowang is open to exploring ways to improve social interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaowang address and manage negative self-perceptions related to social exclusion. Begin developing communication and conflict resolution skills to enhance relational experiences.
