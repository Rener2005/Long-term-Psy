# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaofang is a student who has recently transitioned to a new school and is struggling with loneliness and fitting in. She is dealing with disagreements with her roommate and feels sidelined at her prestigious school, affecting her social interactions and self-esteem.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaofang experiences feelings of inadequacy and social anxiety, but there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder mentioned.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaofang feels isolated and frustrated by her inability to form close connections in her new environment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaofang is overwhelmed by social challenges but displays a willingness to engage in exercises that could improve her situation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to assist Xiaofang in challenging her negative thoughts and improving her social skills. Encourage practicing effective communication and role-playing to build confidence.
