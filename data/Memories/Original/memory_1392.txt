# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is an adult who is experiencing emotional uncertainty and anxiety related to past and current relationships. Recent events include unresolved feelings towards a previous partner named A, and difficulties in her relationship with her current boyfriend.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li exhibits symptoms of anxiety due to relational conflicts and uncertainty but has not reported a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li experiences anxiety and confusion due to unresolved feelings about a past relationship and current relational challenges.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li is caught in a cycle of emotional turmoil between past and present relationships, but shows willingness to explore and understand her feelings towards both.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li identify and challenge negative and confusing thoughts associated with her relationships. Encourage open communication with her current partner to clarify her feelings and reduce anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious and uncertain about dating, struggling with connecting her emotional needs and expectations in relationships.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Li's fears of rejection and making wrong choices inhibit her from engaging in meaningful social interactions, yet she is becoming more aware of these patterns.

[Consultation Goals]
