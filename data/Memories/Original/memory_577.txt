# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) is a partner in a committed relationship, seeking to leave behind past challenges and build a new, stable life with his wife. Zhang is actively working on recovery and reintegration into society, with expressed concerns about finding employment and forming new social connections.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences anxiety related to financial pressures, job search challenges, and past experiences, without a formal diagnosis of a specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels uncertain about the future, particularly regarding socio-economic reintegration, and desires a sense of belonging and normalcy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes Zhang's eagerness to connect with community activities and social groups, albeit with hesitation and anxiety about initial interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to create a structured plan for Zhang and his wife to engage in community activities and workshops, fostering a sense of belonging and gradual social integration. Encourage emotional support strategies focusing on communication and positive reinforcement of small achievements.
