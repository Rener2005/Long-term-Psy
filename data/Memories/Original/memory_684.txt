# First Consultation

## User Background

Xiao Jun (pseudonym) is a student dealing with anxiety related to academic performance and the pressure of high expectations from himself and perceived expectations from his parents. He is reflective and seeks to better manage his emotional responses, particularly with upcoming exams.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Jun experiences symptoms of anxiety centered around performance and self-worth but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Jun feels anxious and overwhelmed due to the pressure of upcoming exams, resulting in physical symptoms such as shaking and tingling sensations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Jun sets high standards for himself, leading to intense pressure and fear of disappointment, yet demonstrates insight and openness to exploring alternative viewpoints.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Jun challenge perfectionistic thoughts and build a more balanced perception of his abilities. Develop strategies to help him manage academic stress and anxiety through a structured study plan and relaxation techniques.
