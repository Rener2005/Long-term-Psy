# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoxi (pseudonym) is a student facing high academic pressure and anxiety, particularly related to exams and the expectations she places on herself to achieve high grades.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxi experiences symptoms of anxiety triggered by academic stress and self-imposed high expectations, but she has no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxi feels overwhelmed by academic pressure, fearing that failure to achieve top scores will lead to disappointment for herself and her parents.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoxi's tendency to set high expectations leading to anxiety and panic, but also recognizes her insights into moments of past success and confidence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize CBT techniques to help Xiaoxi recognize and challenge negative thought patterns and develop study strategies to manage anxiety and enhance confidence.
