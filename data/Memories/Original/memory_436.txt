# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ming is currently living in a shelter and is feeling disappointed and helpless about his future. He is looking for a job to gain independence and financial stability.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming experiences anxiety and feelings of inadequacy but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels anxious about his future, particularly regarding job prospects and his current living situation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Ming's pervasive anxiety and negative self-perceptions but observes his openness to developing a practical plan for change.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Ming manage anxiety and challenge his beliefs about inadequacy. Encourage small, manageable steps toward job search goals and introduce anxiety management strategies, like deep breathing.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels slightly better about his anxiety but is confused about his future direction after discovering new interests.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Ming's eagerness to explore new interests balanced by his fear of missing out on other opportunities.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiao Ming in setting short-term goals to explore interests concurrently. Encourage the practice of self-reflection and mindfulness to reduce pressure and aid decision-making.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
Xiao Ming exhibits significant self-doubt when comparing himself to others but shows enthusiasm for exploring creative interests further.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Xiao Ming in developing skills in creative fields by setting practical steps to practice and improve. Employ CBT to address self-doubt and promote a growth mindset.
