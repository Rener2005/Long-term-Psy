# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wenwen (pseudonym) is a university student experiencing feelings of loneliness and anxiety, particularly related to social interactions and being ignored by dormmates. Despite these challenges, Wenwen is making efforts to expand her social circle and seeks to improve her relationships within her living environment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wenwen displays symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, primarily when managing peer relationships. She has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wenwen feels lonely and anxious due to being ignored by her dormmates, negatively impacting her confidence and focus on academics.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Wenwen’s strong desire for connection and recognition while noting her struggle with feelings of rejection and self-worth.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wenwen challenge negative self-perceptions and explore proactive strategies for improving dorm relationships. Encourage small, achievable social goals to reduce loneliness and build confidence.
