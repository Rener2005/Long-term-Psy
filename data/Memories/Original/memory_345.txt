# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming (pseudonym) is a student dealing with anxiety related to familial communication and social relationships. Li Ming often feels misunderstood in interactions, particularly with their mother, and desires to improve these connections for better emotional stability.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming experiences symptoms of anxiety regarding interpersonal relationships, though there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels anxious and forgotten, stemming from arguments with their mother and perceived distance from friends.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming deeply struggles with feelings of rejection and misunderstanding, yet demonstrates a willingness to explore healthier communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Li Ming in challenging the perception of being misunderstood, and foster skills for effective communication to strengthen important relationships.
