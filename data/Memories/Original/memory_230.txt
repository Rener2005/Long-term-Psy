# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, Mark (pseudonym), is an adult male who recently experienced a major disagreement with his wife, resulting in police mediation. He feels lost and constrained due to unresolved marital issues and seeks better communication strategies to manage relational conflicts healthily.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Mark experiences feelings of inadequacy and defensiveness during conflicts with his wife, which lead to escalations. He has a tendency toward feeling scrutinized and unappreciated but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Mark feels overwhelmed and marginalized during arguments with his wife, contributing to unsuccessful attempts to express himself calmly.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Mark battles with significant defensiveness and frustration in conflict situations but shows insight into the need for change and a willingness to reassess his interaction methods.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Mark challenge unproductive thoughts during conflicts and adopt new, effective communication strategies, such as using "I" statements and initiating calm discussion techniques.
