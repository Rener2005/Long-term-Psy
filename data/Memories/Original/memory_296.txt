[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yang exhibits symptoms of anxiety, particularly in relation to exams and social situations, but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

Yang feels tense and worried about upcoming exams, experiences difficulty concentrating due to persistent feelings of inadequacy and fear of judgment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Yang's tendency toward negative thinking and fear of social judgment but also observes a willingness to consider constructive strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yang identify and reframe negative thought patterns and build confidence. Develop methods for handling academic pressure and social anxiety.
