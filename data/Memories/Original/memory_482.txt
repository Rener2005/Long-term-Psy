# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Zhang is an adult female who recently discontinued lithium medication and experiences fluctuations in emotions, particularly an increase in anxiety. She is working on managing daily tasks and emotions more effectively, seeking stability in her routines.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Zhang reports symptoms consistent with anxiety, compounded by difficulties in managing emotions and stress. No formal diagnosis of a specific disorder is provided.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Zhang is experiencing increased anxiety and emotional instability since stopping her medication, feeling overwhelmed by daily tasks.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ms. Zhang is deeply affected by thoughts of inadequacy and failure but is open to developing new strategies for emotional regulation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ms. Zhang reframe negative thoughts and regain control by setting small, achievable daily goals to manage her anxiety.
