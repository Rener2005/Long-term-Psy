Wang has been feeling misunderstood and anxious about fitting in, particularly after an incident where classmates questioned Wang's mental state. Wang is interested in improving social skills and building more comfortable interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt in social situations, but there has been no formal diagnosis mentioned of a mental health disorder.

Wang feels anxious and misunderstood, grappling with self-doubt about social capabilities, particularly following negative interactions with classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Wang experiences social anxiety and avoidance tendencies but also shows openness to learning new social strategies and self-improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang develop practical social skills and challenge self-critical thoughts. Encourage practice in smaller, familiar settings to gradually build confidence.
