# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Han (pseudonym) is a professional dealing with high levels of anxiety, particularly related to work situations and communication. Han is highly sensitive to feeling unprepared and is driven by a perfectionist mindset, which often exacerbates stress in professional settings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Han experiences significant anxiety primarily surrounding phone interactions and demonstrates traits of perfectionism, but no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han feels anxious and overwhelmed due to a recent work incident involving a difficult client, which has intensified a longstanding fear of phone calls.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Han struggles with a pervasive need for control and perfection across both personal and professional domains, contributing to avoidance behaviors and heightened anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Han reframe phone calls from fear-inducing events to manageable conversations. Develop small, actionable strategies to reduce avoidance and ease into phone communication challenges.
