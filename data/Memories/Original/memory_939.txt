# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) recently began a new job and struggles with anxiety and feelings of exclusion in social settings. Zhang desires to improve social connections and confidence at work.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences social anxiety and feelings of inadequacy but has not reported a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels anxious and excluded in social settings at work, often interpreting situations negatively.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang's anxiety is fueled by negative thoughts and assumptions about social interactions, yet Zhang shows insight and willingness to adopt healthier perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge negative assumptions and gradually engage with colleagues through actionable steps like initiating conversations.
