# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is an adolescent facing significant challenges in managing family dynamics and academic expectations. He often feels misunderstood by his family, leading to anxiety and fear regarding potential conflicts.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly concerning familial relationships and self-worth. He has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels overwhelmed and anxious due to a recent argument with his mother about his academic performance, resulting in fears of being alone and not being good enough.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming struggles with expressing his emotions due to fear of conflict, yet shows a willingness to explore more peaceful communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming openly express feelings without fear of conflict and develop more constructive relational dynamics. Begin building confidence in self-worth and communication skills.
