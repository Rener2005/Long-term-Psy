# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Li is a professional struggling with anxiety and self-doubt, particularly after a significant project failure at work. This situation has left Xiao Li questioning personal abilities and feeling disconnected in personal relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt but has not been officially diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels lost and anxious following a project failure, with worries about future prospects and a sense of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Li holds onto negative self-conceptions and appears caught in a cycle of self-doubt, yet shows readiness to explore these feelings and challenge them.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Li challenge negative self-beliefs and recognize past successes, fostering a more realistic self-perception. Plan steps to re-engage with personal relationships.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li experiences confusion with persistent anxiety, despite some improvement in motivation at work, due to difficulties in balancing personal relationships.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Xiao Li struggles with anxiety regarding social interactions, often assuming negative outcomes, which leads to withdrawal.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Xiao Li in challenging anxious thoughts about social interactions using evidence and self-compassion. Practice focusing on the present to reduce social anxiety and fear of judgment.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels anxious yet safe to explore taking initiative in social contexts, reflecting a growing desire to strengthen personal relationships.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li's increased awareness of and openness to positive social experiences, although self-doubt still occasionally surfaces.

[Consultation Goals]  
Reinforce Xiao Li's awareness of positive social outcomes and encourage steps to reconnect with friends. Use CBT to focus on realistic evaluations of social interactions.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels more confident professionally, though uncertain about how to sustain new professional momentum and integrate skills into career advancement.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Li's eagerness to build on recent professional development, while facing normal anxieties about responsibilities and meeting expectations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiao Li in setting professional goals with actionable steps and using reflective practices to manage anxiety. Encourage a practical approach to applying new skills.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels optimistic and accomplished after achieving phase goals, but remains cautious about balancing long-term professional and personal goals.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li's positive motivation supported by a new social circle, while noting occasional concerns regarding long-term aspirations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Strengthen Xiao Li's boundary-setting and time management skills to maintain a healthy work-life balance. Encourage quality-focused productivity and regular self-assessment.

# Sixth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels confident and finds more meaning in life, yet is concerned about maintaining current momentum and avoiding past patterns of feeling lost.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li's effective use of goal-setting and reflection, along with a growing sense of self-compassion and understanding of past experiences.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Xiao Li in continuing the practice of self-compassion and reflective journaling as tools for sustaining progress. Encourage planning rewarding activities to reinforce positive habits and achievements.
