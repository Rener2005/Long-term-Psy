G (pseudonym) is a university student feeling isolated due to a lack of close friendships. G experiences loneliness and struggles with social engagement, especially when observing peers who seem more socially active.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
G shows signs of loneliness and social anxiety. No formal diagnosis has been disclosed, but G has expressed feelings of inadequacy in social contexts.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
G feels lonely and isolated, finding it difficult to motivate themselves to engage in activities or studies.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage G to participate in activities aligned with their interests to foster new social connections. Utilize CBT to address negative self-beliefs and enhance self-efficacy in social contexts.
