# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ming is a student who feels overwhelmed by academic pressures and family financial expectations. She is experiencing a decline in academic performance, which worries her due to the potential impact on scholarships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming experiences anxiety related to academic stress and self-worth but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels overwhelmed and anxious, struggling with feelings of inadequacy and pressure to meet her parents' expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Ming's tendency to internalize pressure from her own aspirations and family expectations, which affects her focus and leads to negative self-assessment.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to help Xiao Ming challenge and reframe negative thoughts, focusing on improving study habits and recognizing personal efforts rather than elusive goals.
