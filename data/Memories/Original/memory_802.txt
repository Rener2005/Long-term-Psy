# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a university student, faces anxiety and confusion stemming from conflicts in personal relationships, leading to feelings of isolation and inadequacy.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and isolated following a conflict with a roommate, leading to repeated negative thoughts about their relationship.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user is caught in a cycle of negative thinking and self-doubt but is open to considering alternative, more positive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user identify and challenge distorted thoughts, encouraging them to approach conflict resolution with effective communication strategies.
