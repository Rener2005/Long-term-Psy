# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Little Kitty (pseudonym) is a young student dealing with feelings of embarrassment and anxiety related to bedwetting. Recent teasing at school has intensified these feelings, making bedtime especially stressful.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Little Kitty experiences symptoms of anxiety, particularly around nighttime due to bedwetting incidents, but there is no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Little Kitty feels embarrassed and anxious about bedwetting, which has led to a heightened fear and difficulty relaxing before bedtime.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Little Kitty's struggle with negative self-judgment, but also notes a readiness to explore more compassionate and potentially kinder perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Little Kitty reframe negative thoughts and develop a more understanding attitude towards self. Establish a calming bedtime routine to mitigate anxiety and improve relaxation.
