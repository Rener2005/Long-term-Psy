# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ai is a university student preparing to study abroad, facing anxiety and self-doubt about living independently and managing various responsibilities. She expresses concerns about handling finances, cooking, and potential feelings of loneliness abroad.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ai exhibits anxiety related to major life changes and low self-confidence in managing independent living, though she has not reported any formal mental health diagnoses.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ai feels anxious about important exams and the impending change of studying abroad, doubting her ability to cope with new responsibilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Xiao Ai experiences considerable stress and self-doubt but is receptive to exploring skills she already possesses that can aid her transition.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help Xiao Ai recognize and build upon her existing skills and strengths, and develop practical strategies to manage anxiety and responsibilities.

# Second Consultation
