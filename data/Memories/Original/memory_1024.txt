Recently, Xiao Zhao has faced setbacks in studies, notably failing two courses last semester, which amplifies the concern of disappointing family members.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Zhao shows symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem related to academic performance and familial expectations but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Zhao feels overwhelmed and anxious about academic pressures, coupled with a fear of disappointing family due to poor academic performance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Zhao is caught in a cycle of negative thinking regarding inadequacy and failure but is open to using compassionate self-reflection to challenge these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Zhao identify and challenge negative self-beliefs and adopt a more compassionate self-view. Introduce relaxation techniques to manage academic stress and anxiety.
