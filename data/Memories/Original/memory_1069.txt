# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a university student in their final year, experiences significant anxiety related to academic performance and leadership responsibilities. They are reflective about these challenges and aim to improve their emotional management and focus on studies.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and a persistent sense of inadequacy but has not received a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and exhausted by the pressure of maintaining academic performance while managing class leadership.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's struggle with perfectionism and self-doubt but notes their willingness to explore more supportive and balanced perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge negative thoughts and establish boundaries to manage their academic and leadership responsibilities. Initiate strategies for reducing stress and improving focus.

# Second Consultation
