# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Rosie (pseudonym) is a mother feeling overwhelmed after discovering she's pregnant again, expressing concern over managing finances and her current children's needs. She has a history of feelings of inadequacy, stemming primarily from her role as a parent and her children's reluctance to attend school.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Rosie exhibits symptoms of anxiety related to financial instability and parenting challenges but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Rosie feels overwhelmed and anxious, expressing deep concerns about financial stability and her ability to provide adequately for her children's futures.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Rosie's pervasive sense of inadequacy and constant self-criticism but notes her willingness to explore alternative thoughts and seek support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help Rosie challenge negative self-perceptions and develop healthier coping mechanisms for her anxiety. Plan to explore resources for community support and financial planning.
