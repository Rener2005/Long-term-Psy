# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Deborah (pseudonym) is an adult who struggles with anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, particularly in social situations and work-related scenarios. She has a desire to improve her interpersonal interactions and self-perception, seeking to gain confidence and emotional resilience.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Deborah experiences symptoms of social anxiety but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Deborah feels anxious and guilty about leaving the house and interacting with others, worrying about judgment and inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Deborah struggles with negative self-perceptions, yet demonstrates an insightful awareness and willingness to explore cognitive reframing techniques.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) strategies to help Deborah identify and challenge negative thoughts, develop coping mechanisms for social anxiety, and foster a supportive mental framework for social interactions.
