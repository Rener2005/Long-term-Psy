# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaocheng (pseudonym) is a student who deals with disappointment regarding academic performance and struggles with family expectations, particularly from their mother, who is critical of their study habits. Xiaocheng is striving to improve grades and feels overwhelmed by the pressure at home.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaocheng experiences feelings of inadequacy and pressure-induced anxiety but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaocheng feels overwhelmed and anxious due to not meeting parental expectations, internalizing a sense of failure and low motivation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaocheng tends to be self-critical, fueled by external familial evaluations, showing a willingness to challenge these perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive-behavioral techniques to help Xiaocheng reframe negative perceptions and enhance motivation. Develop strategies to manage academic tasks and reduce familial pressure.
