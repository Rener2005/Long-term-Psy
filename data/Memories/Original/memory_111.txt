[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Mr. Li experiences significant stress and frustration related to parenting challenges, but there's no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Mr. Li reshape his thought patterns, focusing on understanding his son's perspective to foster open lines of communication and reduce conflicts.
