# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Eve (pseudonym) is a woman who recently completed a stay in a treatment facility and is in the process of rebuilding her life. She is navigating the challenges of reconnecting with her son after a period of separation due to his time in foster care. Eve is adjusting to a new living environment and often experiences anxiety related to her responsibilities at home and uncertainties regarding her role as a mother.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Eve is coping with anxiety linked to reintegrating into family life and insecurity about her parenting abilities. There is no formal diagnosis mentioned in her self-report. Her anxiety manifests as self-doubt and an overwhelming sense of inadequacy, particularly in managing her family responsibilities.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Eve feels anxious and uncertain about reconnecting with her son, fearing rejection and inadequacy in her role as a mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Eve is concerned about rebuilding her relationship with her son, displaying self-doubt and a fear of failure. However, she is open to exploring and adopting new approaches to foster that connection.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Eve challenge negative thoughts regarding her parenting and develop strategies for connecting with her son. Encourage positive reinforcement and gradual, engaging interactions to build the relationship.
