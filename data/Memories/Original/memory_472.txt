# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a parent who currently feels overwhelmed and anxious due to concerns about his son's conflicts with classmates, which have placed pressure on family dynamics. He is reflective on his parenting and seeks to improve family communication.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences symptoms of anxiety related to perceived parenting inadequacies and has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious and frustrated, overwhelmed with the pressure of managing his son's school conflicts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang Wei's tendency to internalize his son's behavior as a personal failure, yet notes his openness to exploring more constructive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei address negative self-perceptions regarding parenting, and develop actionable strategies for improving family communication and reducing anxiety.
