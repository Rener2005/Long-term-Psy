## User Background
[Personal Profile]

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of stress, anxiety, and low self-esteem and has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder. Physical symptoms like headaches and difficulty sleeping are present.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed and anxious about their internship performance, accompanied by self-doubt and feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li's tendency to focus on negative experiences over positive ones, alongside a desire to build confidence and reduce peer comparison.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li identify and challenge negative thoughts, focus on personal achievements, and develop resilience to peer comparisons.
