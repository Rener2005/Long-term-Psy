# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu (pseudonym) is a professional dealing with feelings of anxiety and shame related to a recent divorce. She expresses concerns about how her colleagues perceive her and experiences difficulty focusing at work due to this stress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu experiences anxiety related to social interactions and self-perception but has not reported a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu feels anxious and isolated, fearing judgment from her colleagues about her divorce.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Liu is heavily influenced by negative self-perceptions, although she shows a willingness to reframe her thoughts and recognize personal growth.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu challenge and reframe her negative beliefs about divorce, and encourage small steps towards engaging socially at work to reduce feelings of isolation.
