The user is an individual experiencing increased anxiety, particularly manifesting through compulsive handwashing due to fear of germs and illness. The user works in an environment where interactions with others, like customers, can exacerbate these fears.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms consistent with anxiety and potential obsessive-compulsive behaviors related to cleanliness, though no formal diagnosis has been mentioned.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user is feeling anxious about cleanliness and fears contamination, which is affecting social interactions and daily functioning.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user struggling with pervasive anxiety about germs, resulting in behavior patterns that isolate them from others, yet they show motivation to change.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user Aidentify and challenge catastrophic thoughts related to contamination. Implement specific strategies to reduce handwashing and manage anxiety in social settings.
