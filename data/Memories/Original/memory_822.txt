[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ahong presents symptoms of social anxiety, particularly manifesting in avoidance of eye contact and fear of negative judgment, though there is no formal diagnosis mentioned.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
