The user experiences ongoing doubts and anxiety about their career path and self-worth, though there is no mention of a formal mental health diagnosis.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user experiences persistent self-doubt and cyclical negative thinking but is willing to challenge these beliefs and explore a more balanced self-perception.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user reframe negative thoughts and recognize personal achievements through journaling. Encourage exploration of values and passions to align academic efforts with personal interests.
