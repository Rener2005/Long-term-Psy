# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is an adult male facing challenges in his marriage. He struggles with communication issues, which have led to emotional distress and difficulty balancing marital and work obligations. Recent tensions include a "cold war" with his wife following arguments, influencing his mood and professional focus.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of emotional distress connected to relationship difficulties but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels isolated and anxious due to tensions in his marriage, leading to avoidance behavior and difficulty focusing at work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li's anticipatory anxiety about potential conflicts, highlighting a pattern of avoidance driven by fear of escalated arguments.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li in using cognitive behavioral techniques to approach his wife calmly, utilize "I" statements to express feelings constructively, and develop strategies to manage pre-conversation anxiety.
