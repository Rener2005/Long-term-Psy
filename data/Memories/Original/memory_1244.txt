# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Miss Li is a professional struggling with anxiety and self-doubt, exacerbated by workplace stress and project challenges. She feels pressure to prove herself, often influenced by her familial expectations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Miss Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly in professional contexts, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Miss Li feels increasingly anxious and down due to overwhelming work demands and self-doubt about her contributions to team projects.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Miss Li places substantial pressure on herself, tied to how her colleagues might perceive her, yet she is open to exploring the origins of these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to help Miss Li challenge negative self-perceptions and develop strategies for managing workplace stress and anxiety.
