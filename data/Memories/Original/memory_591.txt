# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, whose name is not disclosed, is an elder adult residing in a nursing home, experiencing health challenges due to a recent stroke. They struggle with accepting medication and lifestyle changes and often feel lonely within the institutional environment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of depression and anxiety related to their health condition and significant lifestyle changes. They have not disclosed any formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and frustrated by recent health changes, particularly regarding medication and lifestyle adaptations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a sense of resignation towards change and a belief that they are too old to alter their habits, yet there's a willingness to explore social connections for motivation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user identify opportunities for positive change in their routine and build social connections to support health improvements.
