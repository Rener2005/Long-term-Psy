# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Little Y (pseudonym) is a university student who struggles with feelings of loneliness and anxiety, particularly in social situations. Little Y is navigating the complexities of forming and maintaining friendships, wishing to increase social confidence and inclusivity among classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Little Y experiences social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Little Y feels lost and anxious about fitting in with classmates, experiencing fear of being disliked or not belonging in social settings.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Little Y interprets social situations as personal rejections, yet shows a willingness to challenge negative thoughts and consider alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Little Y recognize and reframe negative thoughts in social contexts. Encourage small steps towards social engagement and provide practical strategies for initiating conversations.
