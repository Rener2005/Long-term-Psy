# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chen (pseudonym) is a woman dealing with significant emotional distress following a recent surgical procedure related to her pregnancy. She is experiencing profound grief and emotional upheaval, particularly in family dynamics with her in-laws, and is seeking ways to manage her emotional responses better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chen is experiencing symptoms of grief, anxiety, and emotional instability following a pregnancy-related surgery, though there has been no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen feels overwhelmingly sad and lost after her surgery, experiencing intense anger, helplessness, and a sense of grief.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Chen's struggle with communicating her needs and emotions, especially with her in-laws, though there is a willingness to explore how to express her feelings effectively.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to aid Chen in articulating her emotions and setting boundaries with family members, encouraging her to practice self-compassion and communication skills.
