# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoxue is a student facing feelings of low motivation and boredom, particularly in the context of academic pressures, social connectivity, and recent changes in friendships. Xiaoxue expresses concerns about academic performance and has started feeling disconnected from classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxue experiences symptoms indicative of anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly around academic and social situations, though there is no formal diagnosis reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxue feels stuck and demotivated, overwhelmed by recent academic challenges and a persistent cycle of negative thoughts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices Xiaoxue's tendency to engage in negative self-comparisons and avoidance behaviors but also observes an emerging readiness to challenge these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral strategies to help Xiaoxue confront and reassess negative thought patterns. Encourage identification of personal achievements and initiate steps to reconnect socially with classmates.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxue is feeling lonely and disconnected, with a heightened fear of social rejection following changes in friendship dynamics.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoxue's deep-seated fear of social rejection, alongside a cautious openness to reconnect with classmates.

[Consultation Goals]  
Facilitate Xiaoxue in exploring evidence-based challenges to negative social thoughts. Encourage proactive, small steps towards re-establishing social connections and assessing outcomes to mitigate loneliness.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxue is experiencing anxiety regarding academic performance, though school art activities provide some positive distraction.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoxue's struggle to balance positive experiences with academic challenges, recognizing self-imposed pressure and the potential for peer influence on self-esteem.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage establishment of a balanced routine integrating academic work with leisure activities. Promote self-reflection on pressures and initiate open dialogue with family about these experiences.

# Fifth Consultation
