# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) is an individual struggling with housing instability, anxiety, and substance use issues. Zhang recently experienced the loss of housing, which compounded feelings of being lost and anxious about life management, along with difficulties related to drug use.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences anxiety, instability due to housing insecurity, and challenges with substance dependency, although there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels lost and anxious following housing loss, and is apprehensive about seeking help for substance use, with fears of losing control.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's fear and apprehension about change and the uncertainty tied to sobriety. Despite this, Zhang shows a willingness to explore options for finding stability and support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Zhang in managing anxiety, initiating small steps towards coping without substances, and exploring stable housing opportunities. Foster Zhang’s sense of agency and control in addressing life challenges.
