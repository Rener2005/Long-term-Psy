# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Lin (pseudonym) is a college student who is struggling with feelings of anxiety and stress related to academic performance and social interactions. Lin often feels overwhelmed by academic pressures and finds it difficult to maintain harmonious relationships with peers and roommates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lin has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder but experiences significant anxiety and stress, which affects social and academic aspects of life.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lin feels anxious and overwhelmed due to slipping grades and arguments with a roommate, contributing to a stressful home environment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives Lin as struggling with stress from both academic performance and interpersonal issues, yet willing to explore techniques for managing anxiety and communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Lin manage anxiety, improve communication skills, and create productive study habits. Introduce relaxation exercises for stress reduction.
