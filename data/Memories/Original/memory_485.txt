# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is a professional in a family services role, who struggles with anxiety related to work performance and decision-making. Recent events include handling complex family cases at work, leading to self-doubt about capability and efficiency.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt concerning professional competence but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels anxious and overwhelmed due to the pressure of handling a challenging family case and perceives a lack of impact in their role.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang experiences cognitive distortions—such as jumping to conclusions about their impact—yet shows openness to considering different perspectives on their work.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang recognize and challenge negative thoughts, while developing realistic strategies for professional stress management and achievement acknowledgment.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Consultation Goals]

# Third Consultation
