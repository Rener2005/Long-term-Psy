# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Lin is a university student adapting to a new environment and struggling with nightmares and extreme anxiety related to academic pressure. Recent events include starting university, moving to a new place, and the strain of new academic challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lin experiences heightened anxiety, sleep disturbances due to nightmares, and stress about academic performance. There is no mention of a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lin feels unsettled, anxious, and scared due to recurring nightmares and academic pressure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Lin's high level of anxiety due to new environmental stressors, compounded by a lack of effective coping mechanisms for stress and sleep disturbances.

[Consultation Goals]  
To introduce cognitive behavioral techniques that help Lin develop calming pre-sleep routines, challenge negative thoughts, and manage anxiety related to nightmares and academic stress.
