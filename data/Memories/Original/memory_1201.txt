# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
H (pseudonym) is a university student struggling with feelings of anger, frustration, and resentment, partly due to past experiences in childhood. H is currently trying to navigate social dynamics and improve personal relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
H experiences persistent feelings of anger and jealousy and has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
H feels anger and frustration, particularly when observing classmates who appear carefree and happy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that H's feelings are deeply rooted in past experiences, and H is willing to explore healthier outlets and perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help H identify triggers and express anger in non-destructive ways. Develop strategies for building self-worth and fostering positive social interactions.
