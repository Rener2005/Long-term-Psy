# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyi (pseudonym) is a high school student who feels lonely and struggles to connect with peers and family. Recently, Xiaoyi has experienced feelings of isolation and frustration in social situations, both at school and home. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyi exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health condition.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyi feels isolated and undervalued, exacerbated by unsuccessful attempts to connect with peers and perceived lack of comprehension from family.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoyi's harsh self-judgment and reactive frustration when faced with social challenges, yet sees potential for empathy and self-reflection.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help Xiaoyi reframe negative thoughts and improve social interaction through manageable steps, like initiating small peer interactions.
