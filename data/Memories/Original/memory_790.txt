# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yan Yan (pseudonym) is a university student experiencing difficulties adjusting to university life. She feels lonely and misses her parents. Yan Yan is looking to build her confidence in social settings and wishes to enjoy her time at college by making friends and feeling comfortable in social interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yan Yan exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and low confidence in social situations, with no formal mental health disorder diagnosis reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yan Yan feels down, lonely, and struggles with confidence, feeling lost and disinterested in her current situation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Yan Yan's fear of judgment and insecurity in social interactions, but notes her willingness to explore strategies to build confidence and manage anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Yan Yan in identifying and challenging negative thoughts regarding social interactions. Start with manageable social goals to gradually increase her confidence.
