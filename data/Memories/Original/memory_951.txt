# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
An An is a high school student feeling pressured by academic expectations and seeking exploration of personal interests like music. An An has experienced conflict with family over future aspirations and seeks a more supportive environment for personal growth.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
An An exhibits symptoms of stress and anxiety but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
An An feels overwhelmed and frustrated due to conflicts with family about pursuing music alongside academic responsibilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that An An experiences internal conflict between personal interests and external expectations, showing a desire for self-expression without causing harm.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help An An express emotions constructively and balance personal interests with academic demands. Encourage strategies for managing stress and familial conflicts.
