# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhen (pseudonym) is a woman who has recently experienced the loss of her child and is struggling with grief and communication issues with her husband. She feels criticized for not showing emotions and is overwhelmed by the situation.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhen experiences symptoms of grief, emotional repression, and relationship strain, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhen feels lost, sad, and overwhelmed, struggling to express her emotions and feeling isolated in her grief.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhen is struggling with communication due to fear of criticism and the burden of being emotionally strong, but she shows some willingness to explore new ways of expressing her grief and reconnecting with her husband.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhen express her needs without feeling the pressure to be strong, and improve communication with her husband by fostering mutual understanding and connection.
