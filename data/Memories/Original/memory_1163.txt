# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a university student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety related to academic performance, often comparing themselves unfavorably to peers. Li feels lost and overwhelmed, particularly with course pressures, and exhibits an interest in art that has been set aside due to academic demands.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels stressed and lost due to academic pressures and perceives a sense of emptiness, compounded by negative self-comparisons and regret about not pursuing previous passions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li struggles with deep-rooted feelings of inadequacy and regret but is open to reconnecting with past interests and exploring self-perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li challenge negative thoughts, explore passions without guilt, and develop a balanced approach to academics and personal interests.
