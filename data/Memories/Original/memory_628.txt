Xiaoc experiences symptoms consistent with low self-esteem and anxiety due to social isolation and bullying but has not disclosed a formal mental health diagnosis.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoc's tendency toward self-critical thoughts and a need for empathetic connection, noting potential strengths in creativity and kindness.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoc identify and challenge negative self-perceptions, promoting self-compassion and emotional resilience. Introduce calming strategies like deep breathing to manage overwhelming emotions.
