# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Jiajia (pseudonym) is a student grappling with anxiety related to academic performance and parental expectations. She expresses concerns about failing to meet standards and is reflective about her achievements and challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Jiajia experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly around academic tasks, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiajia feels anxious and overwhelmed due to increased school workload and perceived parental disappointment from her exam performance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Jiajia is burdened by high expectations and self-doubt, but shows a willingness to reflect on past successes as a potential avenue for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Jiajia challenge the belief that perfection is required for acceptance and to encourage a focus on effort and achievable goals.
