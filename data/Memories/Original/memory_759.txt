# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhao (pseudonym) is a school-aged student who has recently transitioned to a new school environment where he struggles with academic pressures, particularly in English and mathematics. Zhao describes gaming as a significant part of his downtime and is eager to find a balance between his interests and academic responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhao experiences symptoms of academic stress tied to feelings of inadequacy and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao feels overwhelmed by schoolwork, particularly English and math, and uses gaming as an emotional escape.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhao's reliance on gaming to cope with academic stress and notes his past academic confidence has been shaken due to the new school environment.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhao recognize and challenge negative thoughts while developing a structured study routine. Aim to gradually reduce gaming time by increasing engagement in academic tasks and rewarding study efforts.
