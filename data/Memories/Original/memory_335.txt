# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Li is a single mother navigating the challenges of balancing her job and raising her daughter. She often feels overwhelmed by her responsibilities and is concerned about her daughter’s well-being, safety, and academic performance. Ms. Li aims to improve communication and foster a supportive relationship with her child.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Li experiences symptoms of anxiety concerning her parenting role and her daughter’s safety and performance. She has expressed doubts about her parenting skills but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Li feels anxious and overwhelmed due to her daughter’s changing behavior, expressing self-doubt about her effectiveness as a parent.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Ms. Li’s anxiety as rooted in her concern for her daughter’s safety and well-being, with a tendency to imagine worst-case scenarios. Nevertheless, Ms. Li shows a keen willingness to explore new communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ms. Li challenge negative thoughts and enhance trust in her parenting ability. Encourage open communication with her daughter to foster a supportive relationship and alleviate anxiety.
