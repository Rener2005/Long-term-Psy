# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a student, grapples with feelings of inadequacy and anger, often stemming from peer interactions on social media. The user is introspective and recognizes the need for emotional regulation and improved interpersonal relations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, characterized by anger and frustration in social settings, but has no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels angry and hurt due to negative comments made by peers on social media, leading to feelings of worthlessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency to react with self-deprecating thoughts and a strong desire to be respected, though the user is open to reframing perceptions and gaining self-assurance.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help the user counter negative self-beliefs and cultivate self-assurance. Encourage positive reinforcement by focusing on personal achievements and promoting involvement in confidence-building activities.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user continues to feel isolated and angry, struggling to understand peer perceptions and seeking acceptance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user's self-protective actions inadvertently exacerbate feelings of isolation but acknowledges the user's willingness to explore new communication approaches.

[Consultation Goals]  
Aid the user in developing non-confrontational communication strategies, emphasizing "I" statements and the understanding of others' perspectives to foster better peer relationships.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels misunderstood and unsupported by family, compounded by a recent argument, leading to frustration and isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives the user's communication challenges with family, stemming from defensive dynamics, yet the user shows interest in adopting more effective expression techniques.

[Consultation Goals]  
Guide the user in employing "I" statements for clearer communication and manage anxiety with deep breathing techniques before conversations to enhance familial relationships.

# Sixth Consultation
