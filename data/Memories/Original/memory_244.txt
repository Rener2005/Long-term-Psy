# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Fu (pseudonym) is a high school student navigating the emotional turmoil of her parents' recent divorce. She struggles with feelings of loneliness and inadequacy, often expressing worry about the instability in her life. Xiao Fu's self-worth has been affected, influencing her academic performance and personal relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Fu exhibits symptoms of anxiety and mild depression but has not been formally diagnosed. Her expressions of self-harm indicate a need for immediate support and the development of healthier coping mechanisms.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Fu feels overwhelmed and isolated due to her parents' divorce, resulting in anxiety that impairs her concentration on academic work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Fu's profound loneliness and acknowledges her bravery in sharing her struggles, highlighting her openness to exploring new ways to manage her emotions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Fu challenge catastrophic thoughts and develop healthier emotional expressions, such as poetry. Encourage seeking support from friends to alleviate loneliness.
