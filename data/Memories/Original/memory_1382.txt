# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wei is experiencing anxiety and regret concerning a past action involving a student and is worried about the potential impact on his current relationship. He is seeking guidance on managing these feelings and exploring open communication with his girlfriend.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wei exhibits symptoms of anxiety linked to relationship concerns and experiences persistent feelings of regret regarding past actions.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wei feels anxious and remorseful about his past actions, which impacts his current relationship, causing a sense of living dishonestly with his girlfriend.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Wei's struggle with guilt and anxiety, noting his fear of being perceived as untrustworthy but also his willingness to explore honesty in relationships.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wei manage anxiety and explore honest communication strategies with his girlfriend, emphasizing the value of vulnerability and trust-building.
