# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a male and is currently grappling with intense feelings of anger and insecurity related to his girlfriend's past relationships. He finds it challenging to communicate effectively without arguments arising.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user showcases symptoms of anxiety and anger driven by feelings of inadequacy but has mentioned no formal diagnosis of a mental health condition.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels consumed by anger and insecurity linked to his girlfriend's past, manifesting in fears of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that the user's emotions are highly tied to perceived competition with his girlfriend's ex, yet he shows openness to exploring techniques to better manage his emotional responses.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge intrusive thoughts and perceptions of inadequacy. Focus on strategies to improve communication and manage feelings of anger.
