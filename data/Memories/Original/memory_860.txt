[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaotong exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem. There is no mention of a formal diagnosis, but she frequently feels overwhelmed and pressured by familial and social expectations.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Xiaotong experiences significant pressure from external sources and internalizes these expectations, leading to self-critical thoughts and loneliness.

[Consultation Goals]
