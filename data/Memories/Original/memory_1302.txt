# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a young adult navigating experiences of emotional distress including loneliness, self-doubt, and anxiety. Recent life challenges involve shifting friendships and increased work stress. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and depression, alongside a pervasive sense of inadequacy and self-doubt. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user reports feeling low, burdened by a sense of emptiness and isolation following the estrangement from a close friend.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency towards self-blame and negative self-assessment, but also a willingness to consider reframing these thoughts with support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to challenge negative self-beliefs, encouraging compassionate self-reflection and fostering new social connections for emotional support.

# Second Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
