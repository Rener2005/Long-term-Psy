# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is an adult professional who struggles with feelings of anxiety and overwhelm, particularly related to work pressures and deadlines. Li Ming has been attempting to balance work commitments with personal well-being and seeks to develop coping strategies to manage stress effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming exhibits symptoms of anxiety related to work stress but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels overwhelmed by work-related stress, experiencing persistent anxiety and a racing mind.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li Ming's tendency to focus on negative outcomes and inadequacies, yet there is an acknowledgment of successes and a willingness to implement new coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming recognize and challenge negative thoughts related to work performance and develop a positive routine to alleviate stress and anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]
