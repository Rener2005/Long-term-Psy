[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A-Jing exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, with no formal diagnosis disclosed.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help A-Jing challenge negative self-comparisons and foster a more balanced self-image. Develop strategies for improving academic focus and social engagement.
