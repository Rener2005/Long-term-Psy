[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li exhibits signs of attentional difficulties but has not been formally diagnosed with Attention Deficit Disorder or any related condition.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral strategies to help Li manage distractions during class, fostering self-reassurance and techniques such as note-taking to enhance focus.
