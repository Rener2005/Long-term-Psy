# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Daisy is an adult struggling with intense emotions, particularly anger and feelings of being overwhelmed, both personally and professionally. She has a history of trauma from her childhood and is interested in managing her emotional responses more effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Daisy experiences significant levels of stress and anxiety, especially in work-related situations, but there is no mention of a formal diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Daisy feels anger and frustration are easily triggered, often leading to feelings of guilt after such episodes.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a pattern of stress-induced reactivity in Daisy's emotions, as well as links to past feelings of powerlessness.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to help Daisy identify triggers and practice reframing her thoughts to manage emotional responses better. Reinforce the development of coping mechanisms for stress and anxiety.
