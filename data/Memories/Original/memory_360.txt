# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a mother named Hana who juggles responsibilities from parenting and work, striving to balance these roles effectively. She experiences feelings of anxiety and seeks strategies to manage her emotional responses better while maintaining clarity and calmness throughout her daily routine.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user struggles with anxiety, particularly in interpersonal and professional settings, and often harbors negative self-judgments related to her roles and responsibilities. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been stated.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels a mix of pride and anxiety, mainly centered around engaging in social situations and the fear of judgment or embarrassment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's genuine efforts to engage socially, coupled with significant anxiety that stems from self-consciousness and fear of negative evaluation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage the user to challenge negative thoughts and focus on building confidence through social interactions, employing cognitive behavioral techniques to reframe fears and facilitate a more positive self-perception.
