# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is an individual who has recently experienced the loss of their mother, leading to feelings of loneliness and anxiety about the future. They express concerns about managing life independently and have a history that includes moving to another city for college, indicating past capability.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences significant anxiety and feelings of inadequacy following a personal loss, but there is no indication of a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and overwhelmed due to the recent loss of their mother, leading to worries about handling life independently.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's struggle with self-doubt and loneliness but also notes a willingness to acknowledge the extremity of their negative thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user reframe negative thoughts and reconnect with activities that bring joy, such as starting with small goals like walking and journaling.
