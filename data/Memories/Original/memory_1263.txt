[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Beibei displays signs of separation anxiety typical for children transitioning to new environments but has not been formally diagnosed with any psychological disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Beibei feels anxious about attending kindergarten and is scared of interacting with unfamiliar peers and teachers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes initial anxiety and apprehension in Beibei but also observes openness to comfort measures and engagement through familiar and enjoyable activities.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to aid Beibei in developing coping strategies for separation anxiety, such as bringing familiar items to kindergarten for emotional comfort. Encourage gradual interaction with peers through practiced scenarios.
