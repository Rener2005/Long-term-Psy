# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
J is a university student preparing for exams and graduate studies. Struggling with a disrupted study routine due to the pandemic, J expresses feelings of anxiety, overwhelmed by pressure and self-doubt about academic performance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
J exhibits symptoms of anxiety linked to academic stress but has not disclosed a formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
J feels overwhelmed and anxious about academic demands, experiencing difficulty with focus and reduced confidence.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes J's persistent self-doubt and reactive emotional state, yet identifies a readiness to engage in constructive strategies for managing stress.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help J identify and challenge negative self-perceptions, while setting achievable study goals to build confidence progressively.
