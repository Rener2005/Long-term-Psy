# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu (pseudonym) is a student who feels anxious and upset due to a recent incident in which a teacher accused him of cheating. Liu enjoys studying math but the accusation has caused distress and anxiety about facing the teacher and attending class.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu experiences anxiety and stress related to the fear of judgment and his damaged reputation, affecting focus and emotional well-being.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu feels stressed and embarrassed about being accused of cheating, leading to anxiety about attending math class and facing the teacher.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Liu's struggle with feelings of injustice and fear of judgment, which hinders his ability to engage in class.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu reframe the situation, challenge negative thoughts of being labeled a cheater, and develop relaxation methods to manage anxiety before interactions with the teacher.
