# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Layla is a dedicated individual juggling multiple roles and responsibilities, including being a mother, maintaining her job, and organizing events. She experiences a strong pursuit of perfection and struggles with guilt when she feels she falls short, particularly regarding family and work balance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Layla exhibits symptoms of stress and perfectionism, but she has not indicated a formal diagnosis of any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Layla feels overwhelmed by the pressure of striving for perfection and experiences intense guilt over perceived failures.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Layla's perfectionistic tendencies lead to significant stress and emotional discomfort when facing unanticipated events or personal lapses.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) to help Layla challenge unrealistic expectations and establish more attainable standards. Encourage self-compassion and strategies to manage stress effectively.
