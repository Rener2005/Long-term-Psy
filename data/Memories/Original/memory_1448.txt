# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaotu is a teenager navigating challenges with familial relationships, particularly with his father, around issues such as phone usage and future aspirations. Recently, conflicts have arisen over perceived control, escalating tensions within the household. Xiaotu is eager to improve communication and develop a better understanding between himself and his father.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaotu experiences frustration and anxiety linked to family dynamics and academic stress. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaotu feels frustrated and trapped due to conflicts with his father about phone usage, contributing to feelings of being misunderstood and controlled.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaotu is eager to be heard and to demonstrate responsibility, indicating a strong desire to find a solution through better communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaotu express his feelings constructively and explore alternative communication strategies with his father to foster mutual understanding.
