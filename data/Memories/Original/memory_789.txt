# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao G is a young individual dealing with feelings of loneliness and frustration following their parents' divorce. They seek comfort in video gaming and struggle with a sense of disconnection from family.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao G experiences symptoms of loneliness, anxiety regarding family interactions, and potential gaming addiction, but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorders.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao G feels down and uses video games as an escape from confronting personal and familial issues, resulting in feelings of emptiness when not gaming.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Xiao G's reluctance to open up to parental figures due to a fear of rejection and judgment, showing a desire to express feelings but lacking confidence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Empower Xiao G with cognitive behavioral techniques to articulate feelings and build communication strategies with parents. Encourage reduction of dependency on gaming as a coping mechanism.
