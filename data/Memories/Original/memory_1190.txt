# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
J (pseudonym) is an individual navigating the complexity of social interactions and personal authenticity. They are currently exploring how to develop a unique social style and often feel caught between admiration for others and self-acceptance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
J experiences feelings of inadequacy and anxiety in social situations, as well as hesitation and overthinking; however, no formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
J feels lost and inadequate in the face of admired social skills, struggling with a desire to mimic others while fearing inauthenticity.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes J’s conflict between wanting acceptance and staying true to themselves, noting a readiness to explore personal authenticity in social engagement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist J in identifying adaptive behaviors from admired individuals while nurturing their own style, and set practical goals to reduce anxiety in social settings.
