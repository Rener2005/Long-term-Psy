# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Song is a student preparing for graduate school entrance exams who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and motivation related to academic performance. Song feels pressure from family expectations and is reflective about personal study habits and achievements.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Song experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt related to academic pressure but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Song feels overwhelmed by academic pressures and envious of peers' successes, leading to doubts about personal motivation and capabilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Song's tendency to self-critique and struggle with maintaining motivation, but notes a readiness to explore practical solutions for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Song challenge self-doubt, set manageable study goals, and cultivate self-compassion and realistic expectations regarding academic performance.
