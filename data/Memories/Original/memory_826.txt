Yang (pseudonym) is concerned about her relationship with her roommate, as it causes tension and anxiety, impacting her sleep and daily life. Despite difficulties, Yang is open to finding ways to improve this relationship and her emotional management.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yang experiences anxiety primarily related to social interactions and self-worth, although no formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yang feels anxious and overwhelmed due to conflicts with her roommate, leading to a sense of isolation and inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Yang's negative self-perception and avoidance tendencies, but also notes her readiness to explore healthier perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yang identify and challenge distorted thoughts about her roommate, and develop effective communication strategies to manage conflicts constructively.
