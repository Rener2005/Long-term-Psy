Zhang Wei experiences anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang Wei's issue with negative self-comparison and self-criticism, yet notes a willingness to develop self-compassion and healthier thought patterns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei challenge cognitive distortions and cultivate self-compassion. Introduce practical strategies like mindfulness to manage stress and anxiety.
