# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a parent concerned about his son's behavior at school and its impact on the family dynamic. He experiences anxiety over parenting effectiveness and desires to improve family communication and his son's social well-being.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei demonstrates symptoms of anxiety primarily centered around parenting concerns, without any disclosed formal diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious and overwhelmed by his son's troubles at school, leading to tensions at home and self-doubt about his parenting abilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang Wei is caught in a cycle of self-blame and parental anxiety but is open to exploring alternative perspectives and solutions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei identify and challenge negative thought patterns, with a focus on improving communication and reducing self-imposed pressure regarding his son's behavior.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
