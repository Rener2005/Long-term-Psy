# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
D (pseudonym) is a student dealing with feelings of pressure and anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and familial expectations. D is eager to balance personal aspirations with these external demands and improve communication within familial relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
D experiences symptoms of anxiety related to performance in academic and social environments but has not mentioned a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
D feels anxious and overwhelmed due to pressure to meet academic expectations, particularly fearing disappointment from their mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes D's struggle with equating self-worth to academic success and notes the impact of parental expectations on D's self-esteem and performance anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help D challenge thoughts linking self-worth to academic performance, and develop coping strategies for managing familial pressure. Encourage reflection on personal achievements beyond grades.
