# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoxin (pseudonym) is a student who experiences anxiety and loneliness, particularly missing their mother while trying to adjust to a new school environment. Xiaoxin is interested in developing strategies to cope with these emotions and manage social interactions better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxin struggles with severe anxiety and feelings of loneliness but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxin feels a deep sense of emptiness and nervousness, primarily driven by missing their mother and feeling out of place at school.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes that Xiaoxin is overwhelmed by their emotions but shows a willingness to explore healthier coping mechanisms and challenge negative self-perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoxin recognize and challenge negative thoughts about fitting in at school. Focus on developing gradual steps for social interaction and healthier emotional expression.
