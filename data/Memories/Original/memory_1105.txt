Xiao Teng often uses gaming as a primary source of satisfaction and escape from realities such as school, which holds little interest.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Teng experiences symptoms related to behavioral addiction, particularly in relation to excessive gaming. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
