# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Jing (pseudonym) is a university student who has been experiencing feelings of loneliness and disconnection after transitioning from high school. Jing misses the familiarity and connections from high school and is finding it challenging to integrate into college life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Jing experiences symptoms of loneliness and adjustment difficulties but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jing feels down and lonely, expressing difficulties in connecting with others and missing the sense of belonging felt in high school.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a significant longing for past friendships and a struggle with self-doubt in new social situations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Jing identify and challenge negative thoughts related to new social interactions. Encourage taking manageable steps to engage with peers and explore shared interests to foster a sense of belonging.
