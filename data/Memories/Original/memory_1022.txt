[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu experiences symptoms of anxiety and occasional feelings of isolation but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Mental State]  
Liu feels overwhelmed and anxious due to ongoing tension with a roommate and a persistent feeling of isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Liu struggles with interpreting others' behaviors as signs of personal rejection but shows openness to considering alternative perspectives and solutions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu reassess thoughts regarding social interactions and promote open communication. Foster strategies for managing anxiety and enhancing social connections.
