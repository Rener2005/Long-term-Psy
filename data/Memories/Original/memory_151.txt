# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoya is a student who has recently lost her father. She struggles with grief and its impact on her focus and participation in daily life, particularly in academic settings. Xiaoya describes feeling lost and overwhelmed by her emotions while trying to maintain her responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoya exhibits symptoms of grief, sadness, and occasional overwhelming guilt related to the loss of her father. She has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder but is experiencing emotional distress typical of bereavement.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoya feels lost and overwhelmed by the recent loss of her father, experiencing difficulty focusing and dealing with persistent sadness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoya's deep sense of loss and self-blame over not being there more for her father during his final moments, coupled with hesitancy to burden her family with her emotions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoya process her grief, challenge self-blame, and develop strategies for managing her emotions. Encourage open communication with family to foster support.
