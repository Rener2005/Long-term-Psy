# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xi is a university student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, exacerbated by familial expectations and recent personal loss. Xi is committed to personal growth and actively seeks ways to manage emotions and academic pressure better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xi experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xi is overwhelmed and anxious, particularly about academic performance and familial expectations following the loss of a cousin.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xi's significant emotional burden due to family expectations and personal loss, yet observes a willingness to explore healthier cognitive patterns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help Xi recognize and challenge negative thinking patterns, encourage self-compassion, and develop strategies for academic and emotional resilience.
