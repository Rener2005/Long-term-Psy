# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang San is a professional managing significant responsibilities at work. He faces challenges with self-doubt and anxiety, particularly in high-pressure situations that involve leading projects and making presentations. Zhang seeks to develop coping strategies and improve his self-confidence.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms of anxiety linked to self-doubt and fear of failing in professional settings. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels anxious and overwhelmed, expressing significant self-doubt about his ability to manage work responsibilities effectively.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's persistent negative self-assessment despite evidence of competencies, but Zhang shows a willingness to practice self-compassion and explore reframing his thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge negative beliefs and recognize his capabilities. Develop mindfulness practices to manage anxiety and reinforce positive affirmations.
