# First Consultation

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A experiences anxiety related to academic performance and self-worth, without any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A feels down after recent exams, struggling with feelings of disappointment and insecurity due to a perceived inadequacy in meeting expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that A's high standards lead to pressure and self-criticism, but shows openness to exploring factors affecting academic performance and adopting new strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help A manage negative thoughts and improve sleep quality. Encourage A to recognize strengths and achievements, and develop strategies for stress management.
