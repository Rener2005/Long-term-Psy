# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Yu (pseudonym) is a young adult experiencing heightened anxiety related to cleanliness and social interactions. Recently, a specific incident involving a friend's presence in their personal space intensified these feelings, leading to obsessive cleanliness behaviors.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Yu exhibits symptoms of obsessive-compulsive tendencies related to cleanliness and social anxiety, though no formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yu feels overwhelmed and anxious, particularly about cleanliness, resulting in difficulty enjoying social interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Xiao Yu is struggling with intrusive thoughts about cleanliness, leading to avoidance and discomfort in social settings, but is open to exploring cognitive challenges to these beliefs.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Yu address obsessive thoughts, focus on alternative perspectives, and implement gradual exposure to feared situations with anxiety management strategies.
