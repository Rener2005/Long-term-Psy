# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Qianqian is a high school student navigating the challenges of academic pressure and familial expectations. She experiences anxiety around her academic performance and fears disappointing her father, who holds high expectations for her achievements.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Qianqian faces symptoms of anxiety, particularly related to performance and familial relationships. There has been no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Qianqian feels overwhelmed by school pressure and her father's disappointment, with persistent thoughts of inadequacy and failure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Qianqian internalizes academic struggles as personal failures, illustrating a cycle of anxious thoughts that hinder her confidence and concentration.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Qianqian identify and reframe negative thoughts about her performance and self-worth. Establish manageable coping strategies, like drawing and journaling, to improve emotional expression and academic focus.
