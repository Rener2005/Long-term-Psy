[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
M experiences social anxiety and hesitation in public speaking situations, marked by fears of judgment and inadequacy. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help M question and modify extreme social anxiety thoughts. Introduce exposure activities to build confidence in controlled social settings.
