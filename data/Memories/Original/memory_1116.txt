# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyun is a PhD student facing overwhelming pressure related to academic performance. She often feels isolated, anxious, and struggles with self-comparison to peers. Recently, Xiaoyun has been more introspective about her emotional responses and is motivated to understand and manage them effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyun exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, although no formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyun feels low and struggles to concentrate due to the overwhelming pressure from her PhD studies, leading to self-doubt and feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoyun's tendency to negatively compare herself to peers, which fuels her isolation and anxiety, but she is willing to work on self-understanding and develop supportive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyun recognize and challenge negative self-perceptions and comparisons. Develop strategies for managing academic pressure and improving focus.
