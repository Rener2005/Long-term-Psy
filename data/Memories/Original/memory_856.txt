# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Kong (pseudonym) is a university student dealing with anxiety and feelings of loneliness. They often reject social invitations, fearing they won't fit in, and this impacts both their social and academic life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Kong experiences anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly related to social interactions, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Kong feels anxious about social interactions and simultaneously lonely due to self-isolation from classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Kong's struggle with fear of rejection and a pattern of avoiding social situations, yet notices a willingness to explore change.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Kong challenge negative assumptions about social interactions, begin developing strategies to enhance social engagement and manage anxiety.
