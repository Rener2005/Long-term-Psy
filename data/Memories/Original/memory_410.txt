# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) works in a demanding environment experiencing anxiety and pressure due to recent team restructuring. He often feels a need to prove himself, struggles with self-doubt, and finds it hard to handle responsibilities without feeling overwhelmed.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences anxiety symptoms, primarily triggered by workplace challenges and high self-imposed standards, though no formal mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels overwhelmed and anxious, particularly about meeting expectations and being replaced at work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Zhang struggles with all-or-nothing thinking and heightened self-criticism but is open to exploring more balanced perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Zhang's negative thoughts and encourage a more balanced perspective about his workplace performance. Introduce relaxation strategies to alleviate stress and enhance focus.
