# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu Hui is a recent high school graduate who is experiencing self-esteem issues and anxiety, particularly after not performing up to expectations in college entrance exams. Liu Hui has a keen desire to improve self-acceptance and develop coping strategies for negative self-comparisons.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu Hui shows signs of anxiety and low self-esteem, although has not received a formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu Hui feels down and frustrated, struggling with self-confidence due to poor exam performance compared to classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notices Liu Hui's tendency to engage in negative self-comparison and self-critical thoughts, suggesting a need for enhanced self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral methods to challenge Liu Hui's negative thoughts and foster self-compassion through recognizing personal achievements and practicing kinder self-talk.
