# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is a young student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly in social situations and self-expression. Li describes dealing with pressure from familial expectations and is interested in building confidence and finding a sense of belonging in social contexts.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li shows symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, although no formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious and inadequate, particularly worrying about others' judgments and comparisons with peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li takes others' opinions, especially family, very seriously, impacting self-esteem, but Li is open to trying new perspectives and strategies for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li identify and reframe negative thoughts, while fostering self-compassion and encouraging involvement in social activities to build self-esteem.
