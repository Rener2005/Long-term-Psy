# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Li is an adult professional who reports feelings of being undervalued in the workplace, struggling with communication and managing emotions. Ms. Li is keen on developing strategies to enhance emotional regulation and improve workplace relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt related to workplace performance but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Li feels frustrated and undervalued at work, experiencing a cycle of negative self-perception and defensiveness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Ms. Li's tendency to equate criticism with self-worth inadequacy but notes her willingness to identify and challenge this cycle.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ms. Li recognize and challenge negative thoughts and reinforce positive self-evaluation. Develop strategies to manage feelings of being undervalued and improve communication dynamics with colleagues.

# Second Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
