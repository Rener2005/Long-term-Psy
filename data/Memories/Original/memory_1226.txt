# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
User G is a university student navigating the aftermath of a breakup, exhibiting feelings of sadness and loneliness. G is proactive in seeking emotional clarity and aims to reconnect with personal interests and social circles.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
User G faces symptoms associated with anxiety and feelings of worthlessness, particularly following personal relationship challenges. No formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
G feels overwhelmed and lost following the breakup, experiencing significant sadness and isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that G has deeply internalized feelings of inadequacy but is open to rediscovering personal strengths and support networks.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist G in challenging negative self-beliefs and fostering a sense of self-worth. Encourage exploring personal interests like art for emotional expression.
