# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaona is a student grappling with issues regarding self-image and social interactions, recently exacerbated by a physical conflict in an English class that heightened feelings of embarrassment and self-consciousness about her appearance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaona experiences self-esteem issues and social anxiety, stemming from a continuous internal narrative of inadequacy, especially regarding her physical appearance. No formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaona feels frustrated and isolated due to negative self-perception and the belief that her appearance prevents her from being liked or making friends.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaona engages in frequent negative self-comparisons but demonstrates a capacity for empathy and self-awareness, identifying her strengths when prompted.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Xiaona’s negative self-beliefs and enhance self-esteem by focusing on and sharing personal strengths, such as drawing, to foster peer connections.
