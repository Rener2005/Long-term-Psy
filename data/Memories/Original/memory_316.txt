# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a dedicated professional and family man feeling the pressure of balancing work commitments with family responsibilities. Recently, he has been grappling with guilt over not spending enough time with his family due to demanding project deadlines, leading to stress and exhaustion.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences anxiety related to work-family conflict, accompanied by feelings of guilt and inadequacy. He has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder but exhibits stress-related symptoms.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious and guilty about spending insufficient time with his family due to work commitments.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang Wei struggling with the pressure of fulfilling roles both at work and at home, but he shows readiness to explore setting boundaries to improve this balance.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to assist Zhang Wei in setting work boundaries and enhancing family interactions, aiming to mitigate anxiety and guilt through structured time management.
