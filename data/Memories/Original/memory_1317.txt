# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yang is a student who struggles with social anxiety and self-doubt, specifically regarding his interactions with classmates and his academic performance. He desires to build meaningful connections and overcome feelings of inadequacy.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yang experiences significant social anxiety and a lack of self-confidence, although there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder at this time.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yang feels left out and anxious when observing classmates interacting, resulting in negative self-comparisons and feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Yang's pervasive negative self-comparisons and anxious thoughts, but Yang demonstrates a willingness to explore these thoughts and consider more positive interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yang challenge his negative thoughts about not belonging and to encourage gradual social engagement. Develop strategies for reducing anxiety and fostering self-acceptance.
