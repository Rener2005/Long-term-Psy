# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu is a college student adjusting to university life and living in a dormitory environment. Liu expresses feelings of anxiety and loneliness, struggles with social integration, and worries about academic performance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu experiences symptoms of anxiety and a persistent sense of inadequacy but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu feels anxious and overwhelmed by the new university environment, which exacerbates feelings of loneliness and difficulty focusing on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Liu's tendency to engage in negative self-comparison and anticipatory anxiety about academic and social aspects but notes Liu's willingness to take proactive steps to integrate socially.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu challenge negative thoughts and encourage small, actionable steps for social engagement and managing academic stress.
