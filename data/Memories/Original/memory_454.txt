# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is an individual who has recently experienced homelessness, which has significantly impacted his mental health and outlook on life. He often struggles with feelings of isolation, hopelessness, and depression, amplified by the lack of a support network and a stable living environment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei currently battles with symptoms of depression and feelings of worthlessness. He has expressed suicidal thoughts but has not yet received a formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels deeply hopeless and isolated due to his homelessness, experiencing depression and occasional suicidal thoughts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang Wei is burdened by self-critical and defeatist thoughts, with an inclination towards believing he deserves his current hardships, yet he shows a desire to explore alternative approaches to his situation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei identify and challenge his negative thought patterns, fostering a more balanced and compassionate self-view. Encourage small, actionable steps to instill a sense of progress and control over his current circumstances.
