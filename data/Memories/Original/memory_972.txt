# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student experiencing issues with binge eating and negative body image, predominantly under academic stress. They mention pressure from parents to achieve high standards and seek to better manage these emotional challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits signs of emotional eating and discomfort with their body image but does not report a formal diagnosis of an eating disorder or other mental health conditions.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by academic pressure and resultant binge eating, leading to feelings of frustration and helplessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that the user is trapped in a cycle of self-criticism and emotional eating but shows readiness to explore and challenge these thought patterns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to identify triggers for binge eating and develop affirmations and alternative activities. Encourage the user to adopt more positive self-talk and self-compassion.
