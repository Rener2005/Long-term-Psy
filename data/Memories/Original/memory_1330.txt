# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaozhang is a university student who experiences significant anxiety related to classroom participation and fear of judgment. Xiaozhang has been dealing with feelings of embarrassment and self-consciousness, particularly following a negative experience in an English class.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaozhang exhibits symptoms of social anxiety, especially regarding public speaking and classroom participation, but has not shared any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaozhang feels overwhelmed and anxious about classroom participation, hindered by a persistent fear of embarrassment and judgment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaozhang struggles with negative self-comparisons and experiences heightened anxiety in academic settings but is open to exploring interventions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaozhang challenge negative thought patterns and build self-confidence. Implement strategies for managing anxiety during classroom interactions through deep breathing and gradual exposure.
