# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaolin (pseudonym) is grappling with feelings of emptiness and loss following a falling out with a close friend. The conflict has left Xiaolin questioning the strength and meaning of friendships, leading to feelings of discouragement and sadness.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaolin exhibits symptoms of depression and anxiety related to interpersonal relationships, though no formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaolin feels lost and overwhelmed by the end of a meaningful friendship, experiencing emptiness and regret.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes Xiaolin’s deep longing for connection and tendency to self-blame, yet observes an openness to exploring personal feelings and fault in the conflict.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaolin recognize and reframe self-critical thoughts, promote open communication, and develop a balanced view towards recovering lost relationships.
