# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Feng is an individual dealing with the challenges of balancing family responsibilities, particularly related to his father's illness, and academic pursuits. He is reflective, expressing guilt and anxiety over his perceived obligations, wanting to support his family financially and excel in his studies.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Feng exhibits symptoms of anxiety and guilt but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health condition.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Feng feels overwhelmed and conflicted between the need to help his family financially and the desire to continue his education.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Feng's deep sense of responsibility and guilt, yet appreciates his openness to discuss these feelings and consider alternatives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Feng explore and balance his thoughts on immediate financial needs with long-term educational benefits. Introduce stress management strategies like relaxation techniques to alleviate anxiety.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Feng continues to feel overwhelmed, experiencing guilt and anxiety about not fulfilling both academic and familial expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Feng's high self-expectations and desire to meet parental sacrifices, recognizing his openness to understanding and adjusting these expectations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Feng in developing realistic expectations and effective communication with his family. Introduce time management strategies to create balance between studies and family responsibilities.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Feng feels anxious and pressured due to concerns about his father's health and the possibility of dropping out of school to provide financial support.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Feng's fear of burdening his family and the weight of perceived financial pressures, highlighting his willingness to explore financial aid as an alternative solution.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Feng to pursue financial assistance options to maintain his education while managing familial financial needs. Reinforce stress management techniques like deep breathing to mitigate anxiety.
