# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu is a university student who is experiencing feelings of insecurity and difficulty integrating into the social environment at the university. Liu is concerned about fitting in and often feels judged by peers, which affects their confidence in social interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu experiences social anxiety and has expressed concerns about belonging and being accepted in a new environment but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu feels low and disconnected, contemplating dropping out due to feelings of inadequacy and the impression of being judged by classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Liu's considerable self-doubt but acknowledges Liu's awareness of the tendency to overthink social interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu identify and challenge negative assumptions about being judged, boosting self-esteem and encouraging active participation in social settings like study groups.

[Consultation Goals]
