# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyan is a college student experiencing significant anxiety related to social interactions, especially in academic settings. Recently, Xiaoyan has been struggling with the transition to college life and the pressure of increased social engagement.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyan exhibits signs of social anxiety, particularly in large groups and academic discussions, though no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyan feels overwhelmed and anxious about social interactions, particularly fearing judgment during group discussions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoyan's anxiety is significantly tied to negative self-perceptions and fear of embarrassment, yet there is a willingness to confront and challenge these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyan identify and challenge negative thoughts associated with social anxiety. Begin developing strategies to participate more comfortably in social settings.

# Second Consultation
