# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Susan is an adult female who has been navigating complex emotions stemming from past traumas and a persistent sense of isolation. She is reflective about her experiences and seeks ways to move forward despite feeling overwhelmed by these memories.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Susan demonstrates symptoms of depression and anxiety, particularly related to past trauma, but there has been no formal disclosure of a diagnosed mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Susan feels overwhelmed and hopeless, struggling with negative self-talk and doubts about her own worth.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Susan's deep entrenchment in negative thought cycles but also notes her recognition of support from friends and a desire to challenge self-doubt.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Susan in identifying and challenging automatic negative thoughts, fostering self-compassion and acknowledging her resilience.
