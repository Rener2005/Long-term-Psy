# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Xiao is a professional experiencing significant anxiety and pressure related to work and personal relationships, particularly due to recent changes at work and a strained relationship with their partner. Zhang feels overwhelmed by team reorganization, uncertain job responsibilities, and communication challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang exhibits symptoms of anxiety, self-doubt, and feelings of inadequacy but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels overwhelmed and anxious about job performance and relationship stability, fearing failure and social comparison.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's tendency towards self-criticism and the impact of negative self-comparisons, though Zhang demonstrates an openness to exploring supportive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge negative thought patterns and develop strategies for communication and managing work-related anxiety.
