# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Li is an adult who experiences significant anxiety, particularly regarding social interactions and going out alone. She is working on building confidence and reducing fear.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Li exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and agoraphobia but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Li feels anxious and overwhelmed by the idea of going out, fearing negative occurrences and judgment from others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Ms. Li's strong desire for improvement and her courage in taking steps toward addressing her social anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques, focusing on gradual exposure and restructuring negative thoughts, to help Ms. Li feel more comfortable and confident in social settings. Encourage small, manageable steps toward social engagement and self-compassion.
