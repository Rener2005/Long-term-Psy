# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a student who struggles with anxiety primarily related to academic performance and familial expectations. The user has recently experienced stress from classmates and communication challenges with parents, especially regarding school subjects.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, with no formal diagnosis disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by academic pressures and experiences a sense of isolation due to peer ridicule and parental communication barriers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a pattern of self-defeating thoughts and recognizes the user's vulnerability but notes willingness to consider alternative viewpoints.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help the user address negative self-talk and foster effective coping mechanisms for stress, particularly with academic concerns and communication with parents.
