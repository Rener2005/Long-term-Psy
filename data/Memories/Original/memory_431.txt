Xiao Hong desires meaningful connections with peers and seeks methods to improve social integration.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaohong experiences feelings of loneliness and occasional low self-esteem associated with her family situation. The discussions have not revealed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Consultation Goals]
