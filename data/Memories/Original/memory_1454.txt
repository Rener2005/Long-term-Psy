# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Zhang is a student facing challenges with self-doubt and anxiety, particularly concerning academic performance and peer interactions. Xiao Zhang is seeking strategies to increase focus and confidence in school settings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Zhang exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem without a formal diagnosis. However, there is a significant impact on academic performance and school attendance.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Zhang feels overwhelmed by academic setbacks and harbors strong fears of judgment from peers, leading to avoidance behaviors.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Zhang's struggle with anxiety-driven avoidance yet notes a willingness to engage in new approaches to improve.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Zhang focus on setting small, achievable goals to gain confidence in academic settings and develop positive self-reinforcement strategies.
