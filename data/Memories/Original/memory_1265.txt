# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Aqi (pseudonym) is a school-aged female who struggles with feelings of inadequacy primarily caused by family comparisons. She is striving to balance school responsibilities with familial expectations and is looking for ways to manage anxiety and loneliness, especially in relation to her sister's achievements.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Aqi experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, exacerbated by familial comparisons and responsibilities at home. She has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Aqi feels upset and anxious due to continuous comparisons with her sister, resulting in feelings of inadequacy and invisibility within her family.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Aqi internalizes her emotions, leading to withdrawal and loneliness, but shows a willingness to explore healthier ways to express and manage her feelings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Aqi articulate her needs and feelings to her family more effectively. Encourage exploration of supportive strategies like creative activities and positive social interactions.
