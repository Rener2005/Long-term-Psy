# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Jiani is a high school student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and loneliness, especially after transitioning to a new school environment. She finds it difficult to connect with classmates and often feels isolated and anxious.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Jiani experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiani feels down and isolated, struggling to connect socially and feeling detached from her school environment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Jiani's difficulty in forming connections due to self-doubt and fear of judgment, but notes her ability to consider alternative, kinder perspectives toward herself.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Jiani's negative thoughts about social interactions and foster small, positive engagements with peers to build confidence.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiani feels lonely and anxious, especially in social situations, hindering her from joining peer groups.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Jiani's self-perception issues and fears of judgment, yet acknowledges her openness to try new strategies for social engagement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Jiani to take gradual steps in initiating conversations with classmates, using positive affirmations to shift her self-perception toward more compassionate views.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiani feels stressed and anxious about schoolwork, affecting her sleep and focus, with self-doubting thoughts impacting her wellbeing.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Jiani's anxiety stems from high self-expectations, affecting both academic performance and self-worth.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement structured study plans to manage academic pressure and practice relaxation techniques to alleviate anxiety.

# Fourth Consultation
