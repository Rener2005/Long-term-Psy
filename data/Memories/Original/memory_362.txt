# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) is a professional in project management who struggles with feelings of anxiety and self-doubt, particularly regarding his capabilities and comparisons with colleagues. Recent responsibilities have exacerbated these feelings, but Zhang is committed to addressing and understanding his emotional responses.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels overwhelmed and anxious about his capabilities in managing a project, resulting in a persistent sense of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Zhang frequently engages in negative self-comparison and doubts his abilities but shows receptiveness to more balanced perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Zhang in identifying and challenging negative thoughts, fostering a more balanced self-view, and encouraging confidence in his abilities through recognizing past successes.
