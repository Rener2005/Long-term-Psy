# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is a university student navigating feelings of inadequacy following a breakup. Zhang is reflecting on the emotional pain from seeing a former partner move on, which impacts daily motivation and leads to self-doubt.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang exhibits symptoms of depression and anxiety related to relationship issues, with no mentioned formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels deeply hurt and isolated following a breakup, with pervasive thoughts of inadequacy affecting overall motivation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's persistent negative self-perception and hurt due to relational dynamics, yet notices a willingness to uncover self-worth beyond external validation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge feelings of inadequacy and develop self-compassion. Begin fostering techniques for rebuilding social connections to combat isolation.
