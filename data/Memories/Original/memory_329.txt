# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ming is a student who struggles with feelings of pressure from academic and parental expectations. Recently, he has been having conflicts with his mother and teachers due to these expectations and his beliefs about self-expression.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming experiences anxiety and frustration linked to perceived academic demands but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels frustrated and constrained by expectations at home and school, harboring a desire for self-expression.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Ming's resistance to external expectations and his challenge in expressing emotions without anger.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Ming identify and communicate his feelings calmly and constructively. Develop strategies to address perceived criticism and enhance self-expression.
