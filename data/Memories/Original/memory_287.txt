# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang is a high school student who feels frustrated with math, particularly due to communication issues with the math teacher. Wang previously enjoyed math but has become anxious and withdrawn in class due to these negative interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang experiences anxiety related to academic performance and interpersonal interactions with the math teacher, but there is no mention of a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels anxious and frustrated about attending math class, which once was a subject of interest and enjoyment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wang's anxiety about the teacher's behavior hinders academic engagement, yet Wang shows a willingness to explore strategies to rebuild confidence in math.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang regain confidence in understanding math, despite teacher interactions. Explore strategies for assertive communication and positive engagement with math concepts.
