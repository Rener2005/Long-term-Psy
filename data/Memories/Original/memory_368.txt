# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Josephine is a new mother dealing with significant changes in her personal life. She worries about ensuring a safe environment for her baby amidst challenges in her relationship with her partner. Josephine seeks to rebuild her confidence and maintain control over her life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Josephine shows signs of anxiety related to her parenting abilities and her partner's impact on her family life. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder mentioned.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Josephine is anxious, fearing the loss of custody of her baby and struggling with feelings of inadequacy as a mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Josephine's significant anxiety concerning her parenting and partner's behavior. However, she shows resilience and a willingness to implement changes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to help Josephine challenge negative thoughts and reinforce her strengths as a mother. Develop strategies to address her safety concerns and increase resilience.
