# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaomi (pseudonym) is an adolescent dealing with anxiety related to academic performance and familial expectations, particularly those stemming from their mother's high standards. Xiaomi is eager to understand these pressures better and improve communication with their mother.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaomi experiences anxiety manifesting as intense pressure concerning academic success and social situations, though there's no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaomi is feeling anxious and tense, primarily due to concerns about meeting academic expectations and their mother's potential disappointment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaomi's intense fear of failure and believes it stems from high familial expectations, yet notes a willingness to explore alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to help Xiaomi challenge extreme beliefs about academic performance, introduce grounding techniques to manage anxiety, and explore effective communication methods with their mother.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
Xiaomi is caught in a cycle of negative self-assessment, but starts to recognize the lack of substantial evidence underlying these fears, which opens them to explore alternative, supportive viewpoints.

[Consultation Goals]
