# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) recently lost his job and is now experiencing increased anxiety and confusion about his career path. His family's concern adds additional pressure, intensifying his feelings of inadequacy and uncertainty. He is actively seeking ways to manage these challenges and foster his self-esteem.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang is experiencing symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt triggered by his recent job loss and the ensuing search for new employment opportunities.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels anxious and overwhelmed due to job loss, leading to self-doubt and fears about his future career prospects.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices that Zhang is caught in a cycle of negative thinking regarding his skills and employment prospects, yet shows willingness to explore these beliefs and apply more positive self-assessments.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge his negative thoughts, recognize his professional strengths, and develop a realistic, step-by-step plan for his job search that incorporates effective communication with family members.
