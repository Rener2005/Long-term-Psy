# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
A (pseudonym) is a student who frequently experiences anxiety and family-related stress. A is particularly concerned about family arguments and their impact on personal well-being and academic performance. A expresses a desire to achieve emotional stability and improved interactions both at home and school.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A shows symptoms of anxiety, particularly related to family dynamics and social integration at school, without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A feels anxious and helpless due to family conflicts and the fear they invoke, impacting sleep and academic focus.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that A carries a significant burden of responsibility for resolving family conflicts, which contributes to feelings of helplessness and stress.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge A's automatic thoughts of responsibility and helplessness. Introduce relaxation strategies to manage anxiety and encourage self-care and open communication with peers and family.
