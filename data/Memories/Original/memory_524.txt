# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is a married woman who experiences anxiety related to her husband's social interactions, impacting her emotional well-being and sleep. She is reflective and seeks to understand and manage irrational fears and improve communication within her marriage.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li suffers from anxiety, primarily linked to trust issues and negative self-perceptions, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li is anxious and overwhelmed by fears of betrayal in her relationship, leading to restlessness and sleep difficulties.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Li's anxiety is driven by uncertainty and imagined scenarios, creating an internal conflict between trust and doubt, yet she shows openness to improving communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Integrate cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li manage intrusive thoughts and facilitate open communication with her husband. Build strategies for expressing emotions calmly without placing blame.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li continues to struggle with anxiety, predominantly related to her husband's social activities, leading to feelings of jealousy and insecurity.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a pattern of cognitive distortions, such as jumping to negative conclusions without evidence, but recognizes Li's openness to challenge these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li in identifying cognitive distortions and developing affirmations to reinforce trust. Encourage the use of "I" statements to improve communication and reduce misinterpretations.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels better about her relationship but is still troubled by lingering doubts and insecurities, particularly when her husband socializes.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li's progress in recognizing the lack of evidence supporting her fears and her willingness to adopt a new perspective on her husband's actions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Focus on fostering mindfulness practices to manage anxiety and encourage Li to view relationship dynamics through a lens of positivity and established trust.
