# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wu (pseudonym) is a professional struggling with anxiety and self-doubt, especially related to work performance and fear of failure. Wu values personal development and seeks to improve the ability to manage stress and emotional responses effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wu experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem related to work challenges, though there has been no disclosure of a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wu feels overwhelmed and anxious after losing an important project at work, leading to self-doubt and persistent thoughts of failure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Wu's acute focus on perceived failures and the impact on self-worth, but also observes an openness to exploring alternative perspectives and coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wu challenge negative thoughts and learn to reframe failures as learning experiences. Develop initial strategies for managing workplace stress and anxiety.
