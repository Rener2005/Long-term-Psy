# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is a college student who experiences anxiety and self-doubt, particularly about future job prospects and academic performance. Zhang has observed job market difficulties faced by peers and is involved in recent family responsibilities following a familial accident, adding to stress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang shows signs of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not received a formal diagnosis of a specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels anxious and overwhelmed by concerns about future job prospects, particularly in comparison with peers, affecting sleep and energy levels.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang’s tendency to engage in negative comparisons with peers, yet Zhang is open to exploring support and strategizing positive coping mechanisms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge negative self-comparisons and increase confidence through recognizing achievements and setting future-orientated goals.
