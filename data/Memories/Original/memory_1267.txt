# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Gina (pseudonym) is a young woman trying to cope with feelings of abandonment and familial changes, particularly involving her sister's recent marriage. Gina desires to understand and manage her emotional responses better while seeking meaningful connections with others.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Gina experiences symptoms of anxiety, frustration, and a sense of displacement but has not formally disclosed any diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Gina feels upset and overwhelmed by her sister's marriage, leading to feelings of abandonment and inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Gina’s struggle with expressing emotions about significant life changes and notes her tendency to feel misunderstood, yet she shows a willingness to explore her feelings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Gina acknowledge and express her feelings through cognitive behavioral techniques. Encourage her to practice self-compassion and begin identifying supportive relationships.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Gina continues to feel rejected and struggles with the notion of belonging in her family post her sister’s marriage.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Gina's harsh self-judgments but sees potential for transforming these thoughts into more compassionate perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Gina in identifying evidence against negative self-beliefs. Encourage her to communicate her feelings with her adopted family to foster a supportive environment.

# Third Consultation

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Gina's challenges in expressing emotions healthily and notes her progress in acknowledging positive relationships despite emotional turbulence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Gina to practice expressing her emotions in safe environments and utilize drawing as an outlet for anger.

# Fourth Consultation

# Sixth Consultation
