# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
A (pseudonym) is a university student who feels compelled to defend the honor of friends, often leading to conflicts. A is reflective about the impact of these actions on relationships and demonstrates a desire to find alternative ways to express strength.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A experiences struggles with impulse control and reactive aggression but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A is frustrated and angry, experiencing regret after impulsively defending friends in conflicts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes A's challenge with impulsive reactions driven by a desire to portray strength, while also noting A’s awareness of the negative impact on relationships.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help A develop self-awareness and manage impulsive responses during conflicts, fostering reflection and alternative conflict resolution strategies.
