# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a student who has been feeling inadequate and anxious, particularly following a recently failed bid for class president. Li is reflective and eager to improve communication and social connections, while struggling with feelings of isolation and disappointment in academic and team settings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels lost and confused after failing to be elected as class president, leading to feelings of inadequacy and concern about social standing.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li's tendency to equate setbacks with personal failure, but also recognizes a willingness to use communication as a tool to regain connections.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li confront feelings of failure and improve self-perception. Start developing strategies for positive communication and reconnecting with peers.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li is experiencing loneliness and anxiety due to perceived social exclusion, leading to avoidance of social activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li’s negative self-beliefs are impacting social interactions, but there's an openness to challenge those beliefs and reconnect socially.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li in identifying and re-evaluating distorted thoughts regarding social exclusion. Encourage gradual social engagement starting with initiating simple conversations.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed and unworthy after receiving poor mid-term exam results, affecting motivation to study.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li’s anxiety during study-related activities, yet acknowledges readiness to develop healthier study habits and paradigms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Li to create realistic study goals and counter negative thoughts with factual evidence. Integrate positive reinforcement strategies to manage academic stress.

# Fourth Consultation

# Fifth Consultation
