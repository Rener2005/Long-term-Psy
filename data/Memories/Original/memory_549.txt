# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Qin (pseudonym) is a recent participant in a detox program who is experiencing anxiety and uncertainty about re-integrating into society. He expresses concerns about finding employment and fears the potential for relapse, reflecting feelings of failure and the pressure of disappointing his family.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Qin exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Qin feels overwhelmed and anxious about re-entering society, fearing unemployment and potential relapse, which contributes to feelings of failure and hopelessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Qin is struggling with self-doubt and a fear of disappointing significant others, but he is open to exploring small, manageable steps for change.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Qin identify and challenge negative thoughts, set small achievable goals for reintegration, and reinforce positive self-affirmations.
