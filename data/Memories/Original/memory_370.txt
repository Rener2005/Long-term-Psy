# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming (pseudonym) is a student experiencing feelings of loneliness and anxiety, primarily due to strained friendships and parental conflicts. Li Ming expresses a struggle with motivation and focus on schoolwork due to these personal issues and desires to explore ways to better manage these emotions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming exhibits signs consistent with anxiety and low mood, notably in response to familial tension and social isolation, but there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels overwhelmed by loneliness and anxiety, leading to withdrawal from social interactions and academic challenges.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices Li Ming's longing for past connection and the burden of self-critical thoughts, yet Li Ming demonstrates a readiness to question the validity of these perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming challenge negative assumptions about peer relationships and family dynamics. Foster strategies for re-establishing social connections and addressing stressors in the home environment.
