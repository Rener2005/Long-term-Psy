# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wei is a university student dealing with anxiety, particularly in academic settings. They feel intense pressure to meet familial expectations, particularly from their father, which amplifies their self-doubt and fear of judgment from teachers and classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wei experiences symptoms of anxiety, especially linked to self-evaluation and academic performance, though no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wei feels anxious during classes, particularly when they perceive being watched by strict teachers, leading to concentration difficulties and panic symptoms.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wei struggles significantly under perceived judgment, yet illustrates a willingness to explore cognitive restructuring and coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce CBT techniques focusing on identifying and challenging negative thoughts. Encourage strategies for handling anxiety, such as reframing perceptions of judgment and practicing grounding exercises.
