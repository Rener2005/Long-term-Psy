# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Mandy (pseudonym) is a teacher and mother who struggles with perfectionism and self-doubt. She feels pressure to maintain flawless standards both at work and in family settings. Recently, she experienced anxiety and shame over perceived shortcomings during her daughter’s birthday party.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Mandy exhibits symptoms of anxiety and perfectionism. She has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Mandy feels anxious and ashamed about forgetting party favors at her daughter's birthday, questioning her capabilities as a mother and host.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Mandy's harsh self-criticism but observes her capacity for more compassionate perspectives when reframing her thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Mandy reframe negative self-assessments and cultivate self-forgiveness. Encourage practices that foster self-compassion and emotional resilience.
