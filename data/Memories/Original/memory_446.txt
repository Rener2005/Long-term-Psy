# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is navigating a transitional period after a recent breakup, contributing to her anxiety. She is a young adult in an educational setting where academic performance and future planning are significant concerns.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei faces challenges with anxiety related to self-worth and social performance, experiencing cycles of self-doubt. No formal diagnosis of a severe mental health disorder has been mentioned.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious about her abilities and uncertain about her future, especially following her recent breakup.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang Wei's patterns of negative self-comparison and avoidance behavior, with an evident desire to improve.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei identify and challenge negative thoughts and enhance self-compassion. Establish small, manageable goals to build social confidence.
