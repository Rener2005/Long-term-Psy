# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang (pseudonym) is a young individual who recently experienced a difficult breakup, which has significantly impacted emotional well-being, concentration, and sleep patterns. Wang is a reflective person who is actively seeking ways to improve self-esteem and cope with anxiety post-breakup.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-worth associated with recent relationship challenges. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels low, frustrated, and unable to focus on studies, troubled by beliefs about self-worth after a breakup.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Wang's recurring self-doubt and anxiety, but notes a willingness to explore the origins of these negative thoughts and replace them with healthier perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang identify and challenge cognitive distortions related to self-worth, and develop healthier sleep and study routines as part of the coping strategy.
