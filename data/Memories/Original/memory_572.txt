# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Kirti is a student struggling with feelings of frustration and misunderstanding in her school environment. She finds it difficult to connect with classmates and teachers, leading to feelings of anger and isolation. Kirti is eager to find ways to express her emotions more effectively and foster better understanding with her peers.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Kirti experiences feelings of anger and frustration, primarily stemming from challenging interactions at school. While these emotions are significant, there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Kirti feels misunderstood and frustrated at school, where she often perceives her efforts as unacknowledged by classmates and teachers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Kirti is stuck in a cycle of anger and misunderstanding but shows insight into her feelings and a willingness to improve communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Kirti explore alternative interpretations of her social interactions. Introduce emotion regulation strategies like deep breathing to manage anger more effectively.
