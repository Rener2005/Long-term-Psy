# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Z (pseudonym) is a university student dealing with challenges related to excessive gaming, academic performance, and the resulting feelings of frustration and inadequacy. Z is aware of the impact of these behaviors and is actively seeking to regain control over their academic and personal life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Z experiences symptoms of anxiety and low motivation tied to academic challenges and excessive gaming but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Z feels lost and frustrated due to failing courses and excessive gaming, which they use as an escape from their academic struggles.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Z is caught in a cycle of negative beliefs about their academic abilities, yet demonstrates a willingness to explore and reconnect with past interests.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Z recognize and challenge negative thoughts about studying, fostering interest in academic pursuits. Establish structured boundaries to manage gaming and encourage engagement in alternative activities like programming and basketball.
