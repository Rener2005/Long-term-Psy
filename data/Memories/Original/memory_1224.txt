# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Qiang is a college student experiencing challenges in adapting to university life. He finds himself struggling with feelings of inadequacy and pressure related to academic performance, coupled with a sense of isolation from the structured environment he experienced in high school.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Qiang is experiencing symptoms of anxiety and low self-efficacy but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Qiang feels overwhelmed and lost, as he struggles with declining academic performance and the pressure to meet parental expectations. 

[Observational Impressions]  
The counselor observes that Xiao Qiang experiences high levels of stress and a lack of self-confidence, but shows a willingness to explore positive changes and new strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Qiang challenge negative thought patterns and develop a more structured approach to managing academic stress. Encourage positive reinforcement by recognizing past successes.
