# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is a professional in the workforce who experiences anxiety related to job performance and workplace interactions. Recent team evaluations and work stress have magnified feelings of inadequacy, which Li Ming is eager to address and overcome.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming displays symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, particularly concerning job performance and peer relationships. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels low and anxious after a poor team project evaluation, doubting personal abilities and fearing negative judgments from colleagues.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming struggles with harsh self-critique and negative self-perceptions, yet demonstrates a willingness to explore more compassionate self-assessments.

[Consultation Goals]  
Focus on applying cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming reframe negative thoughts and cultivate self-kindness. Establish small, achievable goals to rebuild confidence in professional settings.
