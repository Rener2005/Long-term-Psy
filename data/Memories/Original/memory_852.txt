# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a retired individual who was previously active in managing a school. Since retirement, Li reports feeling a loss of purpose and boredom, struggling with adjusting to a less structured lifestyle.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and depressive moods primarily related to the transition into retirement. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels down and purposeless due to retirement, experiencing anxiety and sleep difficulties.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li's vulnerability over feeling disconnected from family, coupled with an underlying desire to regain a sense of purpose and connection.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li challenge negative beliefs about self-worth and explore engaging activities. Encourage Li to enhance family communication and rekindle old hobbies for increased fulfillment.
