# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zheng (pseudonym) is navigating school pressures while finding refuge in gaming. Despite external expectations, Zheng expresses a desire to balance personal desires with academic responsibilities. Recent participation in a school gaming competition exacerbated feelings of pressure and escapism.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zheng displays signs of escapism into gaming to avoid academic pressure and familial conflict. No official mental health diagnosis has been reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zheng feels overwhelmed by the pressure from school and family and uses gaming as a form of escapism.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zheng’s struggle with communication, especially with family, and a tendency to retreat into games when feeling misunderstood or judged.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Zheng express feelings constructively and find balance between gaming and school responsibilities. Encourage open dialogue with family to improve mutual understanding.
