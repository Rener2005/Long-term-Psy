# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaowu is a student dealing with intense feelings of loss and hopelessness following an academic misconduct incident. The user experiences significant anxiety related to academic performance and parental expectations, particularly with a focus on being seen as a failure.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaowu exhibits signs of intense self-blame, anxiety, and low self-worth, especially influenced by parental anger and feelings of disappointment.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaowu feels lost and hopeless, struggling with self-blame and the perception of shattered dreams after being caught cheating.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaowu's intense self-criticism and emotional burden, but also notes an emerging recognition of positive qualities and achievements.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaowu challenge the belief of worthlessness and develop a more balanced self-view. Establish small, achievable goals to rebuild confidence, especially in academics.
