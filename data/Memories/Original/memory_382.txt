# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Zhang is a mother and wife who experiences significant fluctuations in her emotional state, especially after discontinuing lithium medication. She grapples with feelings of inadequacy regarding her household responsibilities and worries about being a good mother and wife.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Zhang has not disclosed any formal diagnosis but describes experiencing anxiety and self-doubt tied to perceived failures in fulfilling her roles.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Zhang feels anxious and overwhelmed, struggling to manage household tasks and grappling with guilt about her perceived inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Ms. Zhang's self-critical nature increases her feelings of guilt and anxiety, though she demonstrates a willingness to implement small, manageable changes and explore coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral strategies to help Ms. Zhang set realistic expectations and recognize her accomplishments. Encourage her to practice small tasks with mindful awareness to better manage anxiety.
