# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Gao (pseudonym) is a university student who experiences feelings of inadequacy and struggles with establishing genuine connections with peers. Gao has recently been feeling under pressure both from academic demands and familial concerns.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Gao demonstrates symptoms of anxiety, low self-esteem, and social reservation, without a specific mental health diagnosis having been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Gao feels overwhelmed and stuck in a cycle of nocturnal behaviors that provide a false sense of connection but ultimately lead to feelings of regret.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Gao battles with self-doubt and isolation but has shown a willingness to explore these issues and practice different social strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Gao challenge negative beliefs about social interactions and practice initiating positive connections, building self-confidence progressively.
