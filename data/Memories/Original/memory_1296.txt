# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Zhang is a veteran dealing with challenging memories from military service. These memories heavily impact daily life, causing significant anxiety and avoidance behavior. Zhang is trying to adjust to civilian life and seeks strategies to improve mental health and quality of life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Zhang exhibits symptoms consistent with post-traumatic stress, characterized by anxiety and avoidance behaviors, without a formal mental health disorder diagnosis yet being disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Zhang feels overwhelmed by persistent memories of military service, leading to anxiety and avoidance of certain triggers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Zhang experiences intense reactions to triggers that connect sounds with danger, indicating entrenched fear responses.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help Xiao Zhang challenge irrational fears and enhance coping strategies by practicing grounding techniques and gradual exposure to anxiety-inducing stimuli.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Zhang continues to face anxiety, especially in social settings, exacerbated by fear of being negatively judged by others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that social anxiety is intensified by thoughts of embarrassment, affecting Zhang's ability to engage in social situations confidently.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage recognition of negative thought patterns and practice relaxation techniques, like deep breathing, to manage social anxiety, combined with gradual exposure to social settings.
