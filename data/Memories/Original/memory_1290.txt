[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming experiences symptoms of anxiety but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorder. He is dealing with performance-related stress and anticipates negative judgment from peers.

[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels anxious and overwhelmed, particularly about an impending work presentation, which is affecting his concentration and sleep.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Ming experiences anticipatory anxiety, manifesting in repetitive negative thoughts about performance and perceived judgment from others.

[Consultation Goals]
