Xiaoyu exhibits signs of social anxiety and feelings of isolation, particularly in response to parental criticism and peer interactions, but has not been disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive-behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyu express emotions constructively and develop strategies to engage in open conversations with parents without escalating conflicts.
