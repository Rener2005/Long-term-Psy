# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoz (pseudonym) is a high school student feeling overwhelmed and anxious about upcoming college entrance exams. He experiences intense pressure to perform academically and feels conflicted about family dynamics, particularly with his stepmother.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoz experiences symptoms of anxiety related to academic performance and familial tension but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoz feels anxious and overwhelmed by the pressure of upcoming exams and conflicts with his stepmother, leading to difficulty focusing on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoz is caught in a cycle of high self-expectation and comparison with peers, yet shows openness to exploring supportive and practical strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoz challenge catastrophic thoughts regarding exam performance and improve focus. Encourage communication strategies to address family tensions constructively.
