# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, who is female, is navigating a challenging phase in her relationship. She has recently experienced a significant conflict with her boyfriend that marked a crucial emotional breaking point for her, characterized by feelings of betrayal and confusion.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user displays symptoms of emotional distress primarily stemming from relationship difficulties and could benefit from enhancing communication skills, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels confused and angry after a recent conflict with her boyfriend, expressing a sense of betrayal and shaken confidence in the relationship.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's difficulty with articulating emotions and fears surrounding potential future conflicts, yet notes her openness to exploring communication strategies for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to equip the user with effective communication strategies, focusing on expressing emotions using "I" statements to minimize conflict and enhance mutual understanding.
