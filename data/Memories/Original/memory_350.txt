# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is an IT professional struggling with anxiety and uncertainty about career expectations and transitions. Recently, he has started a demanding new job and is concerned about performance and adaptation.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences anxiety and self-doubt, primarily in professional settings, but has not been diagnosed with any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious and overwhelmed with the expectations of his new job, and often questions his adequacy in meeting these demands.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang Wei's tendency towards negative self-comparison and persistent self-doubt, but also sees his capacity to appreciate his own achievements when prompted.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei focus on personal progress instead of comparisons. Encourage the use of journaling to acknowledge daily achievements and manage anxiety through task management strategies.
