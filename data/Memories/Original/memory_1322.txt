# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Lily (pseudonym) is a young student experiencing anxiety and emotional turmoil due to increased familial conflicts at home. She struggles with feelings of loneliness and seeks better connections with peers at school, while also dealing with a sense of displacement when observing social interactions among classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lily exhibits symptoms of anxiety and social isolation but has not been formally diagnosed with a specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lily feels anxious and overwhelmed due to familial tensions and experiences loneliness and difficulty connecting with peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Lily is hesitant about opening up due to fear of being misunderstood, but she shows a willingness to explore ways to express herself and connect with others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Lily identify and challenge anxious thoughts, encouraging gradual social engagement and self-expression. Explore strategies to manage academic and emotional stress.
