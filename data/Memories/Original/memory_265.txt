# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Lin is a diligent student facing anxiety and self-doubt, particularly related to academic performance and the expectations from his role as a class representative. Recently, he has experienced pressure from his math exam results, affecting his confidence.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Lin experiences symptoms of academic anxiety and self-doubt, yet has no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lin is anxious and disappointed about his math performance, feeling added pressure from the expectations of others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Lin's strong self-expectation and tendency towards self-criticism, but also observes his potential to adopt alternative perspectives and find constructive solutions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to guide Xiao Lin in reframing negative thoughts about his abilities and encourage realistic, small achievable goals in his academic pursuits.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lin is persistently anxious about his studies, expressing frustration and self-doubt despite increased efforts through additional tutoring.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Lin's anxiety is deeply entwined with negative belief patterns about his abilities, yet he shows openness to recognizing and celebrating small successes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiao Lin in shifting focus from ultimate outcomes to incremental progress using cognitive strategies, and encourage the use of stress-reduction techniques like breathing exercises to manage exam-related anxiety.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lin is overwhelmed by exam pressure, fearing disappointment in himself and from his parents, which exacerbates his feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Lin's anxiety is partly due to external expectations, but acknowledges his readiness to adopt exam preparation techniques that address anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Xiao Lin establish a routine incorporating relaxation techniques and realistic study goals, reinforcing his understanding of material rather than anxiety-driven performance.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lin feels a tentative boost in confidence from improved exam results, yet remains cautious about maintaining progress.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Lin's growing sense of accomplishment and pride from organized study habits and acknowledges his awareness of the need to continuously celebrate small achievements.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Xiao Lin to build on his newfound study strategies, facilitating the maintenance of success through collaborative learning, consistent goal setting, and social reinforcement.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lin feels more supported and motivated after engaging with classmates in study groups, yet still struggles occasionally with procrastination and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist recognizes Xiao Lin's enhanced confidence from social interaction and the validation of shared struggles with peers, highlighting his openness to both give and receive support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Reinforce Xiao Lin's commitment to active participation in study groups while addressing procrastination by combating anxiety-driven thoughts and maintaining a balanced self-perception.

# Sixth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lin has developed a more positive attitude towards his studies and feels a sense of accomplishment, though he occasionally battles thoughts of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Lin's progress in adopting healthier thought patterns and study habits, noting a focus on achievement over perfection and a willingness to challenge negative self-comparisons.

[Consultation Goals]  
Solidify Xiao Lin's positive mindset and study habits by incorporating daily reflective practices, encouraging self-celebration, and continuing to address negative comparisons with affirmations and structured journaling.
