# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming (pseudonym) recently lost a job, leading to feelings of diminished self-worth and uncertainty about future employment prospects. He is grappling with self-doubt and seeks to regain his confidence and find strategies to move forward in his career.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming displays signs of low self-esteem and anxiety related to job loss and unemployment. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder, but the situation has significantly impacted his emotional well-being.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels low and uncertain about his self-worth after losing his job and struggles with feelings of isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming is experiencing self-doubt and isolation but demonstrates a willingness to explore his thoughts and challenge negative perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Li Ming in identifying his strengths and developing actionable steps for his job search. Encourage a shift in mindset toward a more supportive self-perception.
