# First Consultation

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Mickey displays symptoms of emotional dysregulation and social anxiety but has not been formally diagnosed with a psychological disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Mickey feels angry and sad, experiencing emotional outbursts that lead to social isolation from peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Mickey's struggle with controlling emotional reactions and recognizes potential for growth through self-awareness and coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Mickey identify emotional triggers and develop calming strategies like deep breathing and taking breaks to manage anger and anxiety.
