# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student experiencing stress and anxiety primarily stemming from interpersonal conflicts with roommates and academic pressures. The student has been feeling overwhelmed and guilty following a recent argument and is reflective about the impact of this on self-perception and relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety related to interpersonal relations and academic performance, though no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and anxious due to a recent altercation with roommates, accompanied by guilt and worry about academic responsibilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user tends to internalize conflicts, resulting in heightened anxiety and perceptions of self-blame but notes a willingness to explore self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist the user using CBT techniques to challenge harsh self-judgments and reframe thoughts around the incident. Develop strategies for resolving the conflict with roommates and managing academic stress.
