# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xu (pseudonym) is a student who recently went through a breakup, leading to feelings of confusion and isolation. Xu is navigating social dynamics at school and is concerned about peer perceptions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xu experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, but there is no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xu feels lost and confused following a recent breakup, resulting in increased overthinking and isolation from peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xu's tendency to internalize perceived judgments, creating a self-critical mindset, while Xu shows potential in understanding and altering these narratives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Xu to foster self-compassion and challenge negative self-perceptions using cognitive behavioral techniques. Begin developing strategies to engage with peers more openly.
