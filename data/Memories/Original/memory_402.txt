# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is an individual adjusting to a new job and work environment, feeling anxious about fitting in and connecting with new colleagues. Li expresses self-doubt about capabilities and fears regarding failures in the workplace.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li is experiencing anxiety related to work transitions, self-esteem issues, and a tendency towards self-critical thoughts but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li is feeling anxious and overwhelmed by the new work environment, with a persistent sense of self-doubt and insecurity.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li exhibits cycles of self-doubt and negative thinking but shows a willingness to explore strengths and achievements as a counterbalance.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li challenge negative thoughts and focus on strengths. Encourage strategies for managing work-related anxiety and increasing confidence in social interactions.
