# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Hua Hua is a student who has been struggling with anger management and emotional responses. Recently, he had a conflict in class that left him feeling guilty about his reactions, and he is looking for ways to communicate more effectively without becoming overwhelmed.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Hua Hua experiences symptoms of anger and impulsive reactions, particularly in situations where he feels his opinions are devalued. There is no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Hua Hua feels overwhelmed by quick anger, especially in interactions with classmates, leading to feelings of frustration and regret.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Hua Hua's difficulty in managing sudden emotional reactions, noticing the internal conflict between wanting to be heard and avoiding confrontation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies such as deep breathing to create pauses between feeling and reacting, helping Hua Hua manage anger and improve communication.
