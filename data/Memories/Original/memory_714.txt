# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaomei (pseudonym) is a university student who experiences feelings of inadequacy and isolation, particularly in response to academic challenges and familial interactions. Xiaomei desires increased parental support and seeks to build better connections socially and with classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaomei shows symptoms of low self-esteem and situational anxiety exacerbated by familial detachment and academic pressures but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaomei feels low and isolated, struggling with self-worth after academic setbacks and perceiving a lack of parental attention and support.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Xiaomei's tendency towards negative self-perceptions and social withdrawal but notes an openness to discussing and identifying these issues.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to help Xiaomei challenge negative thought patterns and foster strategies for building social connections and managing academic pressures.
