[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
C experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, particularly connected to uncertainty about future plans and self-comparison.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes C's conflict between personal interests and societal expectations, yet notes a willingness to explore personal aspirations and practical strategies for decision-making.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help C explore personal values and strengths, while developing strategies to manage academic and career-related anxiety. Support C in fostering a unique perspective on their individual journey.
