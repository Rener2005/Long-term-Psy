# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a dedicated professional who experiences stress and anxiety due to work-related pressures, particularly concerning project delays and team dynamics. Zhang Wei is eager to improve focus, manage stress effectively, and develop healthier work-life balance strategies.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei exhibits symptoms of work-related anxiety and stress but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis related to mental health disorders.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels overwhelmed by work stress, experiencing difficulty concentrating and intrusive thoughts about job performance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist identifies Zhang Wei's struggle with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety about potential disappointment but notes Zhang Wei's openness to exploring new coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques, such as reframing negative thoughts and developing stress management strategies. Encourage mindfulness practices to reduce anxiety and promote emotional balance.
