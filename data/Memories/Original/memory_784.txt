Li experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li is experiencing strong negative thoughts about self-worth, particularly regarding social acceptance and connection but is open to exploring alternative perspectives and expressing emotions more constructively.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li identify and challenge negative thoughts about self-worth. Develop communication strategies to express feelings of exclusion and foster new social connections.
