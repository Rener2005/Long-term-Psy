# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ning is a high school student who struggles with anxiety related to traumatic experiences witnessed at school and academic pressures. She has been trying to cope with intrusive memories and feelings of inadequacy related to these events.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ning exhibits symptoms of anxiety and possible post-traumatic stress, though no formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ning feels overwhelmed and anxious due to intrusive memories of traumatic incidents and concerns about her academic performance, leading to insomnia and negative self-evaluation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Ning is caught in a cycle of guilt and helplessness related to past events yet is open to exploring her feelings and thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help Xiao Ning challenge her negative thoughts and create a structured plan for managing her anxiety, particularly regarding her study habits and intrusive memories.

# Second Consultation

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Ning's struggle with negative anticipation of failure, compounded by past experiences, yet observes readiness to explore new strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiao Ning through CBT techniques to break down overwhelming study tasks into manageable parts and foster a compassionate self-narrative to counteract anxiety.

# Third Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
