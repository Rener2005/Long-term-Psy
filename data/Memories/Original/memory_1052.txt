# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a university student, feels challenged by an upcoming internship and has health concerns that contribute to feelings of inadequacy and anxiety. Reflective about past experiences, the user is eager to manage emotional responses more effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety, self-doubt, and possible low self-esteem. There is no mention of a formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and pressured due to the upcoming internship, experiencing anxiety tied to perceived inadequacies and comparisons with peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's cycle of self-doubt and pressure to perform, noting their willingness to explore alternative, positive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help the user recognize and challenge negative thought patterns. Establish a practice of acknowledging successes and incorporating stress-relieving activities like drawing.
