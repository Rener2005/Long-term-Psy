Li shows symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not been formally diagnosed with any specific mental health disorder.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li recognize positive interactions and challenge negative thoughts. Establish a strategy for managing social anxiety and improving communication skills.
