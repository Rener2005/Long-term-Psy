# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is a young individual grappling with feelings of loneliness and emotional distress, particularly following a recent breakup. Currently, they report difficulties with sleep and appetite, yet express a desire to nurture self-compassion and reconnect with enjoyable activities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms indicative of depression and anxiety following the breakup, though there has been no formal diagnosis disclosed of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang is experiencing pervasive loneliness and low self-worth after a breakup, indicating a decline in mood and emotional resilience.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang's negative thought patterns and feelings of abandonment, yet observes a willingness to recognize personal strengths and seek ways to shift perception.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge negative self-views and cultivate self-compassion. Introduce mindfulness and activities like painting for emotional regulation and expression.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
