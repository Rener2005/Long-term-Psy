# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Wang is a university student struggling with anxiety and the pressure of academic performance, particularly worried about judgment from peers and family, which affects confidence in education and social settings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Wang experiences anxiety symptoms, particularly regarding academic and social settings, without a disclosed formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Wang feels anxious and overwhelmed by academic expectations, experiencing physical symptoms of anxiety such as palpitations and nervousness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Wang ties self-worth to academic success, leading to anxiety, but shows a willingness to challenge these perceptions and discover new coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Wang challenge the belief that academic performance defines self-worth. Introduce relaxation techniques to manage anxiety symptoms effectively.
