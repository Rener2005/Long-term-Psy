# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is actively searching for a stable job and has been feeling anxious and helpless due to the challenges in securing employment post-pandemic. The user frequently compares themselves to peers, exacerbating feelings of inadequacy and uncertainty about the future.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly related to career insecurity and social comparison, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by the job search process, experiencing significant anxiety and a persistent sense of inadequacy compared to peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user struggles with negative self-comparisons and feelings of hopelessness in the face of career challenges, yet demonstrates a willingness to explore more balanced perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge negative beliefs and foster more balanced self-perceptions. Develop strategies to manage job search anxiety and establish a structured job application routine.
