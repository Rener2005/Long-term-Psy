# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao L (pseudonym) is a young individual struggling with feelings of worthlessness and inadequacy, mainly influenced by personal and professional comparisons. They have recently been hospitalized, which provided a structured environment they've found supportive. Xiao L is open to various therapeutic interventions to manage these feelings better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao L experiences symptoms consistent with low self-esteem and anxiety but has not disclosed a formal diagnosis related to their mental health.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao L feels overwhelmed by negative self-perceptions and isolated due to perceived failures and inadequate social progression.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices that Xiao L is trapped in a cycle of negative self-comparisons and doubts but is willing to consider new methods to reframe their current worldview.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to assist Xiao L in identifying and challenging negative thought patterns and developing a compassionate self-view. Encourage Xiao L to recognize personal growth and small achievements.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao L feels slightly improved due to familial support but continues to struggle with persistent feelings of worthlessness and being stuck.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that, although Xiao L values support from family, negative self-comparisons occur frequently, especially against peers, but shows glimpses of insight regarding the support network available.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiao L in recognizing the skewed nature of their negative thoughts, focusing on fact-based counterarguments. Encourage resilience through mindfulness and support from trusted individuals for emotional regulation.

# Third Consultation

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Reinforce Xiao L’s strengths by identifying positive feedback and achievements as credible alternatives to negative self-talk. Encourage practicing techniques like deep breathing for managing stress and anxiety.
