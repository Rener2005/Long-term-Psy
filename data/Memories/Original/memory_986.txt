# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Jiang is a high school student experiencing pressure and anxiety over academic performance and familial expectations. Recently, Xiao Jiang has been facing overwhelming stress from impending exams and fears of disappointing parents. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Jiang shows symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly related to academic performance. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Jiang feels overwhelmed and trapped by academic expectations and fears of parental disappointment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Jiang's strong link between self-worth and academic performance, coupled with an avoidance of discussing emotional needs with family.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to challenge the belief that self-worth is tied solely to academic success. Encourage communication skills to express feelings with parents and develop a healthier perspective on personal achievements.
