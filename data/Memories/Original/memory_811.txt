# First Consultation

Recently, failing to join an English experimental class and student council has heightened Xiaoyang's self-doubt, leading to increased concern about social perception and academic abilities. Xiaoyang is starting to explore new academic interests to better manage emotions and social connections.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyang experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyang feels anxious and embarrassed about not meeting expectations in an English class, leading to hesitancy in social interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoyang experiences pressure and self-doubt, struggling with negative thoughts about personal worth but shows willingness to challenge these perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyang recognize and challenge negative self-beliefs about academic performance and social interactions. Encourage engagement in small social interactions to build confidence and lessen anxiety.
