# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaolin is a class representative who faces feelings of isolation and misunderstanding from peers despite her efforts to connect. Xiaolin is reflective and actively seeks strategies to improve interpersonal relationships and manage responsibilities effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaolin experiences feelings of inadequacy and anxiety stemming from social interactions, but she has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaolin feels upset and isolated due to perceived misunderstandings from classmates regarding her resignation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaolin’s strong investment in her responsibilities, coupled with a fear of misunderstanding, indicating a need for clear communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaolin articulate her feelings and intentions effectively. Encourage the development of individual conversations to facilitate understanding and reduce feelings of isolation.

# Second Consultation
