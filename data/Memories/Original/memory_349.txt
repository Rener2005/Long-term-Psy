Li Ming is keen on building confidence and improving communication skills to enhance team dynamics and professional relationships. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming experiences symptoms associated with anxiety, particularly tied to feelings of inadequacy and fears of judgment, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels anxious and lacks confidence, particularly concerned about perceived judgments from team members and doubts about work contributions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes that Li Ming is highly affected by negative self-comparisons and team dynamics but shows openness to self-improvement strategies and skill-building for better team interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming recognize and challenge negative thought patterns. Encourage the development of strategies for confidence-building, such as maintaining records of positive contributions and trying mindfulness exercises to manage anxiety.
