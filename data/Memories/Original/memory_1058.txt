# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
A (pseudonym) is a student who experiences anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and the expectations from family, especially her mother. A is determined to manage and overcome these feelings to succeed in her studies.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A exhibits symptoms of anxiety related to academic pressures but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A is feeling anxious and overwhelmed due to upcoming exams and a fear of failure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that A is preoccupied with the fear of disappointing her mother and exhibits avoidance behavior when faced with academic pressure. However, A is open to adjusting her study habits and exploring alternative strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to assist A in restructuring negative thought patterns and formulate a structured study schedule to manage exam anxiety effectively.
