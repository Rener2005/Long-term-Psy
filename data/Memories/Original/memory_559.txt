# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Tom is a sports coach dealing with pressure from recent team performance. He is focused on how to foster better team dynamics through the application of sports psychology.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Tom experiences stress related to coaching and player management. He seeks to improve team cohesion but hasn't disclosed any formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Tom feels overwhelmed yet eager to learn sports psychology strategies to enhance team performance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Tom's openness to new strategies and his earnest concern for his team's well-being, indicating a willingness to try innovative approaches for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Tom introduce team-building exercises and improve communication within the team, fostering trust and cooperation.
