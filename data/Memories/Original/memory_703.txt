# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a university student who deals with anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, especially regarding social interactions and family dynamics. Li often feels caught between family obligations and personal peace, particularly concerning conflicts between parents.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious and frustrated due to family pressures and self-comparison with peers, leading to feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li struggles with guilt and a strong sense of obligation, resulting in self-doubt and the belief that their worth is tied to external validation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li explore and challenge negative thought patterns, focusing on self-worth and reducing anxiety. Encourage small steps towards engaging more with peers.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed by a sense of invisibility in group settings, contributing to increased anxiety and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes a cycle of negative self-perceptions in social situations but sees willingness in Li to change this narrative and improve social connections.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li in identifying thought patterns that contribute to anxiety, using CBT to develop strategies for initiating and maintaining social interactions, focusing on gradual exposure to group settings.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels nervous and experiences self-doubt following unsuccessful attempts to engage in conversations with classmates, leading to increased anxiety.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li harbors critical self-thoughts post-interaction but shows openness to learning and improving through practiced social skills in low-pressure environments.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Li to practice social skills in relaxed settings, using CBT to reframe self-critical thoughts and build confidence through manageable social goals.

# Fourth Consultation

# Sixth Consultation
