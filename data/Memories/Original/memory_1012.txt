# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Dan Dan is a university student who struggles with loneliness and feelings of social inadequacy, especially in relation to forming and maintaining friendships. Recently, she has been feeling increasingly distanced from her friends, which has affected her sleep and concentration.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Dan Dan experiences symptoms of social anxiety and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Dan Dan feels lonely and disconnected from her peers, with self-doubt impacting her self-worth and social interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Dan Dan’s self-doubt stems from perceived social exclusion, yet she demonstrates a willingness to engage in self-reflective activities to improve her situation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Dan Dan’s negative perceptions and encourage her to engage in small, positive social interactions to build confidence.
