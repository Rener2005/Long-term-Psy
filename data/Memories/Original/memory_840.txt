# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student grappling with anxiety and sensitivity regarding their relationships, particularly feeling isolated in their dormitory. They worry about their roommates' perceptions related to academic and social standing, often feeling judged and inadequate.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits signs of anxiety, particularly social and performance anxiety, without a specific formal diagnosis reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and isolated, worried about judgment from roommates, particularly concerning academic and social standing. 

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a significant influence of social comparisons on the user's self-worth, but also notes the user's potential to nurture self-compassion through reflection.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge and reframe negative self-perceptions and foster self-compassion. Encourage steps towards social engagement and reduce feelings of isolation through manageable activities.
