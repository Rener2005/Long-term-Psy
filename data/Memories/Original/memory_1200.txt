# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
G (pseudonym) is an individual experiencing struggles with self-esteem and confidence, particularly influenced by eating habits and social interactions. They are reflective and eager to improve their emotional responses and personal habits.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
G is exhibiting signs of anxiety and self-esteem issues but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
G feels overwhelmed and anxious about their eating habits, especially binge eating, and experiences a persistent cycle of guilt and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes G's struggles with self-critical thoughts and the tendency to seek comfort through food, but also observes a willingness to explore healthier alternatives and self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help G identify and challenge negative thought patterns, promote the use of self-compassion, and explore alternative self-soothing activities beyond food.
