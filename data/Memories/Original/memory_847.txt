# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang (pseudonym) is a student who struggles with self-esteem issues, particularly concerning body image and weight. Wang has faced peer criticism and feels self-conscious in social and academic settings. Despite these challenges, Wang shows a desire for personal growth and improved self-confidence.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang experiences symptoms of anxiety linked to body image and social interactions but has no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels uneasy and anxious due to classmates' comments about body image, impacting concentration on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wang shows significant self-consciousness and anxiety about social judgment but has a compassionate perspective towards self-worth and potential for growth.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Wang's negative self-perceptions and develop coping mechanisms for anxiety related to body image. Encourage participation in social settings with a focus on self-worth beyond appearance.
