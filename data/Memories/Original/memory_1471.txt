# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Jun (pseudonym) is a high school student facing challenges with peer interactions and self-esteem due to teasing about his physical appearance, specifically his height. He is eager to improve his emotional management and find constructive ways to deal with conflict.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Jun experiences feelings of anger, inadequacy, and struggles with self-esteem. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Jun feels anger and frustration due to being teased about his height by classmates, which leads to a sense of helplessness and a desire to retaliate.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Jun’s reactions stem from a deeply rooted frustration and sense of being undermined, but he shows a willingness to explore more assertive and calm responses.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral strategies to help Xiao Jun express his feelings assertively and develop healthy responses to teasing. Begin building techniques for anger management and emotional regulation.
