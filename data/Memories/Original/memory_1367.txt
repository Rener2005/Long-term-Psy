# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Qin (pseudonym) is an adult who experiences intense anxiety, particularly related to taking public transport. The anxiety is exacerbated by a traumatic experience on a crowded bus. Qin is determined to overcome this fear, having no disclosed formal mental health diagnoses.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Qin is dealing with symptoms of anxiety, particularly in response to specific situations such as crowded environments. There is no formal diagnosis of an anxiety disorder shared.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Qin feels overwhelmed and panicked when thinking about or experiencing crowded bus rides, fearing a loss of control and physical symptoms like heart attacks.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Qin’s intense fear response is tied to perceived physical threats, but Qin is open to utilizing coping mechanisms and CBT strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to identify and challenge irrational fears, helping Qin develop coping statements and gradually desensitize to triggers through exposure therapy.
