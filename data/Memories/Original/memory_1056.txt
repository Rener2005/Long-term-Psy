# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhao (pseudonym) is a student who recently transferred to a new school, and he feels out of place. He struggles with feelings of inadequacy when comparing himself to peers and turns to video games to escape the pressure.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhao experiences symptoms of anxiety related to academic performance and social interactions, without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao feels inadequate and anxious about his academic performance, often resorting to video games as a means of escape.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhao's cycle of negative self-comparison and reliance on video games for a sense of accomplishment, coupled with the desire to make positive changes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhao manage gaming habits and enhance motivation for academic studies. Encourage initial steps toward building peer connections to reduce feelings of isolation.
