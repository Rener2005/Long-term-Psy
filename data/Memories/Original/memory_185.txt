# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyou (pseudonym) is a university student experiencing feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly in social settings and personal interactions. Recent conflicts with a roommate and academic pressures have intensified these feelings, which Xiaoyou is keen to address.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyou exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, but has not reported a formal diagnosis of any mental health disorders.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyou is feeling low and anxious, primarily due to a conflict with her roommate, resulting in feelings of judgment and a struggle to concentrate on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoyou is caught in a cycle of negative self-perception and feels heightened anxiety in social interactions but shows readiness to explore and challenge these patterns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyou identify and challenge negative thought patterns, fostering self-compassion, and develop strategies for managing feelings of judgment and improving concentration.
