# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Gang (pseudonym) is a university student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, primarily connected to academic performance and managing high expectations. Recently, Xiao Gang has been dealing with increased pressure due to upcoming exams.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Gang experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt related to academic challenges. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder reported at this time.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Gang feels overwhelmed and unfocused due to academic pressure, and expresses feeling demotivated by the volume of material to study.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Gang experiences a significant amount of stress and anxiety related to academic workload, but shows motivation to develop better study strategies and manage stress.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Gang create structured study plans and reduce anxiety through the implementation of relaxation techniques and breaking study material into manageable sections. Encourage mindfulness and focused study sessions.
