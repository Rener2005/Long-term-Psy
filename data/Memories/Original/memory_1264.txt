# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Stefanie (pseudonym) is a young girl dealing with feelings of being overlooked in her family due to the arrival of a new sibling. Stefanie is navigating through these familial changes while trying to maintain emotional connections with her family.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Stefanie experiences feelings of neglect and frustration, leading to expressions of loneliness and anger, though no formal mental health disorder has been diagnosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Stefanie feels upset and unimportant due to shifting family dynamics with a new baby in the house, which she perceives as a loss of attention.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes feelings of anger linked to loneliness, but Stefanie shows an understanding of her emotions and is open to considering alternative ways to express her needs.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Stefanie recognize and express her emotions constructively. Encourage her to develop alternative methods to seek familial attention and validation.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Stefanie feels sad and lonely, missing the time spent with her mom due to changes in her schedule, which heightens feelings of being ignored.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Stefanie's courage in acknowledging loneliness and desires for closeness, indicating potential for developing healthier communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Stefanie in articulating her needs and feelings to her family in a positive manner. Encourage forming new social connections and balanced participation in group activities at school.

[Consultation Goals]  
Build Stefanie’s confidence in social settings by using CBT techniques to challenge negative self-perceptions and practice positive engagement strategies.
