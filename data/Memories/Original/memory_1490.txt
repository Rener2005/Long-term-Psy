[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaohua struggles with anxiety linked to performance and social concerns. While she hasn't been formally diagnosed, she exhibits symptoms of anxiety and stress potentially rooted in perfectionism and familial pressures.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohua feels heightened anxiety due to exam pressures and is struggling with negative self-comparisons.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaohua's tendency to equate self-worth with academic success and observe a pattern of internalized familial expectations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaohua challenge the association between self-worth and grades. Encourage self-compassion and the development of coping strategies for exam-related anxiety.
