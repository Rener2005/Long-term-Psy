[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms of social anxiety, particularly in work-related social interactions and professional settings, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels anxious about work performance and comparisons with colleagues, especially in meetings and social contexts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang's pervasive fear of judgment in social and professional scenarios, yet observes a willingness to explore new perspectives and strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang reframe negative thoughts and manage anxiety. Equip Zhang with practical strategies for improving confidence in social and professional communication.
