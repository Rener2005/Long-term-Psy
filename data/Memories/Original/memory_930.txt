# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly regarding body image and social interactions. Recently, their anxiety has increased due to school group activities, where they feel judged by classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, manifesting in fear of judgment and negative self-comparisons in group settings.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels insecure and anxious about their appearance and participation in group activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's intense fear of judgment and self-doubt, noting the cycle of silence despite having valuable contributions to share.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge the user's negative self-talk and promote active participation in social settings. Encourage gradual exposure to anxiety-inducing situations with positive reinforcement.
