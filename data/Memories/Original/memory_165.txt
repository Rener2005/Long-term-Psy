# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Qing (pseudonym) is a high school student struggling with the pressure of academic expectations and frequent conflicts with parents regarding gaming habits. Qing finds solace and recognition in gaming and seeks to improve parental understanding and communication.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Qing experiences feelings of isolation and frustration due to family dynamics but has not disclosed any formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Qing is feeling frustrated and misunderstood, experiencing anger and isolation due to frequent parental criticism concerning grades and gaming habits.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Qing's difficulty in expressing feelings to parents, but notes Qing's potential openness to alternative communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral strategies to help Qing communicate stress and emotional needs effectively to parents. Encourage Qing to explore constructive approaches in conversations to avoid defensive responses.
