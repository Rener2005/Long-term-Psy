# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is a university student who has recently experienced trauma due to an incident with a boyfriend. Li describes feelings of anxiety and sadness which impact daily life and academic focus. Li seeks to process these emotions and manage them better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms indicative of trauma-related anxiety and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed and anxious due to traumatic memories, often questioning personal responsibility in the incident.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li struggles with feelings of guilt and self-blame, but is willing to explore more compassionate viewpoints with guidance.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li challenge self-blaming thoughts and develop coping strategies such as mindfulness and journaling to manage anxiety.
