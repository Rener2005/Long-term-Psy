# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Hui (pseudonym) is a high school student experiencing feelings of inadequacy and pressure from high parental expectations. He is adjusting to the transition from middle school to high school and struggles with self-esteem due to negative feedback from teachers. Li Hui often uses video games and social media to cope with stress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Hui experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Hui feels lost and overwhelmed by the pressure to meet academic expectations, leading to increased stress and a sense of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li Hui's struggles with self-confidence and his reliance on technology as a coping mechanism, yet he shows willingness to explore healthier coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Hui manage anxiety and develop healthier coping strategies. Encourage small steps to reduce phone usage and facilitate communication with parents about his feelings.
