# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xi is a university student struggling with anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, stemming from familial expectations and recent personal losses. She is seeking to regain control over her academic performance while navigating social connections.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xi exhibits symptoms of anxiety linked to academic pressure and low self-esteem, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xi feels overwhelmed and anxious due to academic pressures and familial expectations, describing a sense of disconnection and inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xi's tendency to internalize negative self-assessments, yet recognizes her willingness to explore and adopt more positive self-perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xi identify and challenge negative self-perceptions. Encourage strategies to manage academic anxiety and foster social connections through gradual, supportive interactions.
