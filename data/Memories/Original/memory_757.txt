# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is a woman navigating complex family dynamics, particularly in her relationship with her mother-in-law. She faces challenges in maintaining emotional equilibrium due to these interactions, which also affect her motivation and social engagement outside of the family.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of stress and anxiety related to family and financial pressures but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels lost and frustrated due to ongoing conflicts with her mother-in-law, which impacts her self-esteem and motivation at work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Li experiences significant pressure and self-doubt, but demonstrates a willingness to explore her feelings and adopt new communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li express her emotions constructively and recognize her personal achievements. Encourage Li to separate perceptions of failure in her personal life from her professional success.
