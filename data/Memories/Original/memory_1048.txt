# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a student who is battling severe anxiety and self-doubt, particularly in relation to academic pressures and feelings of inadequacy. Li is eager to manage these intrusive thoughts and experiences episodes of significant emotional distress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li exhibits symptoms of anxiety, with indications of low self-esteem and occasional suicidal ideation, but has not been formally diagnosed with any specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li is feeling extremely anxious due to impending exams, with fears of failure contributing to negative thoughts and a sense of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li is overwhelmed by the fear of disappointing family and feels isolated by these heavy thoughts, yet shows some recognition of having a supportive family.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li reframe negative thoughts and utilize coping mechanisms such as creating reminders to practice deep breathing and positive affirmations.

# Second Consultation
