# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Zhang is a mother working to regain custody of her children. She is dealing with anxiety and self-doubt, especially concerning her parenting abilities. Recent events involve pressure from a social worker to start treatment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Zhang experiences anxiety and self-esteem issues related to her role as a parent, but has not shared any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Zhang feels anxious and overwhelmed by the pressure of regaining custody, fearing she might not be enough as a mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices that Ms. Zhang experiences deep shame and self-doubt, yet there is a desire to develop self-compassion and improve her situation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Ms. Zhang's negative self-perceptions and develop self-compassion. Identify first steps towards creating a stable environment for her children.

# Second Consultation
