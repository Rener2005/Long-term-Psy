# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a professional in his mid-30s who has been dealing with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety related to his job performance and interpersonal relationships. Recently, he experienced a project failure at work that heightened his self-doubt and intensified his workplace anxiety.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei exhibits symptoms that suggest anxiety and low self-esteem. There is no formal diagnosis disclosed at this point.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels overwhelmed and anxious following a recent project failure, which has triggered doubts about his career choices.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Zhang Wei has a pattern of linking specific failures to global beliefs about his professional worth, yet he shows capacity for self-reflection and openness to reframing these beliefs.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei reframe the project failure as a learning opportunity. Encourage a step-by-step approach to future projects to build confidence and prevent overwhelming pressure.
