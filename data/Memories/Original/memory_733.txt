# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang (pseudonym) is a university student navigating feelings of anxiety and struggles with social interactions, particularly around relationships with peers. Recently, Wang has been encountering difficulties with a roommate that have heightened stress levels, affecting daily life and studies. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang exhibits symptoms of anxiety related to interpersonal relationships, without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels anxious and tense due to a conflict with a roommate, leading to feelings of isolation and challenges focusing on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Wang's tendency to internalize negative perceptions of peer relationships, which exacerbates feelings of anxiety, yet Wang shows openness to exploring alternative interpretations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang identify and challenge negative thoughts and perceptions about the conflict situation. Develop strategies for managing anxiety through small, positive interactions and communication efforts.
