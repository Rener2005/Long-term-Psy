# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is an individual dealing with the recent passing of their father, which has brought about mixed emotions of sadness and a newfound sense of freedom. They are navigating their grief while trying to honor their father's memory through personal success and fulfillment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences grieving behaviors but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis related to mental health disorders. They are exploring their emotions related to loss and striving for personal growth.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels confused by a mix of sadness and freedom following their father's death, alongside guilt for feeling liberated.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang Wei's struggle with the conflicting emotions of grief and independence, noting a genuine desire to understand and process these feelings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Zhang Wei to explore and express their emotions freely through reflective practices like journaling and sharing with friends. Use cognitive behavioral techniques to address and reframe feelings of guilt and confusion.
