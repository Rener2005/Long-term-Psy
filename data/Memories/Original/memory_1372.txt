# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Shen is engaged in a work environment where leadership conflicts have caused tension, significantly impacting his motivation and sense of belonging at work. He is reflective about his experiences and is keen on finding ways to handle stress and build better workplace relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Shen reports experiencing anxiety and frustration due to work-related stress but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Shen feels anxious and frustrated due to leadership conflicts affecting workplace communication and collaboration.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Shen's tendency to procrastinate and feel discouraged, but also notes his openness to finding small steps to improve team communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Shen identify and challenge negative thoughts about workplace dynamics. Encourage initiation of small, positive interactions to reduce isolation and enhance communication.
