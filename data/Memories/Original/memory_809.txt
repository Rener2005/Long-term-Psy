# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yang (pseudonym) is a recently married woman who struggles with feelings of anxiety and regret regarding her marriage. She feels disrespected by her husband's traditional mindset, especially during intimate moments, and is concerned about how her family might react if the marriage does not work out.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yang experiences symptoms of anxiety linked to relationship distress but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yang feels conflicted and anxious about her marriage, experiencing feelings of being unheard and disrespected by her husband.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Yang struggles with effectively communicating her needs and experiences self-doubt regarding her feelings' validity.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Yang in utilizing CBT strategies to accurately express emotions and assert her needs in a calm, constructive manner. Encourage understanding of self-validation and respectful dialogue in her marriage.
