# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Qian (pseudonym) is a student experiencing significant anxiety, particularly in social situations involving women. He feels uncertain about his social skills and is eager to improve them.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Qian experiences social anxiety, especially when interacting with women, and fears negative judgment. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Qian feels anxious and dreads social interactions with women, leading to avoidance behavior.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Qian's anxiety manifests as avoidance, but shows a willingness to understand and alter his thinking patterns.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Qian challenge his exaggerated fears and develop strategies for social engagement, starting with small steps towards initiating conversations.
