# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a student who experiences stress and anxiety related to their studies, particularly in preparation for exams. They express a desire for change and improvement in their study habits.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and stress tied to academic pressure. No formal diagnosis of a mental disorder has been provided.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and unmotivated in relation to studying, experiencing persistent negative thoughts about academic failure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency towards negative self-assessment and catastrophizing about academic outcomes but observes openness to exploring alternative, positive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user recognize and challenge negative thoughts, promoting motivation and balanced thinking. Develop manageable study goals and strategies to improve focus and reduce anxiety.
