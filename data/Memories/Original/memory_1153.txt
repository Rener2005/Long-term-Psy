# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is a young woman facing pressure from her boyfriend regarding marriage and uncertainty surrounding her family's disapproval of the relationship. She is grappling with these combined pressures while considering her own readiness for such a significant life decision.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences anxiety and feelings of being overwhelmed due to conflicting pressures from her partner and family, without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious and confused, experiencing significant pressure from her boyfriend about marriage, amid her family's disapproval.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li is caught in a conflict between her desires and others' expectations, but she shows an ability to consider her own needs amid this.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Li explore her thoughts and feelings surrounding marriage using cognitive behavioral techniques, focusing on articulating her emotions and needs clearly to her boyfriend and family.
