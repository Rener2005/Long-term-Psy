# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is a professional working in a high-stress environment, feeling overwhelmed by project deadlines and a lack of cooperation at work. Li Ming is motivated to find strategies to better manage work-related stress and improve communication with colleagues.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming exhibits symptoms of work-related anxiety and stress but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels anxious and overwhelmed due to upcoming project deadlines and insufficient team cooperation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li Ming's struggle with a heavy sense of responsibility and self-reliance, leading to feelings of helplessness in the workplace.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Li Ming in addressing negative thought patterns and fostering effective communication with colleagues. Initiate strategies for reducing work-related stress through mindfulness and task management.
