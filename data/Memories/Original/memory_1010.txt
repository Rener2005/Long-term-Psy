[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Bin experiences symptoms of anxiety, particularly related to social settings and performance situations, without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder being disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Bin feels anxious about speaking in class, fearing judgments from peers due to past negative experiences.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Bin's heightened anxiety when anticipating public speaking and notes a cycle of self-doubt reinforced by past incidents.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Bin challenge negative self-perceptions and thoughts. Initiate steps to manage anxiety, including preparation for class and relaxation techniques like deep breathing.
