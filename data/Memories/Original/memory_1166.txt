Li presents symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, with no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory

Li feels overwhelmed and anxious about academics, experiencing frequent thoughts of inadequacy and failure.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
