# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang Hong is struggling with feelings of inadequacy and conflict in personal relationships, particularly with a friend named Li Ming. Wang Hong is seeking to improve communication skills and emotional management while navigating interpersonal tensions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang Hong experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt in social interactions but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang Hong feels overwhelmed and uncertain about handling tensions in the relationship with Li Ming, experiencing a significant feeling of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wang Hong is trapped in negative self-perception during conflicts but shows readiness to challenge these thoughts and adopt a more balanced view.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang Hong challenge negative self-beliefs and foster strategies for open communication to reduce conflict anxiety.
