# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Shao (pseudonym) is a student navigating challenges related to feelings of self-worth and anxiety, often influenced by negative feedback about appearance from family, particularly a grandmother. Shao is attentive to social dynamics and seeks to build more meaningful connections while battling self-imposed limitations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Shao experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, though no formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Shao feels lonely and self-conscious after classmates blocked them on social media, questioning self-worth and fearing judgment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Shao's strong tendency toward negative self-comparisons and susceptibility to external criticisms, yet notes an openness to reframe these thoughts positively.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Shao's negative self-perceptions and encourage proactive steps towards social engagement. Support Shao in identifying positive interactions to foster a more balanced self-view.
