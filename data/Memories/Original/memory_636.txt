# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
B (pseudonym) is a soon-to-graduate student who is passionate about pursuing a career in the maritime industry. B's recent conflicts with their parents center around career choices, with parents advocating for safer, traditional job options.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
B experiences stress and anxiety regarding parental approval and career decisions, but does not report any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
B feels disappointed and frustrated due to recurring arguments with parents about career choices and experiences anxiety about the future.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives B as sincerely passionate about maritime careers and notes the tension from parental disagreements, but also notices B's eagerness to find a peaceful resolution.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help B develop communication strategies to express career intentions effectively while managing conflict with parents. Encourage proactive expression of interests and emphasize skill demonstration as part of the dialogue.
