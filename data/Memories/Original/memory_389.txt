# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei (pseudonym) is a project manager who experiences anxiety surrounding leadership abilities and communication, particularly in light of recent organizational changes. Zhang Wei is eager to learn and apply new strategies to improve team dynamics and personal leadership skills.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences anxiety related to leadership and communication challenges but has not indicated any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious due to uncertainty stemming from recent company changes and doubts about leadership abilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang Wei is experiencing self-imposed pressure to have all leadership aspects figured out, yet shows a readiness to reflect on past capabilities and adopt a perspective of personal growth.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei challenge negative self-assessments and focus on effective communication strategies, promoting self-compassion and resilience in leadership roles.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory

[Consultation Goals]
