[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lili shows symptoms of social anxiety and stress but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lili feels overwhelmed and isolated, worrying about the impact of rumors on her social interactions at school.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Lili struggles with self-doubt stemming from peer judgment, but she demonstrates insight into her own strengths and is open to reframing her thinking.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Lili identify and challenge negative thoughts. Enhance her self-awareness and confidence through recognition of her positive qualities and support network.
