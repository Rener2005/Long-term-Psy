# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Yu (pseudonym) cares deeply for her family and relationships, is a university student who often feels overwhelmed by her social interactions, and struggles with anxiety related to perceived inadequacies and responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Yu experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, notably in her social interactions, though she has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yu feels anxious and overwhelmed, largely due to concerns about her roommate's distant behavior and her sister's mental health condition.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Yu is burdened by feelings of inadequacy and guilt, but also shows a willingness to challenge her self-critical thoughts and cultivate self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Yu challenge negative self-perceptions and develop self-compassion. Assist her in managing relationship-related stress and supporting her sister without compromising her own well-being.
