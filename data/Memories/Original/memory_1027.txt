# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Q (pseudonym) is a senior student dealing with overwhelming feelings due to the pressure of upcoming exams. Xiao Q is particularly worried about falling behind classmates and manages feelings of anxiety surrounding academic performance and expectations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Q exhibits symptoms of anxiety, primarily related to performance and self-doubt concerning academic success and peer comparisons. No formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Q feels overwhelmed by the pressure of exams and experiences significant self-doubt, contributing to anxiety and sleep disturbances.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Q is caught in a cycle of self-doubt and anxiety, impacting motivation and sleep, but is open to challenging negative beliefs.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Q identify and challenge distorted beliefs about hard work and peer comparisons. Develop strategies to improve sleep through relaxation techniques and a consistent nighttime routine.
