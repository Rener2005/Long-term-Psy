# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a female experiencing significant stress and anxiety due to recent conflicts with her husband and challenges in balancing work and parenting responsibilities. She often feels inadequate and overwhelmed.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits anxiety and symptoms of low self-esteem, struggling with negative thought patterns and self-worth issues, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and emotionally unstable, haunted by her husband's mention of divorce, which contributes to feelings of worthlessness and fear.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user struggles with negative self-perceptions and fears of abandonment, but shows openness to exploring more positive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help the user identify and challenge negative thoughts using cognitive behavioral techniques, and introduce strategies for emotional self-regulation, like mindfulness and deep breathing exercises.
