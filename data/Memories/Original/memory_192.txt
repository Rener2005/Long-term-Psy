# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyue is a young individual grappling with the aftermath of her parents' divorce. She shoulders the responsibility of caring for her younger brother and strives for her mother's approval. These dynamics contribute to feelings of inadequacy and emotional distress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyue experiences symptoms of anxiety, self-doubt, and emotional overwhelm. There is no formal diagnosis disclosed, but her feelings of worthlessness and guilt are notable.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyue feels emotionally overwhelmed, caught between a desire for parental approval and the perception of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Xiaoyue’s deep desire to be understood and her current isolation and guilt as primary coping mechanisms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyue challenge negative self-perceptions and develop constructive coping strategies. Focus on fostering self-compassion and emotional self-awareness.
