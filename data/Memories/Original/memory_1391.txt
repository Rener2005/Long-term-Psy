# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a young adult experiencing anxiety and recurring thoughts about potential breakups with their boyfriend, despite acknowledging that he treats them well. The user often feels overwhelmed by work and personal expectations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user has not disclosed any formal diagnosis but exhibits symptoms of anxiety and relationship insecurity.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels trapped in a cycle of anxiety and guilt about relationship thoughts, struggling with perceived dissatisfaction despite positive aspects.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user's thoughts are heavily influenced by stress and external expectations, but there's a willingness to explore these feelings further.

[Consultation Goals]  
To use cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user identify and challenge negative thought patterns, and to develop coping strategies for managing stress and anxiety in the relationship.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user is confused and anxious due to family pressure about marriage, which contributes to relationship doubts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that familial expectations significantly affect the user's feelings of entrapment and relationship insecurity.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage the user to explore feelings toward their boyfriend independently of family pressure, using CBT strategies to assess personal values and relationship needs.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by a conflict between the desire for relationship stability and fear of unhappiness, leading to persistent stress and doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that past relational experiences contribute to current doubts, though there's a readiness to confront these fears meaningfully.

[Consultation Goals]  
Guide the user to articulate personal relationship goals and explore deep conversations with the partner to develop mutual understanding and emotional clarity.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that while the user values their partner, fears from past relationship failures create barriers to feeling secure and satisfied.

[Consultation Goals]

# Sixth Consultation
