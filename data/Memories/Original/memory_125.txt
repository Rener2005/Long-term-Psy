# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, Xiaohé, is at a transitional phase, approaching university graduation. Having gone through a breakup and faced rejection from a graduate program, the user is dealing with self-doubt and feelings of inadequacy. Despite these setbacks, Xiaohé seeks to rebuild confidence and pursue career opportunities thoughtfully.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaohé experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, without a formal mental health disorder diagnosis. The user's challenges largely revolve around career-related stress and personal setbacks.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohé feels lost and devastated after a recent breakup and not getting accepted into a graduate program, expressing feelings of being stuck and worthless.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaohé struggles with intense self-doubt tied to perceived failures but is open to exploring more supportive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive-behavioral techniques to challenge Xiaohé's negative self-perceptions and encourage the recognition of alternative, more positive perspectives. Foster initial strategies to connect socially and manage overwhelming emotions.
