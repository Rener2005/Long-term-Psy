# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, referred to as "A," is experiencing a challenging relationship with their mother, focusing on academic pressures and communication difficulties. A feels overwhelmed by the mother's expectations and struggles with feelings of anxiety and inadequacy, exacerbated by recent declines in academic performance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis. A reported thoughts of self-harm, linked to feeling trapped and overwhelmed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A feels intensely pressured by their mother's expectations, leading to anxiety and thoughts of self-harm as an escape.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes A's struggle with feeling inadequate and a strong desire for validation and emotional support. A displays a willingness to explore healthier coping mechanisms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help A articulate feelings and manage stress triggers. Work on developing strategies to communicate needs effectively and reduce self-harming thoughts.

# Second Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
