# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyu is a high school student dealing with feelings of stress related to academic pressures, particularly stemming from parental expectations. Xiaoyu is trying to navigate communication challenges with her mother, who is focused on academic success.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyu experiences typical stress responses, such as headaches and stomachaches, in academic settings but has not expressed any clinically diagnosed anxiety or mental health disorders.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyu feels frustrated and stressed about her mother's strict expectations but believes her stress is manageable and typical.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes Xiaoyu's desire to be understood by her mother and her effort to express her feelings, yet she struggles to communicate effectively without confrontation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyu express feelings constructively and encourage effective communication strategies to enhance mutual understanding with her mother.
