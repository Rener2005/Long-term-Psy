[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
H shows signs of anxiety stemming from low self-esteem in social situations, particularly with new peers, but has not been officially diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

[Mental State]  
H feels lost and socially disconnected, expressing anxiety about failing to fit in with new classmates and feeling awkward in social situations.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help H adopt more balanced thoughts about social interactions, focusing on small progressive steps to engage with classmates and reduce feelings of isolation.
