# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is a university student dealing with emotional challenges stemming from a recent breakup. With a strong academic background, she often experiences anxiety and self-doubt in social situations, particularly those involving male peers. Recently, she has been trying to navigate her feelings of inadequacy and relationship uncertainty with her classmate, Li.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang exhibits symptoms of anxiety and insecurity related to social interactions and past relationship experiences. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang is feeling lost and overwhelmed due to her recent breakup, replaying negative thoughts and emotions surrounding her relationship with Li.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang's tendency towards self-blame and harsh self-criticism, but also a desire to shift towards self-kindness and positivity.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce Zhang to cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge her negative internal dialogue and promote self-compassion, facilitating a healthier perspective on her relationship ending.
