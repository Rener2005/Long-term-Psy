# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
GXH is a caregiver dedicated to looking after her ill mother for the past eight years. Amidst the relentless responsibilities, GXH struggles with exhaustion, inadequate self-care, and guilt. She seeks moments of reprieve while grappling with the societal and personal expectations of her caregiving role.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
GXH experiences significant anxiety, guilt, and stress, primarily linked to caregiving responsibilities. She has not disclosed a formal diagnosis of any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
GXH feels overwhelmed and exhausted, carrying a heavy burden of anxiety and guilt due to her caregiving duties.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that GXH is deeply dedicated to her mother's care but struggles with feelings of inadequacy and isolation. Nevertheless, she shows readiness to explore small acts of self-care.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage GXH to integrate simple self-care activities into her routine using CBT techniques. Foster a supportive narrative where self-care is viewed as crucial for effective caregiving.
