[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A-Ling experiences symptoms of anxiety and emotional distress, struggling to manage her situation without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help A-Ling manage overwhelming emotions by identifying actionable steps towards obtaining legal guidance and emotional support.
