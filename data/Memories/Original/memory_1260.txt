# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
User A is a university student dealing with significant anxiety and feelings of isolation following a recent breakup. They express concerns about physical health, particularly reproductive health, which exacerbates their anxiety. Social anxiety is pronounced, leading to avoidance of social interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
User A experiences symptoms of anxiety heightened by health-related worries and social situations. There is no mention of a formal diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
User A is feeling low and isolated, with intensified anxiety about physical health following a breakup.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes User A's tendency to magnify health concerns, compounded by an acute awareness of others' judgments in social settings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help User A manage health-related anxiety and address their fear of social judgment. Initiate exploration of small steps to reconnect with friends and engage in relaxing activities.
