# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Song (pseudonym) is a student who experiences significant anxiety, particularly around contamination and cleanliness. Recently, an encounter with classmates triggered excessive hand-washing behaviors, causing distress.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Song exhibits symptoms of contamination-related anxiety and obsessive-compulsive tendencies, though no formal mental health disorder has been diagnosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Song feels anxious and overwhelmed, driven by fears of contamination after a social encounter labeled as unclean.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Song’s difficulty in breaking the cycle of irrational fear and compulsive hand-washing, yet Song demonstrates a willingness to address these behaviors.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Song's irrational fears and reduce hand-washing. Develop strategies to manage social anxiety and encourage gradual exposure to feared situations.
