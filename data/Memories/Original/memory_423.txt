# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a working professional experiencing significant anxiety due to pressure from work, including project delays and team dynamics. He struggles with balancing work and personal life and fears disappointing his team and risking his career.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei shows symptoms of anxiety related to high expectations and stress from his professional responsibilities but has not shared a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels overwhelmed with work responsibilities, encountering feelings of hopelessness and inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notices Zhang Wei's tendency to focus on negative outcomes, yet he is open to discussing alternative viewpoints.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Zhang Wei in recognizing control over stressors and developing practical task management strategies to alleviate anxiety.
