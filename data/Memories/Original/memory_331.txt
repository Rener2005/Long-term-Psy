# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaohong is a young student who struggles with feelings of loneliness and self-doubt, particularly regarding friendships and family dynamics after her parents' separation. She is eager to connect with others and seeks to improve her social skills and emotional expression.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaohong experiences symptoms of social anxiety and insecurity, especially in social situations. There have been no formal diagnoses of a mental health disorder shared.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohong feels lonely and inadequate in social settings at school, worrying about her ability to make friends and feeling left out.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaohong is burdened by self-doubt and loneliness but shows a willingness to reframe negative thoughts and approach social interactions with curiosity and optimism.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaohong challenge negative self-beliefs and encourage authentic self-expression. Foster strategies to increase her confidence in initiating and sustaining friendships.
