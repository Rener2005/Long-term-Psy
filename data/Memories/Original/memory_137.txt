# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
DXJ is an adult who has been facing feelings of anxiety and uncertainty related to unemployment and career direction. Recently, DXJ has been actively seeking employment opportunities and adapting to new roles and environments during this transitional phase.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
DXJ experiences symptoms of anxiety linked to job insecurity and self-doubt, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
DXJ feels anxious and lost due to the challenge of finding work and the accompanying fear of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that DXJ frequently doubts their capabilities but is willing to explore and affirm personal strengths.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help DXJ identify and reframe negative thought patterns about self-worth and employability. Foster self-confidence through skills recognition and preparation practices, such as job interview role-plays.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
DXJ is happy to have found temporary work but feels pressured to prove themselves and quickly adapt to new tasks.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes DXJ's anxiety around job performance is linked to the fear of letting others down, yet there's recognition of support from supervisors.

[Consultation Goals]  
Develop stress management techniques and a structured task management plan using CBT strategies. Encourage balanced self-assessment to reduce anxiety and reinforce positive job performance perceptions.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
DXJ is focused on personal development and has crafted a plan to improve skills and job prospects, yet feels overwhelmed at times.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that while DXJ feels confident with a clear direction, maintaining motivation in the face of discouragement remains challenging.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support DXJ in setting small, achievable learning goals and incorporating motivational strategies for sustained focus. Encourage reflective practices to boost confidence and manage feelings of overwhelm.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
DXJ feels positive and engaged with new skills training, although efficiency in studying remains a concern.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes DXJ's enthusiasm in skill acquisition but acknowledges difficulties maintaining focus due to a lack of structured study habits.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist in establishing an effective study schedule using CBT techniques and emphasize the role of hands-on practice in retention. Promote regular goal setting and celebrate accomplishments to enhance motivation.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
DXJ feels content and secure with new job stability, gaining confidence in self-sufficiency.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives notable progress in DXJ's self-confidence but identifies underlying anxiety regarding maintaining job performance.

[Consultation Goals]
