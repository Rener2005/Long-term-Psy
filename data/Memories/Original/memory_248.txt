# First Consultation

She has recently ended a relationship and developed feelings for a classmate, which has been disapproved of by her parents and teachers. Xiaoqian has ambitious dreams, previously aspiring to be an astronaut, and is now seeking balance and motivation in her studies.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoqian is experiencing anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, exacerbated by interpersonal conflicts and academic pressures. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoqian feels confused and irritated due to a conflict between her feelings towards a classmate and external pressures, which impacts her focus on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoqian struggles with feeling controlled by authority figures but is willing to explore small steps towards regaining focus and autonomy.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoqian recognize and manage her emotional responses. Begin implementing strategies to gradually improve her academic focus through routine rebuilding and reframing personal goals.
