[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels anxious and isolated due to conflicts with classmates and seeks respect and validation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang Wei interprets classmates' behaviors negatively, leading to cycles of self-doubt, yet shows a willingness to consider alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei challenge negative interpretations of social interactions. Encourage initiating conversations to rebuild connections and foster self-esteem.
