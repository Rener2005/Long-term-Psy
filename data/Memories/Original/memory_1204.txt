# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student experiencing feelings of loneliness and anxiety, particularly when communicating with female classmates. With graduation approaching, the user is concerned about future relationships and lacks confidence during conversations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of social anxiety related to low self-confidence and fear of rejection. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder provided.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels lonely and anxious about engaging in conversations with female classmates due to a lack of confidence and fear of rejection.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user struggles with self-doubt and fear of embarrassment but demonstrates a willingness to develop and practice new social strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user reframe negative thoughts and fears about social interactions. Encourage practicing low-pressure conversational scenarios to build confidence and reduce anxiety.
