# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
T is a high school student feeling overwhelmed by academic pressures and social isolation. They struggle with self-doubt and seek to overcome feelings of inadequacy and the perception of being misunderstood by classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
T experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
T feels stuck and isolated due to school pressures, struggling with negative self-perceptions and exhaustion.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that T appears burdened by persistent negative thoughts but expresses a readiness to consider more supportive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help T challenge negative thoughts and develop a more balanced self-view. Encourage small steps towards self-care and positive reframing to improve engagement and mood.
