# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy, anxiety, especially in relation to academic performance, and social interactions. They have recently faced academic setbacks, such as failing a few courses, which have intensified their feelings of pressure and self-doubt.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem and although they have not disclosed a formal diagnosis, these feelings appear to be rooted in academic stress and social insecurity.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by academic pressures, exhibiting anxiety and a persistent sense of worthlessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives that the user is heavily burdened by negative self-talk and doubts their abilities, yet they display openness towards contemplating more positive interpretations of their situation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to assist the user in challenging negative thoughts and fostering small, positive changes. Introduce manageable goals to incrementally enhance self-confidence and improve academic engagement.
