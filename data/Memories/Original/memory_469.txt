# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ming (pseudonym) is an elementary school student who enjoys helping friends and is eager to improve in social and academic areas. Xiao Ming is proud of personal achievements and is motivated to maintain and develop positive relationships with peers.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming exhibits confidence issues in social settings but shows no signs of a diagnosed mental health disorder. There is a strong willingness to learn and improve social skills and behavior.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels happy and proud after being praised by the teacher for good behavior and is eager to maintain this behavior.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Ming has a genuine interest in both personal development and supporting classmates, indicating strong interpersonal values and emotional intelligence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Xiao Ming utilize cognitive behavioral strategies, like deep breathing and self-reminders, to support self-control in social scenarios. Encourage positive behavior reinforcement and leadership qualities through peer support initiatives.
