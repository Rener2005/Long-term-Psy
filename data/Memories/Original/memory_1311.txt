# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a high school student who has been experiencing conflicts with her mother, leading to frustration and sadness. She struggles with managing her emotional responses during arguments and desires to communicate more effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li shows signs of emotional distress characterized by experiences of frustration and feeling misunderstood but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed by constant arguments with her mother and struggles with controlling her reactions during these disputes.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li is caught in a cycle of anger and guilt during conflicts, yet she is motivated to find ways to communicate her feelings calmly.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li manage her emotional responses and improve communication by using "I" statements and taking moments to pause during conflicts.
