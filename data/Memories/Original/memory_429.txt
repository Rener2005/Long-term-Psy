[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming shows symptoms of anxiety relating to interpersonal conflicts, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels anxious due to high expectations from his mother and teacher, leading to stress and frustration in their interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Ming's struggle with feeling misunderstood and his fear of being perceived as a failure, yet he shows a willingness to express his thoughts more clearly.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Ming articulate his feelings constructively, aiming to reduce conflict and enhance mutual understanding with his mother and teacher.
