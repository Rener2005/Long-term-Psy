# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xu (pseudonym) is a university student who feels lost due to the transition from high school to university life. Previously a central figure in high school, Xu now struggles with feelings of insignificance and confusion about identity. Xu recently began university, experiencing emotional ups and downs while seeking to rediscover personal interests.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xu experiences emotional instability characterized by feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, along with occasional depressive symptoms. No formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xu is overwhelmed and struggles with self-worth, feeling unnoticed and lost in the transition to university life.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xu experiences significant internal conflict due to past accomplishments and current perceived insignificance, yet shows potential for self-insight and a desire to reconnect with past interests.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xu form a balanced self-perception, focusing on rekindling interests as a means to define a new identity. Encourage exploration of self-compassion and examine evidence for negative self-evaluations.
