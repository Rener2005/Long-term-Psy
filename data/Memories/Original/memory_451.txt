# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang (pseudonym) is a single mother facing challenges with social services and seeking to provide a stable environment for her daughter. She has expressed feelings of inadequacy and is navigating motherhood alongside past struggles. Zhang is determined to improve her situation and welcomes support to manage her emotions and responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences significant anxiety related to her role as a mother and potential judgment from external authorities, yet she hasn't reported any formal mental health diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels overwhelmed and helpless due to stress from social services and concern for her daughter's safety.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's dedication to her daughter and willingness to reflect on positive aspects of her parenting despite pervasive anxiety and self-doubt.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang identify and challenge negative beliefs about her capabilities as a mother. Foster self-compassion and develop strategies for managing anxiety related to external pressures.
