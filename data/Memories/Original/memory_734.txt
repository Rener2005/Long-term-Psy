# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Tingxia is a university student working on a thesis, experiencing stress and anxiety due to academic pressures regarding publication and PhD applications. Zhang is motivated to improve study habits and seeks guidance on managing these challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang exhibits symptoms of anxiety and struggles with perfectionism, particularly in academic settings, yet there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels overwhelmed and anxious about the thesis, fearing failure if the work is not perfect.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's tendency towards perfectionism, leading to avoidance behaviors and heightened stress.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang challenge perfectionistic thoughts and establish a more balanced perspective. Develop steps for managing study-related anxiety and creating a supportive routine.
