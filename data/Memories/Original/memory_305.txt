# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei is a professional navigating significant stress from work and personal life. Recently, he has been feeling overwhelmed as he didn't get a promotion, is facing tension with his partner, and is concerned about a friend's serious illness.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences symptoms of anxiety characterized by negative self-talk and fears of inadequacy, especially about his professional performance. He has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels overwhelmed by pressures from multiple areas in his life, resulting in low motivation and feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Zhang Wei frequently engages in negative self-comparison and experiences cycles of self-doubt, yet shows openness to applying self-compassion and introspection.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to aid Zhang Wei in challenging negative thoughts and promote self-kindness. Focus on strategies to manage stress and facilitate social engagement for improved well-being.
