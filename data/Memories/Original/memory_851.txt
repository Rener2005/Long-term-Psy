# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chen is a university student grappling with feelings of aimlessness and disconnection, primarily turning to video games as a means of escape. Chen has recently faced conflicts with roommates due to excessive gaming and missed achieving an academic goal, leading to a loss of direction.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chen struggles with symptoms of anxiety and low motivation but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen feels lost and empty, resorting to video games to escape the harsh reality of unmet expectations and academic setbacks.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Chen's dependency on gaming as a coping mechanism for feelings of inadequacy and avoidance of reality, yet notes Chen’s willingness to explore small, positive changes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to guide Chen in setting modest, achievable goals to regain a sense of direction and gradually replace gaming with constructive activities. Encourage accountability and progress over perfection.
