# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yang (pseudonym) is experiencing emotional difficulties following a recent breakup. She is struggling with feelings of loss and seeking ways to process and express these emotions, particularly around the theme of loneliness.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yang demonstrates symptoms of sadness and possible adjustment-related distress, yet has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yang feels low and overwhelmed by intense sadness since the breakup, often feeling isolated and like a burden.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Yang's deep emotional pain, yet also notes her openness to exploring these feelings and seeking ways to reconnect with others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Guide Yang in identifying and expressing emotions related to her breakup using cognitive behavioral techniques. Encourage gradual social interaction and awareness of supportive relationships.
