# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ma Xiaowu (pseudonym) is a high school student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and self-doubt, particularly in relation to academic achievements and familial expectations. Ma is reflective about personal efforts and is eager to find intrinsic motivation for studying.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ma experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ma feels disappointed and frustrated due to not receiving the "Three Good Student" title, leading to doubts about self-worth and efforts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ma is significantly affected by external validation and struggles with internal motivation, yet is open to exploring personal interests as a source of engagement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ma identify self-worth outside of external expectations and reinforce intrinsic motivations for learning.
