# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ji (pseudonym) is a young student who is navigating recent family changes, particularly the arrival of a new sibling. Ji expresses sensitivity towards familial dynamics and strives for understanding and emotional balance in light of these changes.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ji demonstrates tendencies towards anxiety, particularly related to changes in family attention dynamics, and a desire for familial connection. There is no formal diagnosis disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ji is feeling anxious and a bit lonely due to the recent addition of a new sibling, impacting perceived attention from parents.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Ji's care for familial needs but also a tendency to withdraw, resulting in feelings of anxiety and loneliness. Ji shows willingness to express needs and emotions when encouraged.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to facilitate Ji in expressing feelings and needs to family members. Encourage development of self-expression to reduce feelings of loneliness and anxiety.
