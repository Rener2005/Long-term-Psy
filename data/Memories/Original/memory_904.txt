# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is experiencing interpersonal issues and struggles with feelings of anger, loneliness, and isolation, particularly due to conflicts with classmates. They have expressed difficulty in managing emotions and maintaining relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user appears to be dealing with symptoms associated with social anxiety and emotional regulation challenges, although no formal diagnosis has been provided.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels trapped between being bullied and exhibiting aggressive behavior, leading to frustration and isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that the user acknowledges their behavior patterns but struggles with finding alternative responses and feels a strong survival instinct when faced with perceived attacks.

[Consultation Goals]  
To employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user develop strategies for managing anger and improving interpersonal skills, focusing particularly on self-awareness and emotional regulation.
