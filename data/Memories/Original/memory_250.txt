# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoya is a young individual who reports feelings of being misunderstood and neglected, particularly since the birth of a sibling. She struggles with expressing emotions and occasionally resorts to self-harm. Xiaoya desires improved communication with her mother, seeking recognition and support.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoya experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem stemming from perceived familial neglect, but there is no formal diagnosis reported for a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoya feels invisible and experiences significant frustration and anger due to perceived neglect from her mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoya’s intense emotions related to familial changes and recognizes her struggle with expressing feelings constructively.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to explore healthier ways for Xiaoya to express emotions and improve her communication with her mother.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Consultation Goals]

# Third Consultation
