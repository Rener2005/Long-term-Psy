# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Hua is a professional dealing with intense anxiety related to work deadlines and performance. They describe a tendency towards perfectionism and self-doubt, exacerbated by fears of judgment from colleagues and supervisors.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Hua exhibits signs of anxiety and perfectionism but no formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Hua feels anxious about upcoming work deadlines, accompanied by physical sensations of tightness in the chest and an overwhelming fear of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li Hua's tendency towards perfectionism, which exacerbates anxiety, yet Li Hua demonstrates a willingness to consider more self-compassionate perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Hua challenge perfectionist thoughts and develop a more compassionate and realistic self-view. Encourage setting achievable daily goals to manage work-related stress.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Hua continues to feel anxious about their project deadline and wrestles with fear of failure and perceived judgment from colleagues.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that while Li Hua has attempted stress management strategies, there is a need to further shift focus from past mistakes to current capabilities.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li Hua in recognizing and celebrating past successes to foster a balanced perspective. Encourage open communication with colleagues to alleviate project-related stress and anxiety.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Hua is managing work stress better but continues to doubt their abilities and feels pressure to meet expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes improved awareness of cognitive distortions in Li Hua's thinking patterns, and a readiness to apply self-kindness and positive reinforcement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Empower Li Hua to document achievements and positive feedback regularly, and to use positive affirmations to bolster self-confidence. Promote consistent reflection as a means of tracking emotional evolution and celebrating small accomplishments.
