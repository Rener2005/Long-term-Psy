# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a university student dealing with insecurities primarily related to appearance, affecting job prospects and social situations. Recently, Li has started reflecting on personal experiences and seeks self-worth beyond external perceptions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li exhibits signs of low self-esteem and anxiety, particularly surrounding social interactions and personal appearance, without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels insecure and anxious about appearance, particularly in job interview scenarios, with feelings of worthlessness and depressive thoughts.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a strong impact of negative self-perceptions on Li's self-esteem and willingness to explore positive self-affirmation to counter these beliefs.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li identify and counter negative self-talk, building self-esteem through engagement in meaningful activities like volunteering.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li experiences embarrassment and anxiety due to classmates’ jokes, contributing to self-doubt and perceived inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li's awareness of internal strengths but difficulty integrating these positive aspects into self-perception during social situations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Li to list personal achievements and strengthen social connections with supportive classmates using positive reinforcement and gradual exposure to social settings.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious about social interactions, questioning personal interest and acceptance when attempting to engage with classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
Li's anxiety appears connected to predicted negative judgments, although there’s willingness to change interaction strategies and increased self-awareness.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li in practicing engagement techniques through role-playing scenarios and prepare conversation topics to reduce anxiety in social settings.

# Fourth Consultation

# Sixth Consultation
