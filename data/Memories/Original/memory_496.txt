# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is an individual who previously faced job loss and is now navigating the complexities of searching for new employment opportunities. Li Ming experiences feelings of anxiety and self-doubt related to professional worth and is eager to rebuild confidence and establish a supportive network.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming exhibits symptoms of anxiety connected to career transitions and self-worth doubts but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels anxious and down, struggling with self-doubt and professional worth after a recent layoff.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming is hindered by anxiety and a fear of judgment, yet demonstrates a willingness to reframe negative thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming challenge negative thoughts about worth and reach out to supportive networks, reinforcing self-esteem and proactive job search behaviors.
