# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is an academic student experiencing anxiety and self-doubt, particularly related to exams and academic performance. They recently faced a disappointing quiz result, which triggered feelings of inadequacy and trouble focusing.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and anxious due to the recent poor quiz performance, leading to doubts about their abilities and difficulties sustaining concentration.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes the user's tendency for negative self-talk and highlights their focus on a singular failure rather than past successes, yet detects a readiness to alter these perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to assist the user in challenging negative thoughts and focusing on previous academic successes. Introduce mindfulness practices to enhance focus and manage anxiety, particularly before study sessions.
