# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Lan (pseudonym) is a university student who experiences anxiety and low self-esteem, particularly in social settings. She is eager to overcome her social anxiety and improve connections with her peers. Zhang Lan is reflective and open to trying new strategies to manage her anxiety better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Lan exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem. No formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Lan feels overwhelmed by social anxiety, viewing herself as a failure and experiencing difficulty in social interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes Zhang Lan's intense negative self-perception and anxiety during social interactions, but she shows readiness to challenge these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral strategies to help Zhang Lan challenge intrusive negative thoughts and encourage small social interactions, building her confidence gradually.
