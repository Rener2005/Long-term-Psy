# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Huang is a university student experiencing emotional challenges, particularly in relation to familial connections and academic pressure. They have recently engaged in reflecting on interpersonal relationships and aim to strengthen communication, especially with their mother.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Huang shows symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt, primarily tied to academic performance and familial interactions, but there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Huang feels anxious about exams and inadequate due to perceived parental indifference, impacting their academic focus.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes persistent anxiety and self-critical thoughts, though Xiao Huang demonstrates openness to cognitive reframing and new coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive-behavioral methods to help Xiao Huang shift focus from external validation to internal self-worth, fostering effective academic study habits and reducing anxiety.
