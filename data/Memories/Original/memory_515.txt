# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaohao (pseudonym) is a student dealing with feelings of anxiety and emotional instability, particularly concerning school performance and interpersonal relationships. Xiaohao lives with his grandmother and cousin, while his parents are often absent due to work. He expresses a desire to manage his emotions independently without parental involvement.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaohao experiences symptoms consistent with anxiety and emotional upheaval but does not mention any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaohao feels overwhelmed, frequently experiences intense emotional lows, and struggles with feelings of isolation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaohao faces challenges with emotional regulation and experiences a strong sense of solitude due to a lack of familial support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaohao identify and challenge negative thought patterns. Develop tools for emotional regulation to foster a sense of control, focusing on independence in managing feelings.
