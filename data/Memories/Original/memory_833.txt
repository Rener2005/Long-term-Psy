# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Xiao is a university student who has recently experienced the loss of a father. The bereavement has left Zhang feeling isolated, overwhelmed by guilt, and questioning self-worth. Zhang is attempting to cope with these emotions while balancing academic responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Xiao is experiencing symptoms consistent with grief, anxiety, and low self-esteem but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Xiao feels overwhelmed by sadness and a strong sense of guilt about the inability to prevent the father's death, contributing to a profound sense of loneliness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang constantly grapples with guilt and self-blame but demonstrates an openness to exploring these feelings and seeking alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Xiao challenge self-blaming thoughts and develop strategies for coping with grief. Begin implementing personal coping strategies like journaling and meditation to manage emotional distress.
