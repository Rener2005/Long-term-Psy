## User Background

Jessica (pseudonym) is a woman recently diagnosed with breast cancer, leading to significant life changes, including breaking off an engagement. She feels overwhelmed and finds herself isolated, often reluctant to share her feelings with others due to a fear of burdening them.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Jessica experiences anxiety and depression following her cancer diagnosis, contributing to feelings of sadness and loss. There is no mention of any formal mental health disorder diagnosis beyond her recent cancer diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jessica feels low and overwhelmed by her cancer diagnosis and struggles to navigate her emotions following her breakup.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Jessica is coping with tremendous emotional weight and is hesitant to share her feelings due to fear of burdening others. She seems open to exploring ways to express herself and seek support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Jessica identify and express her emotions healthily, encouraging gradual sharing of feelings for mental relief.
