# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Li is an adult female who experiences agoraphobia, causing significant anxiety about leaving her home and using public transportation. Recently, she has been working on facing these fears by attempting small outings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Li presents symptoms consistent with agoraphobia, marked by anxiety and avoidance of crowded places and public transport. No formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Li feels anxious about going out and doubts her ability to overcome agoraphobia, despite recent progress.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ms. Li demonstrates resilience and a willingness to challenge her fears through gradual exposure but struggles with self-doubt and guilt.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ms. Li manage her anxiety and challenge negative thoughts. Develop a gradual exposure plan to systematically increase her comfort with public transportation.
