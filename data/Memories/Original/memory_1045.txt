# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is facing intense emotions of anger triggered by criticism and ridicule, often resulting in physical reactions. They express a strong desire to change their response patterns and manage their anger more effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user displays symptoms of difficulty managing anger, often due to perceived disrespect or injustice, but has not been formally diagnosed with a specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels challenged by recent incidents where they reacted physically to criticism and ridicule, resulting in frustration and regret.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's heightened sensitivity to criticism and a tendency towards impulsive responses, but also a willingness to explore alternative, non-aggressive reactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help the user identify anger triggers and practice different coping mechanisms, such as deep breathing and physical activity, to manage emotions constructively.
