# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Abo (pseudonym) is a married man facing challenges with his wife, named Abi, and financial difficulties due to gambling losses. He desires better communication in his marriage and aims to regain stability for his family.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Abo experiences feelings of frustration, anger, and guilt, contributing to relationship strain and financial stress. He has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Abo feels frustrated and angry, perceiving isolation and misunderstanding in his marriage, compounded by financial and business challenges.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Abo's inclination to blame his wife for relationship troubles, yet recognizes his openness to consider new communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Abo recognize and articulate his feelings without blame, fostering a more empathetic view toward his wife and reducing marital tension.
