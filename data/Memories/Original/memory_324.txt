# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is an individual experiencing stress and feelings of inadequacy related to professional challenges, compounded by a sense of isolation from friends. They are troubled by doubts about their capabilities, and recent events have intensified these feelings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-confidence, though no formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed by work stress and doubts their self-worth, leading to isolation and avoidance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes the user's pattern of self-doubt under stress but notes a willingness to explore strategies for improvement, including discussing and possibly applying past successful coping mechanisms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user recognize cognitive distortions and develop strategies to manage stress, including breaking tasks into smaller steps and seeking assistance.
