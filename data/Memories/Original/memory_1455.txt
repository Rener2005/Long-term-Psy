# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Xuan (pseudonym) is a student dealing with feelings of anger and rejection, primarily directed towards family dynamics with a strict stepmother. Xiao Xuan experiences frustration and isolation, both at home and in social settings, and is seeking methods to better manage emotional upheavals.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Xuan exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, manifesting as physical tension and self-doubt, without a formal mental health diagnosis yet.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Xuan is grappling with feelings of anger and loneliness following a conflict with a classmate and ongoing domestic tension with a stepmother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Xuan's tendency to internalize conflicts and self-criticize, but recognizes a willingness to explore strategies for emotional management.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Xiao Xuan in identifying and challenging negative self-perceptions and developing healthier ways to process and express emotions.
