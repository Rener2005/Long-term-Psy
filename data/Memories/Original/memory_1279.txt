[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Baoyou is experiencing symptoms of anxiety linked to self-doubt and family expectations but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Baoyou is struggling with self-confidence issues at work, feeling anxious and stressed, often comparing themselves to colleagues.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Baoyou is caught in cycles of negative self-comparison, yet shows readiness to adopt new perspectives and strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ CBT techniques to help Baoyou challenge negative thoughts, focusing on successes and developing strategies to build self-confidence and manage work-related anxiety.
