[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaofan exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem. She struggles with negative self-perceptions, though she has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaofan's self-blame is impacting her self-esteem and that she shows insight into her family's dynamics, but she's caught in a cycle of guilt and frustration.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaofan recognize and challenge her negative thoughts about familial responsibility. Develop coping strategies to manage stress and emotional well-being, focusing on creating personal emotional space.
