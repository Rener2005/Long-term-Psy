# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang Qiang is a student who struggles with managing academic responsibilities and often turns to gaming as a means of escapism. He has recently been facing challenges with focusing on studies, leading to anxiety about potential academic failure.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang Qiang displays symptoms of avoidance behavior related to anxiety, particularly centered around academic stress. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang Qiang feels overwhelmed and trapped in a cycle of avoidance, using gaming to escape his academic pressures, leading to a sense of hopelessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Wang Qiang's pattern of avoidance and notes his willingness to consider small, incremental changes to curtail this cycle, despite deep-seated fears of academic failure.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang Qiang challenge his avoidance behaviors. Set small, achievable study goals to reduce academic anxiety and create a positive study routine linked to rewarding activities.
