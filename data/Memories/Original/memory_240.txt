# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoke is a university student who recently transitioned from humanities to science, feeling inadequate and isolated among new classmates. Xiaoke struggles with communication within this new social context and desires to find a sense of belonging and acceptance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoke experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, though no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoke feels isolated and excluded from the new class environment, experiencing sadness and anger.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoke's strong feelings of invisibility and self-doubt regarding social connections but observes a potential to utilize self-compassion as a tool for cognitive reframing.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoke challenge negative beliefs about social interactions and explore small steps to engage with classmates. Implement relaxation techniques like deep breathing to manage anxiety.
