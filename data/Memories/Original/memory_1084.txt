[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang experiences symptoms of anxiety, particularly linked to academic stress, but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels overwhelmed and anxious about studying, particularly facing nervousness and self-doubt regarding math and physics.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Wang struggles with avoidance due to anxiety but displays a willingness to adopt structured strategies for improvement.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies by breaking down study tasks into manageable parts and incorporating relaxation exercises to ease anxiety.
