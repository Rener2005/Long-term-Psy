# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chen is a university student dealing with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, predominantly linked to academic performance and recently a breakup. He often escapes into video games to avoid these emotions and is seeking ways to regain control and manage them effectively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chen experiences symptoms of anxiety and uses maladaptive coping mechanisms such as excessive gaming to escape feelings of stress and inadequacy. No formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen feels overwhelmed and anxious about his academic performance, often using video games as an escape but recognizes the negative impact on his life.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Chen struggles with avoidance behaviors, specifically through gaming, yet shows willingness to explore healthier coping mechanisms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Chen identify triggers for his gaming and develop strategies for balancing academic responsibilities with leisure activities to reduce stress.
