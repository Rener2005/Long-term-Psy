# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Jiang (pseudonym) is a student adjusting to university life, feeling excited yet lonely and confused about forming new relationships. She is navigating her feelings about a changing friendship with a peer from her hometown.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Jiang exhibits signs of social anxiety and insecurity, particularly related to new social environments, but does not have a formal diagnosis of a mental health condition.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiang feels lonely and insecure about her relationship with a friend, fearing the loss of a vital connection.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Jiang's tendency to jump to conclusions about relationships but also notes her openness to considering alternative, more positive interpretations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge negative assumptions in Jiang's relationship, encourage self-compassion, and reduce anxiety by improving her interpersonal communication skills.
