[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang exhibits symptoms of anxiety and panic attacks, especially related to workplace situations and social engagements but has not reported any formal diagnosis of mental health disorders.

[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
