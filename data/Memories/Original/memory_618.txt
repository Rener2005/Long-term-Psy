## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Long (pseudonym) is an adult male who is addressing past substance use and its emotional impacts. He is working towards rebuilding his life with a focus on personal growth, career development, and improving family relationships.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Long is experiencing anxiety related to possible relapse and doubts about his personal strength. He expresses a need for stability in his recovery journey but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Long feels overwhelmed by his past interactions and experiences strong anxiety about relapse, leading to a sense of hopelessness about his future.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices Long's fear of relapse and struggle with self-doubt, highlighting his awareness and willingness to explore more constructive coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help Long reframe negative thoughts and recognize personal victories. Encourage daily goals to support sobriety and recommend reconnecting with supportive groups for accountability.
