# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Liu (pseudonym) is a university student struggling with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and social pressures. A recent breakup and academic setback have compounded these feelings, leading to self-doubt and social withdrawal.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Liu experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Liu feels sad and lost following a breakup, compounded by academic failure, leading to feelings of pointlessness and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Liu is struggling with heavy negative self-talk and a pervasive sense of inadequacy but exhibits a willingness to consider new perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Liu challenge negative thoughts and reframe setbacks as opportunities for growth. Develop actionable steps to manage academic pressures and encourage seeking support.
