# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Lu is a school-aged student who enjoys social interactions, particularly having fun with friends during class. Xiao Lu is oriented towards seeking enjoyment but has encountered issues with classroom behavior as teachers have mentioned being disruptive.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Lu exhibits challenges with behavior regulation in a classroom setting, potentially influenced by boredom and a need for social interaction. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Lu feels misunderstood and frustrated by teacher feedback regarding behavior, experiencing anger and a desire for more enjoyment in class.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Lu's inclination towards social engagement and playfulness, with a possible lack of awareness of the impact on others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to guide Xiao Lu in recognizing the effects of behavior on classmates and encourage finding alternative ways to express humor and socialize, while respecting the classroom environment.
