# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Li (pseudonym) is a mother who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety about her parental role, particularly concerning her teenage daughter. She is seeking to improve communication and establish a more supportive relationship with her daughter.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Li experiences symptoms of anxiety related to parenting and low self-confidence in her role as a mother. No formal mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Li feels anxious and stressed due to her daughter's late returns, which exacerbate her fears of inadequacy as a mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ms. Li is caught in a cycle of stress, exacerbated by self-blame and the pressure she places on herself concerning her daughter's behavior.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ms. Li challenge her negative thoughts and develop a more balanced perspective on her role as a mother. Support her in finding effective communication strategies with her daughter.
