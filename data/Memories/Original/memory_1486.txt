# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Y (pseudonym) is a student experiencing feelings of anxiety and loneliness due to transitioning into a new class. Xiao Y feels pressure to meet high expectations, notably from their mother, which contributes to their sense of inadequacy and struggle to connect with classmates.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Y exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, which are heightened during social interactions and academic settings. No formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Y feels lonely and anxious due to difficulties in fitting in with new classmates and the pressure to perform academically.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Y struggles with self-doubt and fear of social rejection but is open to exploring and challenging these negative beliefs with support.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Y identify and challenge negative thought patterns, particularly in social situations, and build self-compassion to manage anxiety.
