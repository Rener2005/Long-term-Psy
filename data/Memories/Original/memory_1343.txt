# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yú (pseudonym) is a student who experiences intense anxiety related to cleanliness, which significantly impacts daily activities and academic performance. Yú has recently been focusing on handling shared lab equipment at school, which has amplified existing anxieties around contamination.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yú displays symptoms characteristic of an anxiety disorder, particularly obsessive-compulsive tendencies related to cleanliness, though no formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yú feels anxious and overwhelmed by the thought of using shared lab equipment due to concerns about contamination and getting sick.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Yú's anxiety revolves around a need to control cleanliness, which affects concentration and causes distress, though Yú shows openness to addressing these fears.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yú reframe negative thoughts about contamination and develop balanced perspectives, while managing anxiety around shared equipment through gradual exposure and positive self-talk.
