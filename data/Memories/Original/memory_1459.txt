# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoxi is a new student who is finding it difficult to adjust to her new school environment. She struggles with feelings of loneliness and inadequacy, particularly in social interactions and academic performance. Xiaoxi is eager to build her confidence and establish connections in her new setting.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxi experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem, though there is no formal mental health diagnosis mentioned.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxi feels lost and frustrated with her inability to fit in and focus on her studies, which has led to declining grades and a sense of not belonging.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoxi's tendency to engage in negative self-comparisons, especially in social settings, but also observes her potential for self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoxi challenge negative self-perceptions in social situations, encouraging her to practice speaking up in class to build confidence.

# Second Consultation
