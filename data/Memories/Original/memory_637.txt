# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
C is a university student studying maritime studies. They have expressed feelings of anxiety and uncertainty about their future, particularly concerning their major and prospective career at sea. C is reflective about their preferences and eager to find a path that aligns better with their interests and values.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
C experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt related to their academic and career choices, with no formal diagnosis disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
C feels overwhelmed and disconnected from their studies, with anxiety stemming from uncertainty about a career at sea.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices C's significant reluctance towards a maritime career due to fear and a lack of interest, yet C shows willingness to explore alternative paths.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help C challenge their perceived inadequacies and explore career options that align with their interests. Begin fostering strategies for managing anxiety related to academic and career decisions.
