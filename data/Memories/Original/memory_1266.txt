# First Consultation

## User Background

Lin Xiang (pseudonym) is a high school student struggling with feelings of frustration and alienation, particularly related to school and interpersonal relationships. Lin is sensitive to social injustices and is navigating through challenging interactions both at school and at home, seeking ways to express emotions constructively.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lin Xiang experiences heightened emotions and reactions linked to feelings of isolation and misunderstanding but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lin Xiang feels misunderstood and frustrated, particularly after distressing events, and struggles with reacting defensively to perceived injustices.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Lin Xiang experiencing strong emotions tied to a sense of injustice and a need for validation, indicating difficulty in managing emotional responses but openness to exploring more constructive expression.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Lin Xiang recognize triggers of anger and practice calm communication. Aim to develop strategies to foster positive social interactions and manage feelings of isolation.
