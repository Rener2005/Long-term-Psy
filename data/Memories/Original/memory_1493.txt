# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a school-aged youngster struggling with expressions of anger, feelings of confusion, and fears of not being understood by peers and authority figures. Recent incidents include breaking a classmate's belongings and experiencing anxiety about being judged.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences emotional regulation difficulties, particularly with anger and fear of social judgment, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels confused and struggles with uncontrollable anger, especially in school settings, leading to troubles with peers and subsequent parental disapproval.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user has difficulty managing responses to perceived disrespect or exclusion, yet shows openness to exploring new strategies for emotional regulation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user identify triggers of anger and develop practical strategies for expressing emotions constructively, such as deep breathing and engaging in calming activities.
