# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Li is a university student who often feels isolated and struggles with self-doubt, especially following social setbacks or familial disagreements. Xiao Li is eager to enhance personal communication and relationship skills to manage these emotions better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, though no formal mental health disorder diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels overwhelmed by social rejection and parental disappointment, dealing with a strong sense of isolation and self-doubt following a school incident.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li’s attribution of negative social interactions to personal inadequacies, yet notes a readiness to explore alternative explanations and solutions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply CBT techniques to help Xiao Li reconsider negative interpretations of peer and parental interactions. Encourage open communication and self-compassion to ease feelings of isolation and self-doubt.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels burdened by familial disappointment and academic pressures, leading to pervasive guilt and shame.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li's struggle with feeling responsible for the emotional states of family members, perpetuating feelings of guilt and helplessness.

[Consultation Goals]  
Guide Xiao Li in challenging the belief of personal responsibility for others’ happiness, promoting self-compassion, and fostering assertive communication strategies within the family context.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels more confident in forming new friendships and engaging in volunteering, yet still experiences moments of loneliness and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes increased awareness of personal capabilities but persisting apprehension around others' perceptions and judgments.

[Consultation Goals]  
Facilitate recognition and celebration of personal achievements to bolster self-esteem, using CBT strategies to manage negative thought patterns during social interactions.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li is beginning to see the positive impact of teamwork and collaboration on personal confidence, exemplified by success in school activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li’s growing confidence in collaborative settings and increased willingness to involve family in personal successes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Xiao Li to expand family involvement in personal interests to strengthen relational bonds, employing positive reflections to enhance self-confidence and relationship dynamics.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels more self-assured following successful team engagement in school projects; considering new leadership roles and facing academic pressures.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Li’s readiness to take on leadership roles as confidence grows, albeit with residual concerns about meeting familial and educational expectations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Xiao Li in setting realistic, achievable goals for developing leadership and communication skills, while using time management and stress reduction techniques to balance responsibilities.
