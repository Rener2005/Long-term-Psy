# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoqing is a student who faces pressure from familial expectations, particularly from her mother. She struggles with balancing academic demands and her personal interests, like art. Recent experiences include feelings of being overshadowed by her sibling and efforts to communicate effectively with her mother.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoqing experiences anxiety related to academic performance and family dynamics. There is no formal diagnosis, but she exhibits signs of stress and self-doubt.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoqing feels frustrated and suffocated by her mother's expectations, particularly in relation to her dropping science grades and comparisons to her sister.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoqing's struggle with feeling unheard by her mother and misunderstood, showing a desire to be acknowledged for her efforts rather than just results.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to empower Xiaoqing to express her feelings calmly and assertively to her mother. Encourage the use of specific examples in communication to enhance understanding and appreciation.
