# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
X Grandma (pseudonym) is a widowed elderly woman who experiences feelings of guilt and loneliness following the passing of her husband. She is reflective about her past and expresses a desire to reconnect with others despite anxiety about social interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
X Grandma is dealing with symptoms of grief and social anxiety. She has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
X Grandma is overwhelmed by grief and guilt over her husband's passing, struggling with self-blame and the impact of her perceived shortcomings.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that X Grandma wrestles with a heavy burden of self-guilt and struggles to extend the self-compassion she can easily offer others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help X Grandma recognize and challenge her self-critical thoughts, fostering self-forgiveness and exploring strategies to alleviate grief and guilt.

# Second Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
