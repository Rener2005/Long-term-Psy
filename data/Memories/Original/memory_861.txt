# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang (pseudonym) is a new mother who feels overwhelmed by the responsibilities of parenthood and the lack of familial support. She struggles with feelings of guilt related to her in-laws' perceived expectations and their minimal involvement postpartum. Wang is actively seeking ways to manage these feelings and improve her emotional well-being.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang exhibits signs of postpartum adjustment difficulties, experiencing heightened anxiety and self-doubt, particularly in relation to her performance as a mother. No formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wang's self-critical thoughts may be intensified by assumed familial expectations, yet she demonstrates a readiness to explore and reframe these perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Wang challenge her negative thought patterns by using cognitive behavioral techniques to develop a more balanced self-view and mitigate family-related anxiety.
