# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
John (pseudonym) is an adult dealing with feelings of guilt and self-blame following a recent breakup. He is reflecting on patterns within his past relationships and seeks to enhance his communication skills and emotional awareness to foster healthier connections.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
John is experiencing symptoms of shame and self-criticism post-breakup but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
John feels guilty and confused about his recent relationship ending, burdened by self-blame and a belief that he is incapable of nurturing healthy partnerships.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes John's self-awareness and regret, particularly regarding neglecting partner needs, while also noting his willingness to view the situation as a learning experience.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help John challenge self-blaming beliefs and develop actionable strategies to improve communication and attentiveness in relationships.
