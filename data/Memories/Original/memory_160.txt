# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Yuan is a high school student who feels overwhelmed by the transition from middle school to high school, experiencing a drop in academic performance and pressure to meet parental expectations. Xiao Yuan expresses anxiety about not living up to perceived standards and a strong desire to balance academic duties with personal interests.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Yuan exhibits symptoms of anxiety related to performance and self-esteem but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yuan feels lost and under pressure due to slipping grades and perceived parental disappointment, leading to anxiety about failing to meet expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Yuan's internalization of parental expectations and a reflective awareness about personal fears and assumptions influencing perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Yuan reframe negative perceptions of parental expectations and develop a realistic study plan that balances academics and personal interests.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yuan feels overwhelmed and inadequate due to the pressure of meeting parental expectations and the introduction of a tutor, questioning self-abilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiao Yuan is caught between self-belief and the perceived need to meet external standards, with a tendency to project internal fears onto parental attitudes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Xiao Yuan to challenge skewed perceptions of parental disappointment, set manageable academic goals, and adopt a self-compassionate perspective to reduce anxiety.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yuan feels uncertain about future goals while finding joy and confidence through new friendships and club participation.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes a positive response to engaging in interests, with a struggle to align personal ambitions with parental expectations for academic success.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Xiao Yuan in setting specific goals that integrate creative interests and academic aspirations, facilitating open communication with parents about artistic passions.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yuan feels pressured by parental concerns over balancing club activities with academics, but the club brings significant personal satisfaction.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist discerns a conflict between Xiao Yuan's pursuit of personal fulfillment and parental expectations, with a need for effective communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Empower Xiao Yuan to express feelings to parents using "I messages," linking club activities to personal growth and academic motivation.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Yuan feels more confident after participating in a stage play, with increased interest in integrating creative pursuits into academic life.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist perceives a positive shift towards self-awareness and motivation through combining interests with academic goals.

[Consultation Goals]
