# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, a university student, struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, especially in public speaking and social situations. The user tends to compare themselves unfavorably with peers and is seeking ways to manage emotional responses and build confidence.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and overwhelmed by self-doubt, particularly when speaking in front of others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency towards negative self-comparisons and a cycle of doubt but sees willingness to explore self-compassionate perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge negative thoughts and foster self-compassion. Establish small, achievable goals for speaking in class to reduce anxiety.
