# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaomo is a young student experiencing significant emotional distress due to family changes and academic challenges. Recently, Xiaomo’s family situation has been unstable, with parental separation and a mother who is frequently absent due to work commitments. Xiaomo finds it difficult to express and manage emotions appropriately.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaomo exhibits signs of anxiety and emotional dysregulation, but no formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaomo feels anxious and upset due to family turmoil and related behavioral issues at school, such as acting out in class.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Xiaomo struggles with managing intense emotions, especially in response to situations at school and home, indicating a need for emotional regulation strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize CBT techniques to help Xiaomo identify triggers and replace negative thought patterns with compassionate self-talk. Focus on emotional recognition and expression, particularly in family and social contexts.
