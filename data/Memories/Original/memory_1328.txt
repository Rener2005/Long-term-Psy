# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyun is a university student feeling anxious about imminent graduation and the pressure of securing a job or internship. She expresses a fear of interacting with strangers and being deceived, rooted in past experiences where she felt a strong distrust towards others, having grown up with her grandmother with absent parents.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyun experiences symptoms of social anxiety, exacerbated by feelings of inadequacy. She has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyun feels anxious and overwhelmed by the uncertainty of graduation and the challenges of job hunting, coupled with a fear of social interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoyun's deep-seated distrust of others and a protective barrier that hinders forming connections, yet she shows openness to addressing these issues.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyun challenge negative thoughts surrounding her abilities, fostering small social interactions to build confidence and reduce social anxiety.
