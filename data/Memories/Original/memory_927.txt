# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is a university student facing significant academic pressure, specifically concerning upcoming graduate exams. Li Ming reports increased compulsive behaviors leading to difficulties in concentrating on studies.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming exhibits symptoms aligned with anxiety, exacerbated by compulsive behaviors linked to fears of imperfection and potential failure, though no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder is reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels overwhelmed and frustrated due to intensifying compulsive behaviors affecting concentration on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming struggles with perfectionism and anxiety, but shows readiness to explore strategies for managing these feelings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming challenge perfectionistic thoughts and develop manageable study goals, addressing compulsive behaviors by implementing structured strategies and cognitive reframing.
