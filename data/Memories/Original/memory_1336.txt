# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhou is a high school student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy, anxiety, and self-doubt, particularly in academic and social environments. Zhou recently transitioned to high school and is trying to adapt to the new environment while managing these strong feelings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhou experiences symptoms consistent with anxiety and low self-esteem. There has been no disclosure of a formal mental health disorder diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhou feels overwhelmed and worthless due to academic pressures and persistent negative self-comparisons with classmates.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices that Zhou is trapped in a cycle of negative thinking but is capable of self-reflection and willing to consider different perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Zhou's negative thinking patterns and encourage recognition of personal successes to alleviate feelings of inadequacy.
