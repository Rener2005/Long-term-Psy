# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Lilian is a high school student experiencing significant anxiety concerning academic performance, particularly tied to exams. She struggles with a sense of overwhelming pressure from both internal expectations and external influences, such as parental pressure.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lilian exhibits symptoms consistent with anxiety disorders, including panic responses and negative thought cycles, but no formal diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lilian feels extremely anxious and overwhelmed with upcoming exams, leading to difficulties in concentration and physical symptoms of anxiety.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Lilian's heightened anxiety and tendency to catastrophize academic outcomes, but she shows openness to learning new relaxation techniques and cognitive restructuring.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help Lilian manage anxiety by identifying and challenging negative thought patterns, paired with relaxation techniques to ease physical symptoms.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lilian continues to experience persistent anxiety, particularly triggered by exam-related stimuli, creating a cycle of worry about failure and disappointing her parents.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Lilian's stuck patterns in negative thoughts but also her emerging awareness and willingness to adopt balanced thinking and study practices.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Lilian in fostering adaptive thinking by using reality checks and self-compassion to mitigate anxiety. Promote incremental exposure to study environments with breaks.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lilian is struggling with concentration issues and a resurgence of anxiety, driven by recent performance in mock exams and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Lilian's unrealistic fear of failure but acknowledges her identification of past successes as a resource for resilience.

[Consultation Goals]  
Empower Lilian to reconstruct her study routines with achievable targets and utilize stress management techniques like deep breathing, enhancing focus and reducing exam-related anxiety.
