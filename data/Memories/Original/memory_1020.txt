# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Abo is a married individual who is struggling with relationship conflicts, particularly concerning financial issues and communication difficulties with his wife. Recently, Abo has been dealing with heightened frustration and anger due to a lack of perceived support from his partner, impacting his emotions and family dynamics.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Abo experiences feelings of anger and helplessness linked to financial and relational stressors. He has not mentioned a formal diagnosis of any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Abo feels frustrated and overwhelmed by the lack of effective communication with his wife, especially regarding financial disagreements, which leads to anger and a sense of helplessness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Abo's frustration stems from a desire for understanding and support, yet each partner feels defensive, creating barriers to effective communication.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Abo express his emotions constructively and develop proactive communication strategies. Aim to break cyclical patterns of argument and foster a supportive dialogue with his wife.
