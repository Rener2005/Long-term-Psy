[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang exhibits symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not received a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang challenge and redirect negative perceptions, encouraging small steps towards re-engagement in class and social interactions to counteract feelings of inadequacy.
