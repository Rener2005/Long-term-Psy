# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Fang is a young adult grappling with feelings of inadequacy, primarily after a recent breakup. She often questions her self-worth and battles with negative self-perceptions from unresolved emotional wounds.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Fang appears to show signs of low self-esteem and depressive thoughts but has not been formally diagnosed with any specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Fang feels overwhelmed and deeply saddened by her recent breakup, expressing feelings of abandonment and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant noticed Fang's tendency to internalize feelings of inadequacy and self-blame, though there is a readiness to reflect on and possibly challenge these beliefs.

[Consultation Goals]  
Begin using cognitive behavioral techniques to help Fang recognize and challenge negative self-perceptions. Establish initial steps toward healing and improving self-compassion.
