# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Lu is a college student navigating feelings of loss and confusion following a recent breakup, along with academic pressures. There is a noticeable inner conflict between parental expectations and personal aspirations.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lu experiences symptoms of anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and personal relationships, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lu feels lost and overwhelmed due to the pressure of managing parental expectations while dealing with academic stress and heartbreak.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant notes Lu's struggle with self-critical thoughts and a heightened sense of inadequacy, yet identifies an underlying resilience and a desire to achieve personal clarity.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Lu reframe negative thoughts, focusing on self-compassion and personal goal setting. Encourage the development of actionable plans to manage academic and personal stress effectively.
