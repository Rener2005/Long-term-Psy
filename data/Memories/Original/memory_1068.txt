# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is struggling with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety after not being retained by their university due to appearance-based judgments. They are concerned about their self-worth and future prospects and feel pressured by family sacrifices for their education.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem. There is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and hopeless, battling racing thoughts and questioning their self-worth and future success.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency towards negative self-evaluation and sees potential for building self-forgiveness and exploring constructive coping mechanisms.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge negative self-beliefs and generate practical steps for personal growth and stress reduction.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory

[Consultation Goals]  
Support the user in identifying and challenging negative beliefs using evidence-based thought assessments. Encourage small, achievable goals to alleviate job search anxiety.
