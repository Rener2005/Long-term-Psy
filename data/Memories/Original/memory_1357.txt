# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Li, now a principal, recently transitioned into this role. The user is adjusting to various challenges, such as infrastructure, staffing, and salary reforms, and is navigating feelings related to leadership responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt due to the pressures of the new role but has no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Li feels overwhelmed and anxious about the new responsibilities as a principal, doubting their capability to handle them effectively.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Li's struggle with self-doubt and the tendency to self-compare, yet Xiao Li is open to exploring supportive strategies and compassionate self-talk.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiao Li manage stress by breaking down tasks, promoting self-compassion, and exploring realistic perspectives on the role transition.
