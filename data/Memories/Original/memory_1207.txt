[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
E experiences symptoms consistent with anxiety and low self-esteem, especially evident in social situations, without any disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive-behavioral strategies to help E challenge negative self-comparisons and increase self-acceptance. Focus on fostering self-confidence and diminishing anxiety in social situations.
