# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a university student who experiences feelings of anxiety and insecurity in social situations, particularly after a classroom incident where the user was unable to answer a question, leading to embarrassment due to classmates' reactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li has expressed symptoms of social anxiety, primarily manifesting as fear of judgment and negative self-perceptions in academic and social settings. No formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels anxious and insecure, especially in social settings, and experiences embarrassment and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li's negative self-judgments and avoidance behavior but notes potential for improvement by embracing a more compassionate and balanced self-view.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Li's negative thoughts and build self-confidence. Encourage Li to practice and apply new strategies in a specific upcoming social situation, such as a class presentation.
