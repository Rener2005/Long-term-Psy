# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is a professional experiencing anxiety due to project delays at work, feeling pressure and a fear of letting others down. Li Ming is determined to manage stress better and improve communication skills at work.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming shows symptoms of anxiety and stress related to professional responsibilities but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming is feeling anxious and overwhelmed with work projects, particularly concerned about failing the team due to project delays.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Li Ming struggles with self-critical thoughts and an intense fear of negative judgment from colleagues, but shows awareness of the need for a more balanced self-view.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming identify and challenge negative thoughts, with a focus on cultivating balanced self-perceptions and stress management strategies.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming is experiencing uncertainty and a lack of confidence in communication, leading to anxiety and frustration, particularly in work situations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Li Ming’s heightened concern about how colleagues may perceive communication attempts, yet there’s an openness to exploring ways to improve these interactions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Li Ming in reframing anxious thoughts and fostering effective communication techniques to reduce anxiety and improve teamwork.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels relieved and encouraged by positive colleague feedback, aiming to maintain improved emotional management and communication at work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li Ming’s growing confidence in communication skills and an increased sense of being valued within the team, showing readiness to further enhance these capabilities.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Li Ming in establishing consistent time management practices and maintaining positive communication habits, using CBT to reinforce new strategies and mitigate stress.
