# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a professional navigating anxiety related to work pressures and deadlines. They experience sleep difficulties and feelings of inadequacy concerning managing workload and projects.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms consistent with anxiety, particularly in response to time management and perceived performance failures, but there's no formal mental health diagnosis disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels overwhelmed and anxious due to an impending work project deadline compounded by sleep disturbances.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's struggle with self-critical perfectionist thoughts, but also recognizes their willingness to reflect and consider alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge perfectionist thoughts, adopt healthier self-assessments, and incorporate time management and relaxation strategies to alleviate anxiety.
