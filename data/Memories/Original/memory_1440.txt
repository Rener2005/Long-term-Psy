# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chen is navigating a period of uncertainty, having recently lost a role of responsibility as a class leader. This has impacted Chen's self-worth, as Chen feels efforts go unnoticed, particularly in familial interactions. Chen is committed to improving his relationship with his daughter and managing feelings of inadequacy.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chen experiences symptoms of anxiety and feelings of inadequacy but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen feels frustrated and unappreciated after losing a leadership position and struggles with self-worth issues within family dynamics.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Chen's self-worth is tied to external validation, creating tension in interactions, but Chen shows openness to internal recognition of efforts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Chen recognize and value personal contributions independently of others’ acknowledgments. Foster strategies for effective communication within family settings to express needs assertively.
