# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xu Feng (pseudonym) is a high school student who often struggles with feelings of anxiety and uncertainty, especially related to social interactions and academic performance. Xu Feng is reflective and seeks to build stronger connections with classmates to feel more integrated into the school community.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xu Feng experiences social anxiety and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xu Feng feels regret and isolation after a conflict with a classmate, affecting his ability to connect with peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xu Feng's tendency to harbor negative thoughts about social interactions and fears of being judged, yet also notes his willingness to approach these anxieties constructively.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xu Feng manage social fears by challenging negative assumptions and promoting simple social interactions, ultimately aiding in confidence building.
