# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is an IT engineer struggling with feelings of isolation and depression, exacerbated by frequent arguments with their partner. Zhang has recently been experiencing a loss of interest in activities and difficulties with focus and fatigue.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, especially related to personal and professional inadequacies, but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels overwhelmed and depressed, grappling with pervasive feelings of inadequacy and isolation due to relationship struggles.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhang's tendency toward extreme negative thoughts that contribute to feelings of anxiety and helplessness.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Zhang identify and challenge negative thought patterns, focusing on extending self-compassion. Encourage social re-engagement to improve connection and break isolation cycles.
