He is eager to find strategies to better handle these stressors and to improve his work-life balance.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei experiences significant anxiety related to work performance and productivity. He has no formal mental health disorder diagnosis but has expressed ongoing struggles with managing stress and work-related pressure.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels overwhelmed and anxious due to work stress, concerned about meeting expectations and dealing with team miscommunication.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Zhang Wei struggles with self-perception and anxiety cycles, which are exacerbated by high work demands and lack of task prioritization, but exhibits a commitment to seeking solutions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei manage stress by breaking tasks into smaller, manageable parts, and address negative thoughts through thought-challenging strategies. Implement tools for improving task management to alleviate feelings of being overwhelmed.
