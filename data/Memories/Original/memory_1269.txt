# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
J (pseudonym) is a student dealing with feelings of anxiety and a lack of motivation towards studies, particularly related to the pressure of upcoming exams. J has recently been impacted by witnessing a friend's struggles and expresses a pervasive sense of hopelessness about academic endeavors.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
J experiences symptoms of anxiety and academic stress but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
J feels overwhelmed by academic pressures, expressing a strong sense of hopelessness and a struggle to concentrate on studies.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes J's negative self-perceptions and anxiety regarding academic performance, yet notes J's insight into these feelings and openness to considering alternative perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help J identify and challenge negative thought cycles. Focus on setting small, achievable study goals to alleviate academic pressure and provide motivation.
