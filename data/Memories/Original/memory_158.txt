[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Lan demonstrates symptoms of post-traumatic stress, characterized by anxiety, intrusive thoughts, and avoidance behaviors, but there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder recorded.

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]
