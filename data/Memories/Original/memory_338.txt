# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Li (pseudonym) is a young professional navigating the challenges of balancing personal aspirations with familial expectations. She has recently been engaged in improving her relationship with her mother, experiencing feelings of loneliness and anxiety concerning their interactions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Li experiences anxiety and feelings of inadequacy in familial relationships but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Li feels guilty and anxious after a disagreement with her mother, wanting to reconnect but uncertain about how to proceed.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ms. Li is caught in a cycle of replaying past arguments in her mind, yet she is motivated to mend her relationship.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help Ms. Li approach her mother differently, using "I" statements to express feelings and reduce confrontation, while managing her fear of rejection.
