# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Abel (pseudonym) is a student who is dealing with anxiety related to his mother's deteriorating health and academic pressure, fearing potential expulsion from school. Abel experiences difficulty concentrating due to overwhelming emotions and tends to struggle with feelings of inadequacy.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Abel experiences symptoms of anxiety and fear of failure related to academic performance and a significant preoccupation with his mother's health condition. He has not been diagnosed with any formal mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Abel feels scared and anxious about his mother's health and potential academic failure, which leads to feelings of sadness and loneliness.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Abel struggling with intense emotions tied to his mother's condition and school challenges but showing an openness to using creative outlets and seeking self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Abel manage his anxiety by shifting focus from negative thoughts and adopting self-compassionate practices. Encourage engagement with helpful distractions like drawing and gradual sharing of feelings with trusted individuals.
