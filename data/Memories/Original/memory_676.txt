# First Consultation

## User Background

[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoyou (pseudonym) is a university student experiencing feelings of insecurity and anxiety, particularly related to interpersonal relationships and self-worth. Recently, a conflict with a roommate exacerbated these feelings, leading Xiaoyou to seek better understanding and management of these emotions.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyou experiences symptoms of anxiety, low self-esteem, and self-doubt but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory

[Mental State]  
Xiaoyou feels overwhelmed by insecurity and anxiety, perceiving hostility from others which affects self-worth and relationships.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoyou is trapped in negative thought cycles focused on perceived judgments but shows awareness of these patterns and openness to change.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyou identify and challenge negative thoughts, develop more balanced perspectives, and establish strategies for improving interpersonal interactions.
