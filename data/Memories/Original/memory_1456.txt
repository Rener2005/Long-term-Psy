# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Little Orange (pseudonym) is a young individual who is adjusting to the recent changes in the family dynamic after the birth of a younger sister. Little Orange longs for attention and connection, and seeks ways to balance familial relationships and personal fulfillment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Little Orange experiences feelings of neglect and jealousy, primarily due to the family's attention shift towards the new sibling. The user shows no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder but exhibits signs of emotional distress.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Little Orange feels down and neglected, struggling with feelings of being invisible and unimportant within the family.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes a strong desire in Little Orange for familial connection and acknowledgment, coupled with feelings of sadness and isolation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Little Orange in expressing emotions constructively and exploring positive outlets for self-fulfillment. Encourage the use of a feelings diary to articulate emotions and initiate conversations with family members to enhance connection.
