# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chris (pseudonym) recently moved to a new city to take on a joint venture and is experiencing difficulties and conflicts at work, leading to self-doubt and feelings of being overwhelmed and exhausted.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chris exhibits symptoms of anxiety and self-doubt but has not received any formal diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chris feels anxious and inadequate, particularly troubled by workplace conflicts and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Chris is deeply affected by negative interpretations of work interactions but shows openness to reframe these thoughts in a more positive light.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to help Chris reframe negative thoughts and strengthen self-confidence gradually. Encourage small, actionable steps for improving workplace communication.
