# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a student dealing with anxiety and self-criticism, particularly heightened by recent public performance experiences. These challenges affect Li’s self-esteem and concentration in academic settings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, primarily triggered by self-comparisons and fear of public perception, yet there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed and anxious about public embarrassment, leading to avoidance of similar situations and impacting class focus.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li's critical self-assessment and avoidance pattern but notes a willingness to explore more forgiving self-perceptions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral strategies to help Li reframe negative self-perceptions and build confidence in public scenarios. Implement gradual exposure techniques to confront fears related to public speaking and performance.
