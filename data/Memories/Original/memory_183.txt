# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yuan Yuan (pseudonym) is a high school student experiencing significant anxiety related to academic performance, particularly in mathematics and English. Recently, Yuan Yuan has been feeling overwhelmed by the responsibilities, including being the math class representative, leading to heightened pressure and self-doubt.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yuan Yuan exhibits symptoms of anxiety tied to self-imposed expectations and fear of criticism, which have not been formally diagnosed as a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yuan Yuan feels anxious and overwhelmed, primarily due to academic pressures and the fear of disappointing others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Yuan Yuan internalizes external expectations and has a tendency to overestimate the likelihood of negative judgment, though also shows willingness to adopt self-compassionate perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yuan Yuan reframe negative thoughts and establish realistic academic goals. Begin implementing anxiety reduction strategies such as task segmentation and controlled breathing exercises.
