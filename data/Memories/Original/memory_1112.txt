# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoche is a student who experiences overwhelming intrusive thoughts related to cleanliness and germs, which significantly impact daily activities. Xiaoche is determined to understand and manage these thoughts better and seeks support in dealing with them.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoche does not have a formal diagnosis but shows symptoms consistent with obsessive-compulsive tendencies, particularly regarding anxiety related to contamination and cleanliness.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoche feels overwhelmed by intrusive thoughts about germs, causing anxiety and a compulsion to repeatedly wash hands, particularly in academic settings.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoche is highly motivated to manage intrusive thoughts and is open to discussing these experiences despite the anxiety they cause.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive-behavioral strategies to help Xiaoche delay the urge to wash hands and challenge irrational beliefs about contamination. Foster the ability to tolerate anxiety-provoking situations without resorting to compulsive behaviors.

# Second Consultation
