# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoli (pseudonym) is a high school student experiencing significant pressure from familial expectations, particularly from her mother. She is eager to establish her independence and develop her own identity. Xiaoli struggles with expressing her true desires, feeling caught between meeting her mother's expectations and her own wishes.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoli exhibits symptoms of anxiety and stress related to familial pressure and self-expectations. There has been no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoli feels anxious and overwhelmed by her mother's expectations, leading to difficulty concentrating on her studies and feelings of inadequacy when conflicts arise.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiaoli's oscillation between a desire for independence and compliance with familial expectations. Xiaoli’s responses suggest a struggle with negative self-perceptions during conflicts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoli challenge negative self-assessments and explore strategies for expressing her needs effectively to reduce her anxiety and manage familial expectations.
