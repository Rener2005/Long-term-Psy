Xiaoyu exhibits symptoms of low motivation and stress concerning schoolwork without a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyu identify and challenge negative beliefs about self-worth and peer perceptions. Encourage experimentation with communication strategies to foster peer support.
