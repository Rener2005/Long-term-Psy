# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li is a student who experiences pressure from familial expectations, notably from their mother, which affects their focus and leads to anxiety and stress. Li is passionate about painting, which provides joy and an outlet for creative expression.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences symptoms of anxiety and stress, particularly related to academic performance and classroom engagement, but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels overwhelmed and distracted in class, primarily due to concerns about meeting their mother's expectations and the fear of disappointing her.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Li's susceptibility to external expectations, which impacts their ability to concentrate and elevate their anxiety, yet notes Li's eagerness to improve their study habits.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Li manage distractions, reduce anxiety, and improve focus by setting small, achievable study goals and creating a conducive study environment.
