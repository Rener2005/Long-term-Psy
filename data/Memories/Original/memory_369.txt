# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiao Ming is a young student who is eager to behave well in class and gain recognition for his positive actions. He has recently been working on being quiet in class and received praise from his teacher, which motivates him to continue this behavior.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiao Ming exhibits signs of impulsivity but shows a strong desire to follow classroom rules and earn positive reinforcement through behavioral rewards.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels proud and motivated after receiving praise from his teacher for being quiet in class, which inspires him to maintain this behavior.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiao Ming's enthusiasm for praise and his willingness to implement strategies to manage his impulsivity, such as playing mental games and delaying gratification.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use CBT techniques to help Xiao Ming reinforce self-control and develop positive behavior patterns in the classroom. Encourage self-monitoring and reward systems to foster continued adherence to positive behaviors.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels good about helping and sharing with classmates but struggles with controlling excitement during group activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Xiao Ming's genuine enjoyment of social interactions and his challenges with impulse control, particularly when excited.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Xiao Ming develop strategies for managing excitement, such as deep breathing and positive self-talk. Encourage ongoing growth in sharing and collaboration with peers.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels challenged by excitement during gym class but demonstrates some success in using calming techniques when reminded by his teacher.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist sees Xiao Ming's willingness to apply learned strategies like deep breathing and counting to ten, showing progress in self-regulation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Strengthen Xiao Ming's application of calming techniques through practice and reinforcement. Promote self-awareness and self-talk for better energy regulation.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels excited and proud after participating in class discussions and receiving positive feedback from his teacher.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes an increase in Xiao Ming's confidence when speaking in front of peers, along with a growing interest in storytelling.

[Consultation Goals]  
Build on Xiao Ming's growing confidence by encouraging more participation and expression. Use gradual exposure and positive reinforcement to ease public speaking anxiety.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels proud after receiving a "Behavior Progress Star" for his positive actions and aims to continue earning such recognition.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist sees Xiao Ming's motivation tied to external rewards and acknowledges his proactive approach to maintaining good behavior.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement a structured plan for Xiao Ming that includes self-monitoring and reward systems to sustain positive behavior. Involve family support in Xiao Ming's behavioral goals.

# Sixth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiao Ming feels excited and supported by his family, although he occasionally worries about expressing himself correctly.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notices Xiao Ming's increased self-expression and his family's positive reinforcement, along with occasional self-doubt about his communication skills.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Xiao Ming to focus on positive family interactions and develop confidence in storytelling. Foster environments, both at home and school, for practice and growth in communication.
