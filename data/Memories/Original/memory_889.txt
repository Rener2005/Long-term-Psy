[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
L experiences feelings of overwhelm and anxiety resulting from procrastination and self-imposed pressure, though there is no formal diagnosis mentioned.

[Mental State]

[Observational Impressions]

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive-behavioral strategies to help L challenge negative beliefs and manage tasks in smaller steps. Use time management techniques to help L feel more in control of academic responsibilities.
