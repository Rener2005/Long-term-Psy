# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang is a university student who feels self-conscious and deals with anxiety, particularly related to social interactions and the fear of rejection. Wang's self-esteem is affected by perceived social pressures and comparisons with peers who are dating.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang experiences symptoms of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels anxious and lonely due to difficulties in social interactions, particularly dating, which affects mood and academic focus.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wang struggles with feelings of inadequacy and highlights the impact of social pressures on Wang's self-esteem, yet there is a readiness to explore new perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wang challenge negative thoughts about self-worth and rejection. Encourage gradual re-engagement in social activities to build confidence.
