# First Consultation

Xiaoxiang is a young student who struggles with managing emotions, particularly anger, which has led to conflicts at school. Xiaoxiang enjoys cartoons and sometimes mimics the actions seen in them, which has resulted in misunderstandings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoxiang displays challenges with emotional regulation and social interactions but has not been formally diagnosed with any specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoxiang feels confused and frustrated, especially after facing repercussions at school for fighting.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoxiang's challenges in recognizing and expressing feelings appropriately but notes openness to learning new ways of handling emotions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoxiang identify triggers and practice alternative responses to anger. Focus on verbal expression and developing self-awareness around emotional impacts.
