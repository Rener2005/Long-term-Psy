# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Anna (pseudonym) is an adult who experiences anxiety around thunderstorms due to a traumatic childhood event. She is currently dealing with isolation and avoidance of social interactions, seeking to overcome this while managing her emotional responses better.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Anna experiences significant anxiety linked to thunderstorms. There is no disclosed formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder, but she exhibits symptoms consistent with post-traumatic stress.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Anna feels anxious and fearful, triggered by thunderstorms that remind her of a past traumatic experience, leading to social withdrawal.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Anna struggles with cognitive distortions that amplify fear and anxiety, yet she shows a willingness to learn coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to help Anna challenge her catastrophic thoughts during storms. Initiate a gradual exposure plan to lessen her avoidance behaviors.
