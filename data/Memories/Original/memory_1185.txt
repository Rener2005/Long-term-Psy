# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
E (pseudonym) struggles with social anxiety and self-criticism, especially in social settings. They value genuine connections and have been active in trying to integrate into social groups through club activities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
E experiences social anxiety and a lack of self-confidence, leading to difficulties in forming meaningful connections.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
E feels overwhelmed by social pressures and experiences loneliness due to perceived superficiality in interactions.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that E's sense of isolation stems from a mismatch between their expectations of depth in friendships and reality but notes E's willingness to strive for authenticity despite fears.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help E challenge negative assumptions and embrace opportunities for authentic engagement. Foster confidence in initiating genuine interactions without fear of judgment.
