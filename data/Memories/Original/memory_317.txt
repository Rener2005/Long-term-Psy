# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Robert is a student passionate about science, often feeling anxious about meeting expectations and criticism from authority figures such as teachers. He values feedback but struggles with taking it constructively, leading to moments of self-doubt and defensiveness.  

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Robert experiences feelings of anxiety and self-doubt in academic settings but has not been diagnosed with any specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Robert feels frustrated and defeated by his teacher's feedback on his science project, questioning the value of his hard work.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Robert takes criticism to heart, which affects his self-esteem, but he is open to considering more constructive perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Robert view feedback as a growth opportunity, encouraging peer discussions to gain different perspectives.
