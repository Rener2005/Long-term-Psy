# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yi Yi is a student who struggles with self-doubt and anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and social interactions. Yi Yi expresses a desire to improve self-esteem and foster better relationships with peers and family.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yi Yi exhibits signs of anxiety and low self-esteem, yet no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder has been provided.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yi Yi feels really down, overwhelmed by self-critical thoughts regarding academic performance and perceived parental disappointment.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Yi Yi grapples with harsh self-criticism and a fear of failure, yet displays openness to adopting more self-compassionate perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Yi Yi challenge negative self-talk and recognize personal successes. Initiate strategies to manage anxiety, focusing on self-compassion and resilience.
