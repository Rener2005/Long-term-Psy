# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
H (pseudonym) is a university student experiencing distress from nightmares and a strained relationship with their dorm head. H feels trapped in their living environment, leading to increased anxiety and emotional challenges.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
H struggles with anxiety and low self-esteem but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
H feels overwhelmed by recurring nightmares that manifest as subconscious expressions of anger and fear, fueling daily anxiety and frustration.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that H's dreams symbolize repressed emotions and a struggle for autonomy within their living situation, yet H expresses a willingness to explore these feelings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help H to identify the emotional triggers of the nightmares through cognitive behavioral techniques, mitigate anxiety by fostering awareness of irrational guilt, and promote coping strategies such as dream journaling.
