# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student experiencing anxiety and obsessive thoughts, particularly worrying about social situations and fitting in. The user recently started university and finds the transition overwhelming.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user experiences symptoms of anxiety and obsessive-compulsive thoughts but has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and overwhelmed due to adjustments to university life and obsessive thoughts related to social acceptance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes the user's tendency to overthink social interactions and avoid situations due to the fear of judgment, but there's a readiness to explore reframing thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use CBT techniques to help the user challenge obsessive thoughts and adopt healthier perspectives on social interactions. Begin to create strategies for engaging in social situations with less anxiety.
