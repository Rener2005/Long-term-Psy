# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a student who struggles with feelings of inadequacy and anxiety, particularly around social interactions and communication. They are motivated to improve their confidence and social skills.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user reports symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, especially in group settings, though no formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels frustrated and anxious, particularly when communicating their opinions in group settings, leading to feelings of jealousy and self-dissatisfaction.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency to avoid conflict and default to self-doubt despite a desire for acceptance and acknowledgment of past successes.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user identify and reframe negative thoughts and enhance conflict communication skills, fostering a more confident self-expression.
