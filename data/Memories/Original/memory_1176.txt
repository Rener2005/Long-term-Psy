# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student feeling pressured by societal expectations and struggling with a perceived lack of connections in their career path. They come from a family that cannot provide them with networking opportunities and feel detached from their classmates due to increasing job-related discussions. There's a noted shift from previous enjoyment of social activities to avoidance and withdrawal stemming from anxiety and inadequacy.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, particularly in relation to social and academic situations, but has not been formally diagnosed with any specific mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels lost and overwhelmed by academic and social pressures. They are experiencing sleep disturbances and a persistent sense of exhaustion.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user struggles with feelings of inadequacy and disconnection but expresses a desire to improve their confidence in academic and social settings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to foster confidence through establishing small, achievable academic and social goals. Encourage the development of a bedtime routine to improve sleep quality and manage anxiety.
