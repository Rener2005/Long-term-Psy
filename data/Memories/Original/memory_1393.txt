# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Lin Wen is a student who has been feeling overwhelmed by academic pressures and often struggles with feelings of inadequacy. Recent events include facing criticism from teachers and a perceived failure in exams, contributing to a sense of hopelessness.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Lin Wen exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, with no formal mental health diagnosis disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Lin Wen feels down and ashamed due to academic performance, resulting in anxiety and avoidance behaviors.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Lin Wen's tendency to engage in negative all-or-nothing thinking but notes a willingness to reevaluate this view.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Lin Wen challenge the notion of being a failure and develop healthier self-perceptions. Focus on establishing study routines and coping strategies to manage anxiety.
