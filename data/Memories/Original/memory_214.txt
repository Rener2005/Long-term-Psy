# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wang (pseudonym) is a student who struggles with anxiety related to academic pressures and concerns about self-worth, fearing negative evaluation and striving for perfection. Wang is interested in learning to manage stress and improve self-compassion.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wang experiences symptoms of anxiety, with challenges in self-esteem linked to high academic expectations, although there is no formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wang feels anxious and overwhelmed, primarily due to academic pressures and the belief that mistakes equate to failure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Wang is deeply affected by a perceived need for perfection, but shows an openness to questioning these beliefs and considering self-kindness.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive behavioral techniques to challenge Wang's perfectionistic thoughts, encouraging a more forgiving self-view and developing coping strategies for academic stress.
