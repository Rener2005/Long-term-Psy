# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a young adult enrolled in university who grapples with anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, particularly in social and academic settings. An incident involving losing a personal item recently exacerbated these feelings.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, though there is no formal diagnosis shared.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels embarrassed and anxious about losing a personal item, interpreting it as a reflection of personal inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency toward negative self-judgment but recognizes a willingness to consider more compassionate perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user reframe negative thoughts about self-worth and promote self-compassion. Encourage engagement in enjoyable activities like painting to balance emotions.
