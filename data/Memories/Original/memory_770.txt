# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Feng is a student who often struggles with anxiety and self-doubt when interacting with classmates. He experiences feelings of inadequacy and frustration, particularly in social situations at school. Feng seeks to improve his social interactions and gain self-confidence.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Feng exhibits signs of social anxiety and low self-esteem but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Feng feels upset and frustrated due to a conflict with a classmate and perceives unfair treatment, leading to bottled-up anger and feelings of inadequacy.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Feng struggles with expressing emotions and has a tendency to internalize conflicts, which heightens feelings of helplessness and isolation.

[Consultation Goals]  
Apply cognitive behavioral techniques to help Feng express emotions constructively and cultivate healthier social interactions. Encourage awareness of self-worth beyond others' opinions.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Feng continues to feel stuck and anxious about his interactions with classmates, experiencing a persistent sense of tension and avoidance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Feng's anxiety is fueled by fear of judgment and past negative experiences, yet Feng shows awareness of the benefits of facing fears.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Feng in identifying and challenging anxious thoughts, encouraging gradual exposure to social situations. Foster self-compassion and self-assurance through CBT techniques.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Feng feels anxious and isolated after attempting social interactions, experiencing worry and self-doubt when perceived interest is lacking.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Feng's tendency to conflate negative assumptions with reality but notes progress in exploring alternative interpretations.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Feng to reframe negative assumptions by evaluating evidence and practicing social engagement strategies. Reinforce resilience against perceived social setbacks.

# Sixth Consultation
