# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Yaya (pseudonym) is a student facing significant anxiety related to academic performance, especially when comparing herself to peers. She is experiencing difficulty adapting to remote learning and worries about potential academic setbacks.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Yaya shows symptoms of anxiety and low self-esteem, with concerning thoughts about self-worth. She has not received any formal mental health diagnosis thus far.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Yaya feels considerably anxious about her academic performance and struggles with feelings of inadequacy, perceiving pressure to meet high standards.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Yaya's tendency towards negative self-perception and the internal pressure to match her peers, which exacerbates her anxiety.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Yaya in identifying and challenging negative thoughts. Develop strategies to build a more balanced self-view and manage academic anxiety effectively.
