## User Background

Xiaoyan is a student experiencing feelings of inadequacy and stress, particularly related to academic performance and family expectations. Xiaoyan is eager to explore emotional regulation techniques to enhance personal well-being.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoyan exhibits signs of stress and low self-esteem, but there is no indication of a formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoyan feels down and frustrated due to poor performance in mock exams, resulting in a lack of enjoyment in daily activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Xiaoyan is struggling with negative self-comparisons and feeling defeated, but shows a willingness to discover and appreciate individual strengths and passions.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoyan reframe negative self-perceptions and recognize personal achievements. Integrate small, enjoyable activities like drawing to improve mood and counteract stress.
