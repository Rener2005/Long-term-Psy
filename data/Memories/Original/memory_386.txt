# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li Ming is a professional dealing with self-doubt and anxiety related to work performance, especially in high-pressure situations. Recently, Li Ming experienced a project failure that intensified feelings of inadequacy and comparison with colleagues.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li Ming shows symptoms of anxiety and low self-confidence but has not detailed any formal diagnosis for a mental health condition.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li Ming feels overwhelmed and anxious about work performance, perceiving self-worth through the lens of complete success or failure.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Li Ming struggles with high expectations and self-comparison, yet is willing to explore inner strengths and self-compassion.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li Ming identify and challenge negative thoughts, focusing on strengths and achievements to improve confidence and reduce anxiety.

# Second Consultation
