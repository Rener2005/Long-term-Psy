# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Eve is a mother who recently completed treatment for mental health concerns and is now facing the challenge of managing emotions as a parent. Her son has returned home, which brings mixed emotions of happiness and overwhelm. Eve has expressed a desire to maintain her mental health while being a considerate parent.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Eve experiences symptoms of anxiety and occasionally sadness, triggered by the pressures of parenthood and past mental health issues. There is no mention of a formal diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Eve feels overwhelmed and anxious, concerned about balancing her mental health with her parenting responsibilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Eve's awareness of her emotional impact on parenting and notes a genuine willingness to address these challenges constructively.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Eve find a balance between self-care and parenting. Encourage small, manageable routine changes to reduce anxiety and promote emotional regulation.
