# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Han is a professional in his early thirties grappling with feelings of inadequacy and confusion following a recent romantic rejection from a colleague. He describes feelings of self-doubt and an ongoing struggle with past relationship failures, which have impacted his self-esteem and interpersonal connections.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Han demonstrates signs of social anxiety and difficulty processing rejection, though no formal mental health diagnosis has been reported.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han feels confused and frustrated due to having developed feelings for a colleague who rejected him. He grapples with self-doubt and questions his ability to connect with others.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes that Han struggles with feelings of unworthiness following romantic rejection and negative experiences in past relationships, yet shows readiness to engage in self-reflection and explore positive aspects of his life.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to assist Han in identifying and challenging negative self-assumptions, and encourage engagement in hobbies to foster a more positive mindset and aid emotional healing.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han continues to feel sad and envious, haunted by thoughts of his unfulfilled desire for a deeper connection with his colleague.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Han's negative emotions stem from a pattern of self-critical thoughts, but acknowledges Han's willingness to re-engage socially and shift focus from past rejections to potential new connections.

[Consultation Goals]  
Support Han in acknowledging his emotions without letting them dominate his thought process, and encourage involvement in social activities such as hobby groups to meet new people and build positive experiences.

# Third Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han experiences mixed emotions of excitement and anxiety when engaging in new social activities, feeling out of place especially around female colleagues.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Han's tendency to assume negative perceptions from others, yet recognizes his emerging self-awareness and willingness to challenge these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive restructuring techniques to help Han replace anxiety-inducing assumptions with more balanced viewpoints and practice social interactions that build comfort and rapport.

# Fourth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han feels a refreshing sense of belonging and happiness from newfound friendships and shared activities like badminton and basketball.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist sees positive growth in Han's social confidence and observes a shift from solitary tendencies to an active and engaged social circle.

[Consultation Goals]  
Strengthen Han's ability to maintain and initiate social activities, fostering a sustained sense of belonging, and reinforce his self-worth through continued social engagement.

# Fifth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han experiences an upswing in confidence, fueled by positive recognition at work and active participation in group activities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes increased self-assuredness in Han's communication skills and a readiness to explore greater responsibilities, both professionally and socially.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Han to pursue leadership opportunities by developing decision-making and motivational skills, and maintain a balanced work-life dynamic through structured time for personal interests.

# Sixth Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Han feels uplifted and motivated by recent professional praise, prompting aspirations for greater societal contribution and career advancement.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist recognizes Han's resolve to set clearer long-term goals and pursue meaningful community service as part of his personal growth journey.

[Consultation Goals]  
Guide Han in structuring realistic plans for community service involvement and career progression while maintaining a shift towards meaningful impact and personal fulfillment.
