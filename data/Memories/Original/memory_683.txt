# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang is a university student who often struggles with feelings of anxiety and interpersonal tension, particularly with roommates. Recently, Zhang has been dealing with unresolved conflicts in dorm life, contributing to feelings of frustration and isolation.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang experiences symptoms of anxiety, mainly related to social situations and personal conflicts, but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang feels trapped and misunderstood due to ongoing conflicts with roommates, leading to heightened frustration and anxiety.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang’s tendency to feel undervalued by roommates, though there is a willingness to improve communication strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to improve Zhang’s communication skills and explore effective strategies for addressing roommate conflicts. Encourage Zhang to express feelings calmly and seek compromises to reduce stress and isolation.
