# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a young individual grappling with feelings of anxiety, particularly concerning others' perceptions and relationships at school. Recently, their parent's separation has heightened feelings of loneliness at home. The user is actively seeking ways to express emotions constructively and establish connections.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user is experiencing symptoms consistent with social anxiety and feelings of inadequacy. No formal mental health diagnosis has been disclosed.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and overwhelmed by the pressure to please others and fears losing friendships if they don't meet perceived expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user struggles with perfectionistic tendencies and fear of abandonment, but shows insight and willingness to adopt healthier perspectives.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral techniques to help the user challenge and reframe negative thought processes. Develop strategies for expressing emotions assertively and alleviate anxiety around social interactions.


# Second Consultation

[Consultation Goals]
