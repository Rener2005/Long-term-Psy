# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user, referred to as A, is a university student experiencing pressure in a medicine program not chosen for personal interest but out of familial obligation. A is passionate about electronics and technology.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
A struggles with anxiety, stress, and feelings of inadequacy due to a misalignment between current academic pursuits and personal interests, but has not been formally diagnosed with any mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
A feels overwhelmed and trapped by the academic demands in the chosen field of medicine, experiencing stress-induced physical symptoms.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that A grapples with the conflict between external expectations and personal interests, yet shows a reflective nature and openness to explore different paths.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy to help A manage stress through identifying and challenging negative thoughts, while exploring the possibility of integrating interests in technology to alleviate academic pressure.
