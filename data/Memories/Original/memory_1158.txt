# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Wenwen (pseudonym) is a university student grappling with uncertainty about her academic major and future career. She experiences pressure due to her family's expectations and feels anxious about not wanting to burden her aging parents. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Wenwen shows signs of anxiety related to academic and career decisions but has not indicated any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Wenwen feels overwhelmed and uncertain about her choice of major and its impact on her future, fearing she might not meet family expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Wenwen's anxiety stems from self-comparison and familial pressure, yet she is open to exploring her strengths and interests.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Wenwen explore and evaluate her thoughts about her major. Encourage the identification and development of personal interests to reduce anxiety related to academic uncertainty.
