# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Gerard is a middle-aged male, previously married and currently dealing with the aftermath of a divorce and job loss. He volunteers in a train activity for children, finding fulfillment and joy in it, though he often struggles with past negative emotions and self-doubt.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Gerard experiences symptoms of chronic depression and anxiety, particularly related to past personal and professional setbacks. There's no formal diagnosis mentioned but significant past struggles are evident.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Gerard experiences fluctuating moods, with volunteer work bringing positivity, but old negative emotions related to past divorce and job loss frequently resurface.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that while Gerard is driven by past challenges, he exhibits a capacity for self-reflection and is motivated by meaningful contributions to others.

[Consultation Goals]  
Help Gerard explore and reframe negative self-beliefs using cognitive behavioral techniques. Aim to develop strategies that balance past experiences with current positive involvements.
