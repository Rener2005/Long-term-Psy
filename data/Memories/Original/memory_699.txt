# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Chen Yang is a young adult navigating complex family dynamics and expectations. Recently, feelings of stagnation surfaced after visiting family, sparking self-reflections about personal progress and the perception of his achievements.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Chen Yang experiences social anxiety and self-doubt, particularly in family and academic settings, though there is no formal diagnosis.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Chen Yang feels lost and stagnant, particularly in the context of family dynamics, after a recent visit home.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Chen Yang grapples with feelings of inadequacy and self-imposed pressure, yet demonstrates insight by acknowledging his struggles.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Chen Yang recognize and challenge unhelpful thoughts about personal progress and perceived family expectations. Encourage engagement in enjoyable activities to foster satisfaction and balance.
