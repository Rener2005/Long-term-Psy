# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Jiang (pseudonym) is a student who recently transferred schools and feels lost and overwhelmed, struggling with adapting to the new environment and questioning the value of studies. Jiang feels pressured by parental expectations and desires to regain motivation and purpose. 

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Jiang shows signs of anxiety and low motivation, particularly related to academic and social pressures, but has not been formally diagnosed with a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiang feels lost, overwhelmed, and unmotivated, facing confusion regarding academic goals and feeling pressured by parental expectations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Jiang's struggle to find purpose in studies amid pressure from parents and a general sense of helplessness, but notes an openness to exploring these feelings.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Jiang identify and challenge the negative beliefs surrounding academic pressure and parental expectations, encouraging small steps towards engagement with peers and studies.

# Second Consultation

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Jiang feels frustrated and angry about feeling misunderstood by family, particularly the perceived lack of support and understanding from the mother.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Jiang's internal conflict between wanting to assert independence and the pressure to meet parental expectations, with an observable pattern of retreating during conflicts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Assist Jiang in developing communication strategies to express emotions and needs effectively, using CBT to reframe thoughts about self-worth and family relationships.

# Third Consultation

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Jiang's recognition of cognitive distortions, especially in familial interactions, and willingness to challenge extreme negative thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Encourage Jiang to cultivate balanced self-reflection and engage in a gradual reconnection with friends, using CBT to manage anxious triggers and emotional responses.
