# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhao (pseudonym) is a university student struggling with feelings of loneliness and anxiety following a recent transition to university life. Zhao reports missing home and experiencing difficulty engaging with others in the new environment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhao exhibits signs of social anxiety and loneliness, with no formal diagnosis disclosed for a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhao feels overwhelmed and anxious about being alone in a new environment, experiencing a strong sense of homesickness and social avoidance.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Zhao's tendency towards self-criticism and avoidance but notes openness to exploring these feelings and challenging negative thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Implement cognitive behavioral strategies to help Zhao challenge beliefs about belonging and gradually engage in social activities. Begin fostering strategies to manage feelings of loneliness and enhance social connections.
