# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Li (pseudonym) is a retired individual who previously held a significant position at work. Since retiring, Li struggles with a lack of daily routine and purpose, leading to feelings of anxiety, frustration, and disconnection from social life.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Li experiences anxiety and feelings of worthlessness associated with the transition to retirement but has not reported any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Li feels lost and struggles with a sense of worthlessness stemming from retirement and a lack of routine.

[Observational Impressions]  
The consultant observes that Li grapples with feelings of isolation and fear of judgment from peers but shows readiness to reconnect with old friends.

[Consultation Goals]  
Employ cognitive behavioral techniques to help Li challenge negative thoughts about self-worth and re-establish social connections. Encourage small steps towards reaching out to friends to alleviate feelings of isolation.
