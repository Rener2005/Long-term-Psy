# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student who is battling anxiety and self-doubt, particularly concerning public speaking and participation in group projects. They aim to become more confident and overcome their fear of negative judgment.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety, specifically related to public speaking and social performance. There is no formal diagnosis provided.  

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels intensely anxious about an upcoming group project presentation, fearing judgment from peers.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that the user holds a strong fear of negative judgment and tends to assume the worst-case scenarios, but also shows openness to exploring and challenging these thoughts.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) to help the user reframe negative thoughts and build confidence by practicing positive affirmations and relaxation techniques before their presentation.

# Second Consultation
