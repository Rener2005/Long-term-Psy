# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Zhang Wei (pseudonym) is navigating uncertainties related to career direction and recent professional setbacks. He describes feelings of inadequacy and reluctance to burden his spouse with these concerns, though he desires improved communication and support.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Zhang Wei is experiencing symptoms of anxiety linked to professional performance and self-doubt, yet has not disclosed any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Zhang Wei feels overwhelmed due to a career-related setback and struggles with work-related anxiety and self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes Zhang Wei's tendency toward self-critical thoughts but also recognizes his interest in developing more positive communication strategies with his spouse.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Zhang Wei manage these negative thoughts and improve communication with his spouse, focusing on balanced perceptions and shared experiences.
