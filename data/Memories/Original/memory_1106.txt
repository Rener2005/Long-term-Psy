# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Ms. Xie is a sales professional experiencing stress related to workplace demands and feels pressure to meet expectations while also managing family responsibilities.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Ms. Xie reports feelings of stress and self-doubt but has not stated any formal diagnosis or underlying mental health disorders.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Ms. Xie feels overwhelmed and anxious due to workplace stress and is struggling with self-doubt regarding her capabilities.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes that Ms. Xie is under significant pressure in her work environment and is inclined towards negative self-assessment, although she shows a willingness to explore and implement new coping strategies.

[Consultation Goals]  
Use cognitive behavioral techniques to help Ms. Xie challenge negative self-beliefs and develop practical strategies for reducing workplace anxiety, such as breaking larger tasks into manageable goals.
