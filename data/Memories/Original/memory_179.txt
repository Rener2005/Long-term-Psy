# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
The user is a university student dealing with anxiety, particularly related to academic performance and securing an internship. They express feelings of being overwhelmed by end-of-term exams and the internship application process.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
The user exhibits symptoms of anxiety but has not mentioned any formal diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
The user feels anxious and overwhelmed, struggling with concentration and dealing with self-doubt.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist notes the user's tendency to fear failure and an internalized sense of inadequacy, balanced by acknowledgment of prior achievements and positive feedback.

[Consultation Goals]  
Introduce cognitive-behavioral techniques to help the user reframe negative thoughts and encourage brief breaks for self-care activities to improve focus and reduce anxiety.
