# First Consultation

## User Background
[Personal Profile]  
Xiaoli (pseudonym) is a student who experiences significant anxiety and fear of embarrassment when speaking English in class. Xiaoli is keen on improving communication skills and seeks ways to overcome these challenges to interact more confidently.

[Assessment and Diagnosis]  
Xiaoli exhibits symptoms of social anxiety related to language use but has no disclosed diagnosis of a mental health disorder.

## Conversation Memory
[Mental State]  
Xiaoli feels anxious about speaking in English, fearing judgment from classmates, leading to avoidance of such situations.

[Observational Impressions]  
The therapist observes Xiaoli’s fear of judgment and the impact on self-esteem, but notes Xiaoli's willingness to engage in gradual exposure to build confidence.

[Consultation Goals]  
Utilize cognitive behavioral techniques to help Xiaoli challenge irrational fears and develop strategies for gradual exposure to speaking situations.
